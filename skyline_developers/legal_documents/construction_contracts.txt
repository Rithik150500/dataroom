================================================================================
                         RECITALS
================================================================================

WHEREAS:

A. The Employer is developing a residential project known as "SKYLINE
   RESIDENCY" at Survey No. 45/1, 45/2, Village Wagle, Thane West.

B. The Employer desires to engage the Contractor for the construction of
   the said project.

C. The Contractor has the necessary expertise, experience, and resources
   to undertake the construction work.

D. The parties have agreed to enter into this Contract on the terms and
   conditions set out herein.

================================================================================
                    ARTICLE 1: SCOPE OF WORK
================================================================================

1.1 The Contractor shall execute and complete the following works:

    (a) Civil and Structural Works
        - Excavation and earthwork
        - Piling and foundation
        - RCC structure (3 towers, G+30)
        - Brick work and plastering
        - Waterproofing

    (b) Finishing Works
        - Flooring and tiling
        - Internal and external painting
        - Door and window installation
        - Kitchen platform and counters

    (c) MEP Works (Excluding main equipment)
        - Internal plumbing
        - Internal electrical
        - Fire fighting (civil portion)
        - HVAC (civil portion)

1.2 EXCLUSIONS:

    The following works are NOT included in this Contract:
    - Architect and consultant fees
    - Statutory approvals and fees
    - Main MEP equipment (lifts, DG, pumps)
    - Landscaping and external development
    - Marketing and sales

================================================================================
                   ARTICLE 2: CONTRACT PRICE
================================================================================

2.1 LUMP SUM CONTRACT

    The Contract Price for the complete scope of work is:

    Rs. 175,00,00,000/- (Rupees One Hundred Seventy-Five Crores Only)

    This is a lump sum fixed price contract.

2.2 PRICE BREAKDOWN:

    Component                           Amount (Rs. Crores)
    --------------------------------------------------------
    Civil and Structural Works          120.00
    Finishing Works                      40.00
    MEP Works (Civil portion)            15.00
    --------------------------------------------------------
    Total                               175.00
    --------------------------------------------------------

2.3 PRICE ESCALATION:

    No price escalation shall be applicable for the contract duration of
    36 months. For delays attributable to Employer beyond 36 months,
    escalation of 5% per annum shall apply.

2.4 TAXES:

    The Contract Price is inclusive of all taxes except GST. GST shall be
    payable extra at applicable rates.

================================================================================
                 ARTICLE 3: PAYMENT TERMS
================================================================================

3.1 ADVANCE PAYMENT:

    Mobilization Advance:       10% (Rs. 17.50 Crores)
    Against Bank Guarantee:     Yes
    Recovery:                   Pro-rata from running bills

3.2 RUNNING BILLS:

    Frequency:                  Monthly
    Submission:                 By 5th of following month
    Certification:              Within 7 days
    Payment:                    Within 15 days of certification

3.3 RETENTION:

    Retention:                  5% of each bill
    Release:                    50% on completion, 50% after DLP

3.4 MILESTONES:

    Milestone                   Payment (% of Contract)
    --------------------------------------------------------
    Foundation Complete         15%
    Basement Complete           10%
    Structure (10th floor)      15%
    Structure (20th floor)      15%
    Structure (30th floor)      15%
    Finishing (50%)             15%
    Finishing (100%)            10%
    Handover                     5%
    --------------------------------------------------------

================================================================================
               ARTICLE 4: TIME FOR COMPLETION
================================================================================

4.1 CONTRACT DURATION:

    Commencement Date:          01.08.2021
    Completion Date:            31.07.2024
    Total Duration:             36 months

4.2 MILESTONE SCHEDULE:

    Milestone                   Target Date
    --------------------------------------------------------
    Site Mobilization           15.08.2021
    Foundation Complete         31.12.2021
    Basement Complete           31.03.2022
    Structure Complete          31.03.2023
    MEP Rough-in Complete       30.06.2023
    Finishing Complete          31.03.2024
    Testing & Commissioning     30.06.2024
    Final Handover              31.07.2024
    --------------------------------------------------------

4.3 LIQUIDATED DAMAGES:

    For delay attributable to Contractor:
    - Rate: 0.5% of Contract Price per week
    - Maximum: 10% of Contract Price

4.4 EXTENSION OF TIME:

    Extension shall be granted for:
    - Force majeure events
    - Variations ordered by Employer
    - Delays by Employer in approvals/site access
    - Unforeseen site conditions

================================================================================
               ARTICLE 5: CONTRACTOR'S OBLIGATIONS
================================================================================

5.1 GENERAL OBLIGATIONS:

    The Contractor shall:

    (a) Execute the work with due diligence and in a workmanlike manner
    (b) Comply with specifications and drawings
    (c) Employ skilled supervisors and workmen
    (d) Provide all plant, equipment, and materials
    (e) Maintain insurance as required
    (f) Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

5.2 SITE MANAGEMENT:

    (a) Maintain site in clean and safe condition
    (b) Provide security and watchman
    (c) Maintain site records and progress photographs
    (d) Coordinate with other contractors

5.3 QUALITY ASSURANCE:

    (a) Implement Quality Management System
    (b) Conduct quality tests as specified
    (c) Submit test reports and certificates
    (d) Rectify defective work at own cost

5.4 SAFETY:

    (a) Implement Safety Management Plan
    (b) Provide PPE to all workers
    (c) Conduct safety training
    (d) Report accidents immediately
    (e) Comply with BOCW Act

5.5 LABOR COMPLIANCE:

    (a) Register under BOCW Act
    (b) Maintain muster rolls and wage registers
    (c) Pay minimum wages
    (d) Comply with PF and ESI
    (e) Ensure no child labor

================================================================================
               ARTICLE 6: EMPLOYER'S OBLIGATIONS
================================================================================

6.1 The Employer shall:

    (a) Provide access to site
    (b) Provide approved drawings and specifications
    (c) Arrange statutory approvals
    (d) Make timely payments
    (e) Provide water and electricity connections
    (f) Appoint Project Management Consultant (PMC)

================================================================================
                  ARTICLE 7: VARIATIONS
================================================================================

7.1 The Employer may order variations in the scope of work.

7.2 Variations shall be valued as follows:
    (a) By agreement between parties
    (b) Using rates in Contract
    (c) Using market rates if not in Contract

7.3 Variations exceeding 15% of Contract Price shall require mutual
    agreement on revised price and timeline.

================================================================================
               ARTICLE 8: INSURANCE
================================================================================

8.1 The Contractor shall obtain and maintain:

    (a) Contractor's All Risk (CAR) Policy
        - Sum Insured: Contract Price
        - Coverage: All construction risks

    (b) Third Party Liability
        - Sum Insured: Rs. 5 Crores

    (c) Workmen Compensation
        - As per statute

8.2 The Employer shall be named as co-insured.

================================================================================
                ARTICLE 9: DEFECT LIABILITY
================================================================================

9.1 Defect Liability Period (DLP): 24 months from completion

9.2 The Contractor shall rectify at own cost any defects arising from:
    - Defective materials
    - Poor workmanship
    - Non-compliance with specifications

9.3 Structural defects liability: 10 years

================================================================================
             ARTICLE 10: TERMINATION
================================================================================

10.1 TERMINATION BY EMPLOYER:

     The Employer may terminate for:
     (a) Contractor's insolvency
     (b) Abandonment of work
     (c) Persistent failure to comply with instructions
     (d) Delays exceeding 3 months

10.2 TERMINATION BY CONTRACTOR:

     The Contractor may terminate for:
     (a) Non-payment for more than 60 days
     (b) Prolonged suspension (more than 90 days)

10.3 CONSEQUENCES:

     On termination, the Contractor shall:
     (a) Stop work immediately
     (b) Remove equipment from site
     (c) Deliver all materials to Employer
     (d) Submit final account

================================================================================
             ARTICLE 11: DISPUTE RESOLUTION
================================================================================

11.1 Any dispute shall first be referred to a Joint Committee of senior
     representatives of both parties.

11.2 If not resolved within 30 days, the dispute shall be referred to
     arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

11.3 The arbitration shall be conducted in Mumbai by a sole arbitrator
     appointed mutually.

================================================================================
                ARTICLE 12: GENERAL
================================================================================

12.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

12.2 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India. Courts in
     Mumbai shall have jurisdiction.

12.3 Neither party may assign this Agreement without consent.
