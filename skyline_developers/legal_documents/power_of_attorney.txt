                 GENERAL POWER OF ATTORNEY NO. GPA-001
           (FOR LAND ACQUISITION AND PROPERTY TRANSACTIONS)
================================================================================

THIS GENERAL POWER OF ATTORNEY is executed on this 1st day of July, 2021

BY:

SKYLINE DEVELOPERS PRIVATE LIMITED, a company incorporated under the
Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at 12th Floor, Skyline
Tower, Bandra Kurla Complex, Mumbai - 400051 (CIN: U45201MH2018PTC305678)

(Hereinafter called the "Principal" which expression shall, unless it be
repugnant to the subject or context, include its successors and assigns)

IN FAVOUR OF:

MR. RAJESH KUMAR MEHTA, son of Late Shri Mohan Mehta, residing at 15, Worli
Sea Face, Mumbai - 400018, Managing Director of the Company

(Hereinafter called the "Attorney" which expression shall, unless it be
repugnant to the subject or context, include his heirs, executors, and
administrators)

WHEREAS:

A. The Principal is engaged in the business of real estate development and
   construction.

B. The Principal requires to acquire lands, deal with property matters, and
   execute various documents for the purposes of its business.

C. The Principal considers it necessary and expedient to appoint an Attorney
   to act on its behalf for property-related matters.

NOW THIS DEED WITNESSETH:

That the Principal hereby nominates, constitutes, and appoints the Attorney
as its true and lawful Attorney with full power and authority to do, execute,
and perform all or any of the following acts, deeds, and things:

1. LAND ACQUISITION

   (a) To identify and negotiate for purchase of lands and properties.
   (b) To enter into agreements to purchase, agreements to sell, or any
       preliminary agreements for acquisition of lands.
   (c) To pay earnest money, advance, and purchase consideration.
   (d) To execute sale deeds, conveyance deeds, and transfer deeds.
   (e) To take possession of lands and properties.

2. JOINT DEVELOPMENT AGREEMENTS

   (a) To enter into Joint Development Agreements (JDA) with landowners.
   (b) To negotiate and finalize terms of revenue sharing or area sharing.
   (c) To execute all documents including development agreements, supplementary
       agreements, and addendums.
   (d) To give undertakings and indemnities to landowners.

3. STATUTORY APPROVALS

   (a) To make applications for and obtain all statutory approvals including:
       - Building plan approval
       - Environmental clearance
       - Fire NOC
       - Aviation NOC
       - Tree cutting permission
       - Any other approvals required

   (b) To appear before any authority, tribunal, or court in connection with
       such approvals.

   (c) To sign and submit applications, affidavits, undertakings, and other
       documents.

4. RERA COMPLIANCE

   (a) To register projects with the Real Estate Regulatory Authority.
   (b) To file quarterly progress reports and annual audit reports.
   (c) To update project details and respond to complaints.
   (d) To extend project registration and apply for completion certificates.

5. BANKING AND FINANCIAL

   (a) To open, operate, and close bank accounts including project escrow
       accounts.
   (b) To apply for and avail loans and credit facilities.
   (c) To create mortgages and charges on properties.
   (d) To execute loan agreements, mortgage deeds, and security documents.

6. CUSTOMER AGREEMENTS

   (a) To execute agreements for sale with customers.
   (b) To issue allotment letters and possession letters.
   (c) To receive payments and issue receipts.
   (d) To execute sale deeds and conveyance deeds in favor of customers.

7. LEGAL PROCEEDINGS

   (a) To file, defend, and conduct legal proceedings.
   (b) To appoint lawyers and advocates.
   (c) To sign vakalatnamas and authorize representations.
   (d) To enter into settlements and compromises.

8. GENERAL

   (a) To sign, execute, and register any documents.
   (b) To give undertakings, indemnities, and declarations.
   (c) To appoint sub-attorneys with similar powers.
   (d) To do all acts, deeds, and things necessary for the above purposes.

The Principal hereby agrees to ratify and confirm all acts, deeds, and things
lawfully done by the Attorney by virtue of these presents.

This Power of Attorney shall remain in force until revoked by the Principal
by a written notice to the Attorney.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF the Principal has executed this Power of Attorney on the
day and year first above written.
