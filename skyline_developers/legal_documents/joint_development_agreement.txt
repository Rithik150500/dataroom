================================================================================
                         RECITALS
================================================================================

WHEREAS:

A. The Landowners are the joint and absolute owners of all that piece and
   parcel of land bearing Survey No. 45/1, 45/2, admeasuring 2.02 Hectares
   (approximately 5 Acres), situated at Village Wagle, Thane West - 400601.

B. The Landowners have agreed to develop the said land jointly with the
   Developer for construction of a residential complex to be known as
   "SKYLINE RESIDENCY".

C. The Developer has the necessary expertise, experience, and financial
   capability to develop the said land.

D. The parties have agreed to develop the property on the terms and
   conditions set out in this Agreement.

================================================================================
                       ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

1.1 "Built-up Area" means the covered area of the building measured to the
    outer face of the external walls.

1.2 "Carpet Area" means the net usable floor area as defined under RERA.

1.3 "FSI" means Floor Space Index as sanctioned by Thane Municipal Corporation.

1.4 "Project" means the residential complex to be developed on the Property.

1.5 "Landowners' Share" means 30% of the total saleable area.

1.6 "Developer's Share" means 70% of the total saleable area.

================================================================================
                  ARTICLE 2: PROPERTY DESCRIPTION
================================================================================

2.1 The Property comprises:

    Survey No.:     45/1, 45/2
    Village:        Wagle
    Taluka:         Thane
    District:       Thane
    Total Area:     2.02 Hectares (20,234 sq. meters / 5 Acres)

2.2 Boundaries:
    North:  40 feet wide road
    South:  Survey No. 46
    East:   Survey No. 44
    West:   30 feet wide road

================================================================================
                ARTICLE 3: DEVELOPMENT RIGHTS
================================================================================

3.1 GRANT OF DEVELOPMENT RIGHTS

    The Landowners hereby grant to the Developer the exclusive right and
    license to develop the Property on the terms set out herein.

3.2 SCOPE OF DEVELOPMENT

    The Developer shall construct:
    - 3 Residential Towers (Tower A, B, C)
    - Ground + 30 Floors each
    - Approximately 300 residential units
    - Basement and podium parking
    - Amenities: Clubhouse, gymnasium, swimming pool, landscaped gardens

3.3 APPROVALS

    The Developer shall, at its own cost, obtain all necessary approvals:
    (a) Building Plan Approval from TMC
    (b) RERA Registration
    (c) Environmental Clearance
    (d) Fire NOC
    (e) Commencement Certificate
    (f) Occupation Certificate

================================================================================
               ARTICLE 4: SHARING OF BUILT-UP AREA
================================================================================

4.1 AREA SHARING RATIO

    The total saleable area shall be shared as follows:

    Landowners:  30% of total saleable area
    Developer:   70% of total saleable area

4.2 ALLOCATION OF UNITS

    Based on sanctioned plan with total saleable area of 4,00,000 sq. ft.:

    Landowners' Share: 1,20,000 sq. ft. (approximately 40 units)
    Developer's Share: 2,80,000 sq. ft. (approximately 260 units)

4.3 ALLOCATION PROCESS

    (a) The Landowners shall select their units within 30 days of approval
        of building plans.
    (b) Selection shall be tower-wise and floor-wise to ensure equitable
        distribution.
    (c) Landowners shall have first choice for ground floor and lower floor
        units.

4.4 PARKING ALLOCATION

    The Landowners shall be entitled to 60 car parking spaces (1.5 per unit).

================================================================================
             ARTICLE 5: FINANCIAL ARRANGEMENTS
================================================================================

5.1 NO CONSIDERATION

    This is a pure area-sharing arrangement. No monetary consideration shall
    be paid by either party to the other.

5.2 REFUNDABLE DEPOSIT

    The Developer shall pay to the Landowners a refundable deposit of
    Rs. 1,00,00,000/- (Rupees One Crore Only) as follows:

    (a) Rs. 25,00,000/- on signing of this Agreement (Paid)
    (b) Rs. 25,00,000/- on approval of building plan
    (c) Rs. 50,00,000/- on obtaining Commencement Certificate

    This deposit shall be refunded without interest on registration of
    sale deeds for 50% of the Landowners' units.

5.3 DEVELOPMENT COSTS

    All costs of development shall be borne by the Developer including:
    (a) Approval fees and premiums
    (b) Construction costs
    (c) Marketing and sales expenses
    (d) Taxes and levies
    (e) Legal and professional fees

5.4 MAINTENANCE CHARGES

    (a) The Developer shall bear maintenance for 2 years from OC.
    (b) Thereafter, all owners shall contribute proportionately.

================================================================================
             ARTICLE 6: CONSTRUCTION OBLIGATIONS
================================================================================

6.1 QUALITY STANDARDS

    The Developer shall construct the Project with:
    (a) RCC frame structure with M30 grade concrete
    (b) External walls: AAC blocks / red bricks
    (c) Flooring: Vitrified tiles (branded)
    (d) Bathroom: Branded sanitaryware and CP fittings
    (e) Electrical: Branded switches and concealed wiring
    (f) Doors: Main door - teak wood; Internal - flush doors
    (g) Windows: Powder-coated aluminum with glass

6.2 SPECIFICATIONS

    The detailed specifications are set out in Schedule A hereto.

6.3 MODIFICATIONS

    Any changes to specifications shall require written consent of Landowners.

================================================================================
              ARTICLE 7: TIMELINE AND MILESTONES
================================================================================

7.1 PROJECT TIMELINE

    Total Project Duration: 48 months from Commencement Certificate

    Milestones:
    - Building Plan Approval:        6 months from execution
    - RERA Registration:             7 months from execution
    - Commencement Certificate:      9 months from execution
    - Completion of Structure:       36 months from CC
    - Occupation Certificate:        48 months from CC

7.2 DELAY PENALTY

    If the Developer fails to complete within 48 months (plus 6 months grace):

    (a) Penalty: Rs. 5 per sq. ft. per month on Landowners' share
    (b) Maximum penalty: 10% of value of Landowners' share
    (c) Termination right if delay exceeds 18 months

================================================================================
               ARTICLE 8: SALES AND MARKETING
================================================================================

8.1 DEVELOPER'S SHARE

    The Developer shall have exclusive right to sell its share at prices
    determined by it.

8.2 LANDOWNERS' SHARE

    (a) The Landowners may sell their units independently.
    (b) If Landowners request, Developer shall sell on their behalf at
        prevailing market rates, with 2% brokerage.

8.3 MINIMUM PRICE PROTECTION

    The Developer shall not sell units below Rs. 8,000 per sq. ft. during
    the first 2 years without Landowners' consent.

================================================================================
             ARTICLE 9: REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES
================================================================================

9.1 LANDOWNERS' REPRESENTATIONS

    The Landowners represent and warrant that:

    (a) They are the absolute owners with full right to develop.
    (b) The Property is free from encumbrances (except as disclosed).
    (c) There is no litigation affecting the Property.
    (d) All taxes and outgoings are paid.
    (e) No third party has any right or interest.

9.2 DEVELOPER'S REPRESENTATIONS

    The Developer represents and warrants that:

    (a) It has expertise and experience in real estate development.
    (b) It has adequate financial resources.
    (c) It shall comply with all laws and regulations.
    (d) It shall maintain transparency in all dealings.

================================================================================
                 ARTICLE 10: POWER OF ATTORNEY
================================================================================

10.1 The Landowners shall execute a Special Power of Attorney in favor of
     the Developer for:

     (a) Applying for and obtaining approvals
     (b) Executing customer agreements for Developer's share
     (c) Creating charge on Developer's share for project finance
     (d) Executing conveyance deeds for Developer's share

10.2 The Power of Attorney shall be irrevocable during the term of this
     Agreement.

================================================================================
                ARTICLE 11: MORTGAGE AND FINANCE
================================================================================

11.1 The Developer may mortgage its share (70%) to banks for project finance.

11.2 The Landowners' share (30%) shall NOT be mortgaged or encumbered.

11.3 All loan documents shall clearly demarcate Developer's share.

11.4 The Developer shall provide copies of all loan sanction letters.

================================================================================
                   ARTICLE 12: RERA COMPLIANCE
================================================================================

12.1 The Developer shall register the Project with MahaRERA.

12.2 The Project shall be registered in the joint names of Landowners and
     Developer as Promoters.

12.3 70% of customer payments shall be deposited in RERA escrow account.

12.4 Quarterly compliance reports shall be filed jointly.

================================================================================
                    ARTICLE 13: INSURANCE
================================================================================

13.1 The Developer shall obtain and maintain:

     (a) Contractor's All Risk (CAR) Insurance
     (b) Workmen Compensation Insurance
     (c) Third Party Liability Insurance

13.2 All policies shall name Landowners as co-insured.

================================================================================
                    ARTICLE 14: INDEMNITY
================================================================================

14.1 The Developer shall indemnify the Landowners against:

     (a) Claims arising from construction activities
     (b) Claims by customers for Developer's share
     (c) Claims by contractors and vendors
     (d) Penalties for non-compliance

14.2 The Landowners shall indemnify the Developer against:

     (a) Title defects
     (b) Claims by third parties on title
     (c) Pre-existing litigation

================================================================================
                   ARTICLE 15: TERMINATION
================================================================================

15.1 TERMINATION BY LANDOWNERS

     The Landowners may terminate this Agreement if:

     (a) Developer fails to start construction within 12 months of CC
     (b) Developer abandons the Project for more than 6 months
     (c) Developer becomes insolvent or goes into liquidation
     (d) Delay exceeds 18 months beyond stipulated timeline

15.2 TERMINATION BY DEVELOPER

     The Developer may terminate this Agreement if:

     (a) Landowners fail to provide clear title
     (b) Any litigation restrains development
     (c) Approvals cannot be obtained due to title issues

15.3 CONSEQUENCES OF TERMINATION

     On termination:
     (a) All construction shall vest in Landowners
     (b) Developer shall be compensated for completed work at cost
     (c) Refundable deposit shall be forfeited / returned as applicable

================================================================================
                 ARTICLE 16: DISPUTE RESOLUTION
================================================================================

16.1 Any dispute shall first be resolved through good faith negotiations.

16.2 If not resolved within 30 days, the dispute shall be referred to
     arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

16.3 The arbitration shall be conducted in Mumbai by a sole arbitrator
     appointed mutually or by the High Court.

16.4 The decision of the arbitrator shall be final and binding.

================================================================================
                 ARTICLE 17: GENERAL PROVISIONS
================================================================================

17.1 NOTICES

     All notices shall be in writing and delivered to registered addresses.

17.2 AMENDMENT

     This Agreement may be amended only by written instrument signed by
     all parties.

17.3 ASSIGNMENT

     The Developer may not assign this Agreement without Landowners' consent.

17.4 GOVERNING LAW

     This Agreement shall be governed by laws of India. Courts in Thane
     shall have jurisdiction.

17.5 ENTIRE AGREEMENT

     This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
