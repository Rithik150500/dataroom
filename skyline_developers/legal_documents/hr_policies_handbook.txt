               SECTION 2: CODE OF CONDUCT
================================================================================

2.1 PROFESSIONAL BEHAVIOR

    All employees shall:
    - Maintain professionalism at all times
    - Treat colleagues with respect and dignity
    - Avoid conflicts of interest
    - Protect company assets and reputation

2.2 ETHICAL STANDARDS

    - No bribery or corruption
    - No gifts from vendors beyond nominal value (Rs. 1,000)
    - Disclosure of conflicts of interest
    - Compliance with all laws

2.3 DRESS CODE

    - Business formals for office
    - Safety gear at project sites
    - ID cards to be worn at all times

2.4 ATTENDANCE

    - Office hours: 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM (Monday to Friday)
    - Saturday: 9:30 AM to 1:30 PM (1st and 3rd Saturday)
    - Biometric attendance mandatory
    - Late coming beyond 3 times/month will be treated as half-day leave

          SECTION 4: COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS
================================================================================

4.1 SALARY STRUCTURE

    - Basic: 40-50% of CTC
    - HRA: 25% of Basic
    - Special Allowance: Balance
    - Provident Fund: 12% of Basic
    - Gratuity: As per statute

4.2 SALARY PAYMENT

    - Monthly by last working day
    - Bank transfer only
    - Salary slip on email

4.3 INCREMENTS

    - Annual appraisal cycle: April
    - Based on performance rating
    - Market benchmarking

4.4 BENEFITS

    (a) Group Medical Insurance
        - Coverage: Rs. 5 Lakhs
        - Family: Self, Spouse, 2 Children, Parents
        - Cashless at network hospitals

    (b) Personal Accident Insurance
        - Coverage: Rs. 10 Lakhs
        - 24x7 worldwide coverage

    (c) Term Life Insurance (for senior employees)

    (d) Leave Travel Allowance
        - Once a year
        - Within India
        - For self and family

4.5 STATUTORY BENEFITS

    - Provident Fund
    - ESI (if applicable)
    - Gratuity
    - Bonus

================================================================================
                 SECTION 5: LEAVE POLICY
================================================================================

5.1 LEAVE TYPES

    (a) Casual Leave: 12 days/year
        - Max 3 days at a time
        - Cannot be accumulated

    (b) Sick Leave: 6 days/year
        - Medical certificate for 3+ days
        - Cannot be accumulated

    (c) Earned Leave: 15 days/year
        - After 1 year of service
        - Can be accumulated up to 30 days
        - Encashable on separation

    (d) Maternity Leave: 26 weeks
        - As per Maternity Benefit Act
        - For women employees

    (e) Paternity Leave: 5 days
        - Within 3 months of childbirth

    (f) Bereavement Leave: 3 days
        - For death of immediate family member

5.2 LEAVE APPLICATION

    - Apply through HRMS portal
    - Advance approval for planned leave
    - Manager approval required

5.3 LEAVE WITHOUT PAY

    - Only after exhausting all leave
    - Approval by HR Head
    - Max 30 days/year

    SECTION 9: PREVENTION OF SEXUAL HARASSMENT (POSH)
================================================================================

9.1 POLICY STATEMENT

    Skyline Developers is committed to providing a safe and harassment-free
    workplace. Sexual harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited and will
    result in disciplinary action.

9.2 WHAT CONSTITUTES SEXUAL HARASSMENT

    - Unwelcome physical contact
    - Demand for sexual favors
    - Sexually colored remarks
    - Showing pornography
    - Any unwelcome sexual conduct

9.3 INTERNAL COMPLAINTS COMMITTEE (ICC)

    Presiding Officer:  Mrs. Priya Sharma (Whole-time Director)
    Members:           Ms. Sneha Patel (Company Secretary)
                       Mr. Anil Kumar (HR Manager)
    External Member:   Ms. Kavita Joshi (Advocate)

9.4 COMPLAINT PROCEDURE

    - Written complaint within 3 months
    - Submit to ICC
    - Inquiry within 90 days
    - Confidentiality maintained

9.5 PENALTIES

    - Warning, suspension, or termination
    - Deduction from salary
    - Legal action if warranted

================================================================================
             SECTION 10: GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL
================================================================================

10.1 SCOPE

     Any work-related concern including:
     - Treatment by supervisor
     - Working conditions
     - Discrimination
     - Policy implementation

10.2 PROCEDURE

     Step 1: Discuss with immediate supervisor
     Step 2: Escalate to Department Head
     Step 3: Escalate to HR
     Step 4: Escalate to Management Committee

10.3 TIMELINE

     Each level: 7 working days
     Total resolution: Within 30 days

10.4 WHISTLEBLOWER PROTECTION

     No retaliation against employees who report concerns in good faith.

================================================================================
                  SECTION 11: SEPARATION
================================================================================

11.1 RESIGNATION

     - Written resignation to supervisor
     - Notice period: 30 days (confirmed) / 15 days (probation)
     - Shorter notice: Salary in lieu
     - Exit interview mandatory

11.2 TERMINATION

     - For cause: No notice required
     - Without cause: 30 days' notice or salary in lieu

11.3 RETIREMENT

     Age: 58 years
     Notice: 3 months before retirement date

11.4 FULL AND FINAL SETTLEMENT

     - Within 45 days of last working day
     - Clearance from all departments
     - Return of company property
     - Relieving letter and experience certificate

11.5 EXIT CHECKLIST

     - Knowledge transfer
     - Email handover
     - Asset return (laptop, phone, ID card)
     - Pending claims settlement
     - No dues certificate
