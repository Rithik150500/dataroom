Dear Mr. Mehta,

Sub: Appointment as Managing Director

We are pleased to inform you that the Board of Directors of SKYLINE
DEVELOPERS PRIVATE LIMITED ("Company"), at its meeting held on 12th June,
2018, has appointed you as the Managing Director of the Company for a period
of 5 (five) years with effect from 12th June, 2018.

The terms and conditions of your appointment are as follows:

1. DESIGNATION AND DUTIES

   You shall be designated as Managing Director and shall be responsible for
   overall management of the Company's operations, including:

   (a) Strategic planning and business development
   (b) Project acquisition and execution
   (c) Stakeholder management
   (d) Statutory compliance
   (e) Financial management
   (f) Any other duties as assigned by the Board

2. TERM

   Your appointment is for a period of 5 (five) years commencing from
   12th June, 2018 and ending on 11th June, 2023, subject to retirement
   by rotation and reappointment.

   Re-appointment: Your term was renewed for another 5 years from
   12th June, 2023 to 11th June, 2028 by resolution dated 15th June, 2023.

3. REMUNERATION

   Your remuneration shall be as follows:

   (a) Basic Salary:        Rs. 2,00,000 per month
   (b) House Rent Allowance: Rs. 50,000 per month
   (c) Special Allowance:    Rs. 50,000 per month

   Gross Monthly Salary:     Rs. 3,00,000 per month
   Annual Gross:             Rs. 36,00,000 per annum

4. PERQUISITES

   In addition to the salary, you shall be entitled to the following:

   (a) Company Car with Driver
   (b) Medical Insurance for self and family
   (c) Leave Travel Allowance as per Company policy
   (d) Provident Fund contribution as per statute
   (e) Gratuity as per the Payment of Gratuity Act

5. PERFORMANCE BONUS

   You shall be eligible for an annual performance bonus of up to 50% of
   your annual salary based on achievement of targets as determined by the
   Board.

6. LEAVE

   You shall be entitled to 30 days of paid leave per annum, which may be
   accumulated as per Company policy.

7. TERMINATION

   (a) The Company may terminate your services by giving 3 months' notice
       or salary in lieu thereof.
   (b) You may resign by giving 3 months' notice in writing to the Board.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY

   You shall maintain strict confidentiality of all information relating to
   the Company's business, operations, projects, and affairs.

9. NON-COMPETE

   During your tenure and for a period of 1 year after cessation, you shall
   not engage in any business competing with the Company within Maharashtra.

10. DISCLOSURE

    You shall make all disclosures required under the Companies Act, 2013
    including declaration of interest in any contract or arrangement.

This appointment is subject to approval by members at the ensuing General
Meeting of the Company.

Please sign the duplicate copy of this letter as a token of your acceptance
of the above terms and conditions.

For SKYLINE DEVELOPERS PRIVATE LIMITED

Sd/-

Director

   (a) Financial planning and analysis
   (b) Treasury and fund management
   (c) Banking and investor relations
   (d) Statutory compliance (tax, ROC, etc.)
   (e) Internal controls and audit coordination
