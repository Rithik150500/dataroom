               ARTICLE 4: FINANCIAL COVENANTS
================================================================================

4.1 The Borrower shall maintain the following covenants:

    (a) DEBT SERVICE COVERAGE RATIO (DSCR)
        - Minimum: 1.25x
        - Tested: Quarterly

    (b) LOAN TO VALUE (LTV)
        - Maximum: 65%
        - Tested: Quarterly

    (c) MINIMUM PRE-SALES
        - 30% before 2nd disbursement
        - 50% before 3rd disbursement
        - 70% before 4th disbursement

    (d) DEBT-EQUITY RATIO
        - Maximum: 2:1
        - Tested: Annually

    (e) COST OVERRUN FUNDING
        - Any cost overrun to be funded by equity

4.2 BREACH OF COVENANTS

    Breach of any covenant shall constitute an Event of Default and the
    Lender may:
    (a) Stop further disbursements
    (b) Demand additional security
    (c) Increase interest rate by 2%
    (d) Accelerate repayment
