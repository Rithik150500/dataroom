                          RECITALS

WHEREAS:

A. The Customer and TechVenture have entered into a Master Services Agreement
   dated ___________ (the "Principal Agreement") under which TechVenture
   provides SaaS services to the Customer.

B. In providing the Services, TechVenture may process Personal Data on
   behalf of the Customer.

C. The Parties wish to enter into this DPA to comply with applicable data
   protection laws, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023
   ("DPDPA") and other applicable regulations.

NOW, THEREFORE, the Parties agree as follows:

================================================================================

                  ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

1.1 "Applicable Data Protection Laws" means all laws relating to data
    protection and privacy applicable to the processing of Personal Data,
    including the DPDPA, Information Technology Act, 2000, and related rules.

1.2 "Data Breach" means any accidental or unlawful destruction, loss,
    alteration, unauthorized disclosure of, or access to Personal Data.

1.3 "Data Principal" means the individual to whom the Personal Data relates.

1.4 "Data Subject Request" means a request from a Data Principal exercising
    their rights under Applicable Data Protection Laws.

1.5 "Personal Data" means any data about an individual who is identifiable
    by or in relation to such data as defined under DPDPA.

1.6 "Processing" means any operation performed on Personal Data, including
    collection, storage, use, disclosure, and deletion.

1.7 "Services" means the SaaS services provided by TechVenture under the
    Principal Agreement.

1.8 "Sub-processor" means any third party engaged by TechVenture to process
    Personal Data on behalf of the Customer.

================================================================================

               ARTICLE 2: SCOPE AND PURPOSE

2.1 SCOPE
    This DPA applies to all Processing of Personal Data by TechVenture on
    behalf of the Customer in connection with the Services.

2.2 DETAILS OF PROCESSING
    The details of Processing are set out in Annex 1:
    - Subject matter and nature of Processing
    - Purpose of Processing
    - Types of Personal Data
    - Categories of Data Principals
    - Duration of Processing

2.3 ROLES
    For the purposes of this DPA:
    - The Customer is the Data Fiduciary (Controller)
    - TechVenture is the Data Processor

================================================================================

             ARTICLE 3: DATA PROCESSOR OBLIGATIONS

3.1 PROCESSING INSTRUCTIONS
    TechVenture shall:
    (a) Process Personal Data only on documented instructions from Customer
    (b) Not process Personal Data for any other purpose
    (c) Inform Customer if an instruction infringes Applicable Laws

3.2 CONFIDENTIALITY
    TechVenture shall:
    (a) Ensure all personnel processing Personal Data are bound by
        confidentiality obligations
    (b) Limit access to Personal Data to personnel who need access

3.3 SECURITY MEASURES
    TechVenture shall implement appropriate technical and organizational
    measures to protect Personal Data, including:
    (a) Encryption of Personal Data in transit and at rest
    (b) Measures to ensure ongoing confidentiality, integrity, availability
    (c) Regular testing and evaluation of security measures
    (d) Access controls and authentication mechanisms
    (e) Audit logs and monitoring

    The specific security measures are set out in Annex 2.

3.4 SUB-PROCESSORS
    TechVenture may engage Sub-processors subject to:
    (a) Prior written authorization from Customer (general or specific)
    (b) Written contract imposing equivalent data protection obligations
    (c) TechVenture remains liable for Sub-processor compliance

    Current Sub-processors are listed in Annex 3.

3.5 DATA SUBJECT REQUESTS
    TechVenture shall:
    (a) Promptly notify Customer of any Data Subject Requests
    (b) Assist Customer in responding to such requests
    (c) Not respond directly unless instructed by Customer

3.6 DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION
    In case of a Data Breach, TechVenture shall:
    (a) Notify Customer without undue delay (within 72 hours)
    (b) Provide details of the breach including:
        - Nature of the breach
        - Categories and number of Data Principals affected
        - Categories and number of records affected
        - Likely consequences
        - Measures taken to address the breach
    (c) Cooperate with Customer's investigation

3.7 ASSISTANCE
    TechVenture shall assist Customer in:
    (a) Ensuring compliance with security obligations
    (b) Conducting data protection impact assessments
    (c) Responding to inquiries from regulatory authorities

3.8 AUDIT RIGHTS
    TechVenture shall:
    (a) Make available information necessary to demonstrate compliance
    (b) Allow for audits by Customer or appointed auditor
    (c) Contribute to audits and inspections (at Customer's cost)

================================================================================

             ARTICLE 4: DATA CONTROLLER OBLIGATIONS

4.1 LAWFUL BASIS
    The Customer warrants that:
    (a) It has a lawful basis for Processing Personal Data
    (b) It has obtained necessary consents from Data Principals
    (c) It has provided required notices to Data Principals

4.2 INSTRUCTIONS
    The Customer shall:
    (a) Provide documented instructions for Processing
    (b) Ensure instructions comply with Applicable Laws
    (c) Be responsible for accuracy of Personal Data provided

================================================================================

          ARTICLE 5: INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

5.1 DATA LOCATION
    Personal Data shall be processed and stored in India unless otherwise
    agreed in writing.

5.2 CROSS-BORDER TRANSFERS
    If Personal Data is transferred outside India, TechVenture shall ensure:
    (a) Compliance with DPDPA transfer requirements
    (b) Appropriate safeguards are in place
    (c) Customer has given prior written consent

================================================================================

             ARTICLE 6: RETURN AND DELETION

6.1 UPON TERMINATION
    Upon termination of the Principal Agreement, TechVenture shall:
    (a) Return or delete all Personal Data as instructed by Customer
    (b) Provide data export within 30 days
    (c) Delete all copies within 90 days

6.2 EXCEPTIONS
    TechVenture may retain Personal Data if required by applicable law,
    subject to continued confidentiality obligations.

6.3 CERTIFICATION
    Upon request, TechVenture shall certify deletion of Personal Data.

================================================================================

                    ARTICLE 7: LIABILITY

7.1 LIABILITY
    Each Party's liability under this DPA shall be subject to the limitations
    set forth in the Principal Agreement.

7.2 INDEMNITY
    TechVenture shall indemnify Customer against losses arising from
    TechVenture's breach of this DPA, subject to liability caps in the
    Principal Agreement.

================================================================================

               ARTICLE 8: GENERAL PROVISIONS

8.1 TERM
    This DPA shall remain in effect for the duration of the Principal
    Agreement and for as long as TechVenture processes Personal Data.

8.2 CONFLICT
    In case of conflict between this DPA and the Principal Agreement, this
    DPA shall prevail with respect to data protection matters.

8.3 AMENDMENTS
    This DPA may be amended only by written agreement of both Parties.

8.4 GOVERNING LAW
    This DPA shall be governed by the laws of India.

                        ANNEX 1
              DETAILS OF DATA PROCESSING

1. SUBJECT MATTER AND NATURE OF PROCESSING:
   Provision of SaaS services for enterprise resource management, including
   storage, computation, and analysis of Customer data.

2. PURPOSE OF PROCESSING:
   - Providing and operating the Services
   - Customer support and troubleshooting
   - Service improvement and analytics
   - Compliance with legal obligations

3. TYPES OF PERSONAL DATA:
   - Contact information (name, email, phone)
   - Professional information (job title, department)
   - Account information (username, preferences)
   - Usage data (logs, activity records)
   - Any other data uploaded by Customer

4. CATEGORIES OF DATA PRINCIPALS:
   - Customer's employees
   - Customer's contractors
   - Customer's customers (end-users of Customer's services)

5. DURATION OF PROCESSING:
   For the duration of the Principal Agreement plus 90 days for data return/
   deletion.

================================================================================

                        ANNEX 2
                   SECURITY MEASURES

TechVenture implements the following security measures:

1. ACCESS CONTROLS:
   - Role-based access control (RBAC)
   - Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
   - Unique user accounts with strong passwords
   - Regular access reviews

2. ENCRYPTION:
   - TLS 1.2+ for data in transit
   - AES-256 encryption for data at rest
   - Secure key management

3. NETWORK SECURITY:
   - Firewalls and intrusion detection
   - DDoS protection
   - Network segmentation
   - VPN for administrative access

4. APPLICATION SECURITY:
   - Secure development lifecycle (SDLC)
   - Regular security testing (VAPT)
   - Code reviews
   - Dependency scanning

5. PHYSICAL SECURITY:
   - Data center certifications (ISO 27001, SOC 2)
   - Access controls and surveillance
   - Environmental controls

6. OPERATIONAL SECURITY:
   - Security monitoring and alerting
   - Incident response procedures
   - Regular security training
   - Background checks for personnel

7. BACKUP AND RECOVERY:
   - Regular automated backups
   - Geo-redundant storage
   - Disaster recovery procedures
