                    SECTION 1: INTRODUCTION
================================================================================

1.1 COMMITMENT TO PRIVACY

    TechVenture India Private Limited ("Company," "we," "us," "our")
    is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of our
    users, customers, and visitors ("you," "your"). This Privacy Policy
    explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information.

1.2 SCOPE

    This Privacy Policy applies to:

    (a) Our website www.techventure.in and all subdomains;
    (b) Our mobile applications;
    (c) All services provided through our e-commerce platform;
    (d) All interactions with our customer service;
    (e) Any other digital platforms we operate.

1.3 LEGAL COMPLIANCE

    This Policy is designed to comply with:

    (a) Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA);
    (b) Information Technology Act, 2000;
    (c) Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and
        Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011;
    (d) Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules, 2020.

1.4 CONSENT

    By using our Platform, you consent to the collection, use, and
    disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
    If you do not agree, please do not use our Platform.

                    SECTION 7: DATA RETENTION
================================================================================

7.1 RETENTION PERIODS

    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | DATA TYPE                   | RETENTION PERIOD                        |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | Account data                | Duration of account + 3 years           |
    | Transaction records         | 7 years (legal requirement)             |
    | Customer support records    | 3 years                                 |
    | Marketing data              | Until consent withdrawn                 |
    | Usage/analytics data        | 2 years                                 |
    | Cookies                     | As per cookie policy                    |
    | User generated content      | Until deleted by user or violation      |
    | Tax/financial records       | 8 years (legal requirement)             |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------

7.2 RETENTION PRINCIPLES

    (a) Kept only as long as necessary
    (b) Deleted when purpose fulfilled
    (c) Extended if legally required
    (d) Anonymized for analytics after retention

7.3 ACCOUNT DELETION

    Upon account deletion:

    (a) Personal data deleted within 30 days
    (b) Some data retained for legal compliance
    (c) Backups deleted within 90 days
    (d) Anonymized data may be retained

================================================================================

9.1 RIGHTS UNDER DPDPA 2023

    You have the right to:

    (a) ACCESS
        - Request copy of your personal data
        - Know how your data is processed
        - Receive data in portable format

    (b) CORRECTION
        - Update inaccurate data
        - Complete incomplete data

    (c) ERASURE
        - Request deletion of your data
        - Subject to legal retention requirements

    (d) GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL
        - Lodge complaints with us
        - Escalate to Data Protection Board

    (e) NOMINATE
        - Nominate someone to exercise rights after death

9.2 HOW TO EXERCISE RIGHTS

    (a) Log in to account settings
    (b) Email privacy@techventure.in
    (c) Submit request through grievance form
    (d) Write to Data Protection Officer

9.3 IDENTITY VERIFICATION

    We may verify your identity before processing requests.

9.4 RESPONSE TIME

    (a) Acknowledge request within 7 days
    (b) Respond within 30 days
    (c) Complex requests may take longer (notified)

9.5 OPT-OUT OPTIONS

    (a) Unsubscribe from marketing emails
    (b) Opt-out of SMS marketing
    (c) Disable push notifications
    (d) Adjust cookie preferences
