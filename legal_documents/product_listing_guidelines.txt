                    SECTION 1: INTRODUCTION AND PURPOSE
================================================================================

1.1 PURPOSE

    These Product Listing Guidelines establish standards for creating
    high-quality product listings on TechVenture's e-commerce platform.
    Quality listings improve customer experience, increase conversions,
    and ensure regulatory compliance.

1.2 APPLICABILITY

    These guidelines apply to:

    (a) All sellers on TechVenture marketplace;
    (b) All product categories;
    (c) New and existing listings;
    (d) All listing elements (title, description, images, etc.).

1.3 BENEFITS OF QUALITY LISTINGS

    (a) Higher visibility in search results;
    (b) Improved conversion rates;
    (c) Reduced returns and complaints;
    (d) Better customer reviews;
    (e) Increased seller rating;
    (f) Access to premium programs.

1.4 COMPLIANCE

    (a) Listings must comply with these guidelines;
    (b) Non-compliant listings may be suppressed or removed;
    (c) Repeated violations lead to account action;
    (d) Guidelines updated periodically.

================================================================================
                    SECTION 2: GENERAL LISTING REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

2.1 MANDATORY INFORMATION

    Every listing must include:

    (a) Product title;
    (b) At least one image;
    (c) Product description;
    (d) Price (MRP and selling price);
    (e) Stock quantity;
    (f) Category selection;
    (g) Brand name;
    (h) HSN code;
    (i) Country of origin;
    (j) Package contents.

2.2 ACCURACY REQUIREMENTS

    All information must be:

    (a) Accurate and truthful;
    (b) Current and up-to-date;
    (c) Consistent across all fields;
    (d) Verifiable if required;
    (e) Free from misleading claims.

2.3 LANGUAGE REQUIREMENTS

    (a) Primary language: English;
    (b) Regional languages supported for description;
    (c) Correct grammar and spelling;
    (d) No excessive abbreviations;
    (e) No special characters for decoration.

2.4 DUPLICATE LISTINGS

    (a) One listing per unique product;
    (b) Use variations for size/color options;
    (c) Do not create duplicate listings;
    (d) Duplicates will be merged or removed.

2.5 LISTING OWNERSHIP

    (a) Only list products you can fulfill;
    (b) Have proper authorization for branded products;
    (c) Maintain accurate inventory;
    (d) Update stock promptly.

================================================================================
                    SECTION 3: PRODUCT TITLE GUIDELINES
================================================================================

3.1 TITLE STRUCTURE

    Recommended format:
    [Brand] + [Product Name] + [Key Feature] + [Size/Quantity/Variant]

    Examples:
    - Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra 5G (Green, 256GB, 12GB RAM)
    - Nike Air Max 270 Men's Running Shoes (Black, UK 9)
    - Prestige Iris 750W Mixer Grinder with 3 Jars

3.2 TITLE REQUIREMENTS

    (a) Maximum length: 200 characters;
    (b) Include brand name at beginning;
    (c) Include product type;
    (d) Mention key attributes;
    (e) Use proper capitalization.

3.3 TITLE DO'S

    (a) Start with brand name;
    (b) Include model name/number;
    (c) Mention size, color, quantity;
    (d) Use standard measurements;
    (e) Include pack size (e.g., "Pack of 3").

3.4 TITLE DON'TS

    (a) No promotional text ("Sale," "Discount," "Free Shipping");
    (b) No special characters (*, #, @, etc.);
    (c) No all caps or excessive punctuation;
    (d) No subjective claims ("Best," "Top");
    (e) No seller information;
    (f) No keyword stuffing;
    (g) No HTML tags.

3.5 EXAMPLES

    GOOD:
    - Boat Rockerz 450 Bluetooth Wireless Headphone (Black)
    - Surf Excel Matic Top Load Detergent Powder, 4 kg
    - HP 15s Ryzen 3 Dual Core 3250U - (8GB/512GB SSD/Windows 11) Laptop

    BAD:
    - BEST DEAL!!! Boat Headphone BLACK ***FREE SHIPPING***
    - Detergent Washing Powder Surf Surf Excel Top Matic Best Quality
    - Laptop HP 15s Cheap Price Free Bag Free Mouse

================================================================================
                    SECTION 4: PRODUCT DESCRIPTION STANDARDS
================================================================================

4.1 DESCRIPTION STRUCTURE

    Include the following sections:

    (a) PRODUCT OVERVIEW (2-3 sentences)
        Brief summary of what the product is

    (b) KEY FEATURES (5-10 bullet points)
        Main features and benefits

    (c) SPECIFICATIONS (Table format)
        Technical details

    (d) WHAT'S IN THE BOX
        Complete list of contents

    (e) WARRANTY INFORMATION
        Coverage details

4.2 DESCRIPTION REQUIREMENTS

    (a) Minimum 100 words;
    (b) Accurate and complete;
    (c) Easy to read and scan;
    (d) Use bullet points for features;
    (e) Use tables for specifications.

4.3 CONTENT GUIDELINES

    (a) Focus on facts and features;
    (b) Explain benefits clearly;
    (c) Use simple language;
    (d) Avoid jargon unless necessary;
    (e) Include dimensions and weights.

4.4 DESCRIPTION DO'S

    (a) Highlight unique selling points;
    (b) Answer common customer questions;
    (c) Mention compatibility information;
    (d) Include care/usage instructions;
    (e) Use consistent formatting.

4.5 DESCRIPTION DON'TS

    (a) No promotional language;
    (b) No time-sensitive claims ("Limited time");
    (c) No competitor mentions;
    (d) No contact information;
    (e) No external links;
    (f) No false or exaggerated claims;
    (g) No copied content from other sources.

4.6 FORMATTING

    (a) Use headings and subheadings;
    (b) Use bullet points for lists;
    (c) Use tables for specifications;
    (d) Use paragraphs for narratives;
    (e) Maintain consistent style.

================================================================================
                    SECTION 5: IMAGE REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

5.1 MAIN IMAGE REQUIREMENTS

    (a) Product only on pure white background (RGB 255,255,255);
    (b) Product must fill 85% of frame;
    (c) No text, logos, or watermarks;
    (d) No props or accessories (unless included);
    (e) High resolution and sharp focus.

5.2 TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | ATTRIBUTE               | REQUIREMENT                                 |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | Format                  | JPEG, PNG, GIF                              |
    | Minimum resolution      | 1000 x 1000 pixels                          |
    | Recommended resolution  | 2000 x 2000 pixels                          |
    | Maximum file size       | 10 MB                                       |
    | Aspect ratio            | 1:1 (square) preferred                      |
    | Color mode              | RGB                                         |
    | DPI                     | 72 or higher                                |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------

5.3 ADDITIONAL IMAGES

    Include multiple images showing:

    (a) Different angles (front, back, sides);
    (b) Close-ups of features;
    (c) Product in use (lifestyle);
    (d) Size reference;
    (e) Package contents;
    (f) Labels and certifications.

    Recommended: 5-8 images per listing.

5.4 IMAGE DO'S

    (a) Use professional photography;
    (b) Show accurate colors;
    (c) Display all included items;
    (d) Show scale with reference objects;
    (e) Include infographics for features;
    (f) Show multiple variants.

5.5 IMAGE DON'TS

    (a) No watermarks or logos;
    (b) No promotional text or badges;
    (c) No borders or frames;
    (d) No collages for main image;
    (e) No blurry or pixelated images;
    (f) No stock photos;
    (g) No images of other products;
    (h) No misleading representations.

5.6 CATEGORY-SPECIFIC IMAGE GUIDELINES

    (a) APPAREL
        - Model or mannequin images
        - Flat lay images
        - Detail shots of fabric/stitching

    (b) ELECTRONICS
        - All ports and connections
        - Screen displays (if applicable)
        - With accessories

    (c) FURNITURE
        - Room setting images
        - Multiple angles
        - Dimension diagrams

================================================================================
                    SECTION 6: PRICING GUIDELINES
================================================================================

6.1 PRICE DISPLAY

    (a) MRP (Maximum Retail Price): Mandatory
    (b) Selling Price: Your offer price
    (c) Discount: Calculated automatically
    (d) Currency: INR only

6.2 PRICING RULES

    (a) Selling price cannot exceed MRP;
    (b) MRP must match product packaging;
    (c) Include all taxes in price;
    (d) No hidden charges;
    (e) Accurate pricing always.

6.3 MRP REQUIREMENTS

    (a) Enter MRP printed on product;
    (b) For unpackaged items, use market standard;
    (c) For services, enter service price;
    (d) Keep MRP consistent with market.

6.4 PRICING ACCURACY

    (a) Update prices promptly for changes;
    (b) Honor listed prices for orders;
    (c) No price gouging;
    (d) Fair pricing practices.

6.5 BULK/COMBO PRICING

    (a) Clearly state quantity in title;
    (b) Per-unit price recommended;
    (c) Accurate MRP for combo.

6.6 GST AND TAXES

    (a) Prices must be inclusive of GST;
    (b) Correct HSN code required;
    (c) Applicable tax rate displayed.

================================================================================
                    SECTION 8: LEGAL AND COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

8.1 LEGAL METROLOGY REQUIREMENTS

    Products must display:

    (a) Name and address of manufacturer/packer/importer;
    (b) Country of origin;
    (c) Net quantity;
    (d) MRP inclusive of all taxes;
    (e) Month and year of manufacture/packing/import;
    (f) Customer care details.

8.2 CONSUMER PROTECTION REQUIREMENTS

    As per E-Commerce Rules, 2020:

    (a) Accurate product description;
    (b) Clear images representing actual product;
    (c) Total price including taxes;
    (d) Delivery charges separately displayed;
    (e) Expiry date for applicable products;
    (f) Country of origin;
    (g) Exchange/return/refund policy;
    (h) Warranty/guarantee information;
    (i) Grievance redressal mechanism.

8.3 PRODUCT CERTIFICATIONS

    Display applicable certifications:

    (a) BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards);
    (b) FSSAI (Food Safety);
    (c) CDSCO (Drugs and Cosmetics);
    (d) AGMARK (Agricultural products);
    (e) ISI mark;
    (f) Energy rating (appliances).

8.4 SAFETY COMPLIANCE

    (a) Age warnings for children's products;
    (b) Hazard warnings for applicable products;
    (c) Usage warnings and precautions;
    (d) Storage and handling instructions.

8.5 IMPORT COMPLIANCE

    For imported products:

    (a) Importer name and address;
    (b) Country of origin;
    (c) Import license details (if required);
    (d) BIS registration (for applicable products).

8.6 CLAIMS AND CERTIFICATIONS

    (a) Only make verifiable claims;
    (b) Provide proof for certifications;
    (c) No false or misleading claims;
    (d) Organic, natural claims must be certified.

                    SECTION 9: PROHIBITED LISTINGS
================================================================================

9.1 PROHIBITED PRODUCTS

    The following cannot be listed:

    (a) Counterfeit or replica products;
    (b) Stolen goods;
    (c) Weapons and ammunition;
    (d) Drugs and controlled substances;
    (e) Tobacco and tobacco products;
    (f) Alcohol;
    (g) Pornographic material;
    (h) Hazardous materials;
    (i) Wildlife and wildlife products;
    (j) Products violating IP rights;
    (k) Recalled products;
    (l) Prescription medicines;
    (m) Products prohibited by law.

    Complete prohibited list in Seller Policy.

9.2 PROHIBITED CONTENT

    Listings must not contain:

    (a) Offensive or discriminatory content;
    (b) Adult content;
    (c) Violence or hate speech;
    (d) Contact information;
    (e) External links;
    (f) Promotional language;
    (g) False or misleading claims;
    (h) Competitor references.

9.3 PROHIBITED PRACTICES

    (a) Keyword stuffing;
    (b) Hidden text;
    (c) Fake reviews or ratings;
    (d) Incorrect categorization;
    (e) Misleading images;
    (f) Bait and switch.

                    SECTION 10: LISTING QUALITY SCORE
================================================================================

10.1 QUALITY SCORE COMPONENTS

    Listings are scored on:

    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | COMPONENT                 | WEIGHT      | CRITERIA                    |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | Title quality             | 15%         | Format, completeness        |
    | Image quality             | 25%         | Resolution, count, format   |
    | Description quality       | 20%         | Completeness, formatting    |
    | Attribute completeness    | 20%         | All fields filled           |
    | Pricing accuracy          | 10%         | MRP, selling price          |
    | Compliance                | 10%         | Legal requirements met      |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------

10.2 SCORE RANGES

    (a) 90-100: Excellent - Featured listings
    (b) 70-89: Good - Standard visibility
    (c) 50-69: Needs improvement - Reduced visibility
    (d) Below 50: Poor - May be suppressed

10.3 IMPROVING QUALITY SCORE

    (a) Complete all mandatory fields;
    (b) Add recommended number of images;
    (c) Write detailed descriptions;
    (d) Use proper formatting;
    (e) Follow all guidelines.

10.4 QUALITY SCORE BENEFITS

    High-quality listings receive:

    (a) Higher search ranking;
    (b) Featured placement;
    (c) Higher conversion rates;
    (d) Better customer reviews;
    (e) Lower return rates.

                    SECTION 11: CONTENT MODERATION
================================================================================

11.1 REVIEW PROCESS

    (a) Automated screening on submission;
    (b) Manual review for flagged listings;
    (c) Quality sampling of approved listings;
    (d) Customer-reported review.

11.2 MODERATION ACTIONS

    (a) APPROVE: Listing published
    (b) EDIT: Changes required before approval
    (c) REJECT: Does not meet guidelines
    (d) SUPPRESS: Removed from search
    (e) DELETE: Permanently removed

11.3 COMMON REJECTION REASONS

    (a) Poor image quality;
    (b) Promotional text in title;
    (c) Incomplete information;
    (d) Incorrect category;
    (e) Policy violation;
    (f) Prohibited product.

11.4 APPEAL PROCESS

    (a) Submit appeal within 7 days;
    (b) Provide corrected listing;
    (c) Review completed in 3-5 days;
    (d) Decision communicated via email.

11.5 REPEAT VIOLATIONS

    (a) Warning for first violation;
    (b) Listing suppression for repeated issues;
    (c) Account review for persistent problems;
    (d) Possible account suspension.
