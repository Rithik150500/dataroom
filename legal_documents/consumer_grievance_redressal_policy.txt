                    SECTION 1: INTRODUCTION AND PURPOSE
================================================================================

1.1 PURPOSE

    TechVenture India Private Limited ("Company," "we," "our") is
    committed to providing excellent customer service and resolving
    consumer grievances promptly and fairly. This Consumer Grievance
    Redressal Policy outlines the mechanism for addressing consumer
    complaints in compliance with applicable laws.

1.2 COMMITMENT

    We are committed to:

    (a) Acknowledging all grievances within 48 hours;
    (b) Resolving grievances within 30 days;
    (c) Providing fair and impartial resolution;
    (d) Continuous improvement based on feedback;
    (e) Transparent communication throughout the process.

1.3 SCOPE

    This policy covers grievances related to:

    (a) Products purchased on our platform;
    (b) Services provided by us;
    (c) Third-party seller products (as marketplace);
    (d) Delivery and fulfillment;
    (e) Payments and refunds;
    (f) Data privacy and security;
    (g) Any other platform-related concerns.

                    SECTION 4: TYPES OF GRIEVANCES
================================================================================

4.1 PRODUCT-RELATED GRIEVANCES

    (a) Product not as described;
    (b) Defective or damaged product;
    (c) Counterfeit or fake product;
    (d) Missing items or accessories;
    (e) Quality not meeting expectations;
    (f) Safety concerns with product.

4.2 ORDER AND DELIVERY GRIEVANCES

    (a) Order not received;
    (b) Significant delivery delay;
    (c) Wrong product delivered;
    (d) Partial delivery;
    (e) Delivery to wrong address;
    (f) Package damaged during transit.

4.3 PAYMENT AND REFUND GRIEVANCES

    (a) Amount debited but order not placed;
    (b) Double charge for same order;
    (c) Refund not received;
    (d) Partial refund received;
    (e) Incorrect charges;
    (f) Promotional credit not applied.

4.4 SERVICE-RELATED GRIEVANCES

    (a) Poor customer support;
    (b) Unresponsive service;
    (c) Misleading information;
    (d) Difficulty in returns/exchanges;
    (e) Account-related issues.

4.5 SELLER-RELATED GRIEVANCES

    (a) Third-party seller misconduct;
    (b) False product information;
    (c) Non-delivery by seller;
    (d) Seller not honoring warranty;
    (e) Communication issues with seller.

4.6 PRIVACY AND DATA GRIEVANCES

    (a) Unauthorized use of personal data;
    (b) Data breach or leak;
    (c) Unwanted marketing communications;
    (d) Difficulty exercising data rights;
    (e) Incorrect personal information.

                    SECTION 6: GRIEVANCE HANDLING PROCESS
================================================================================

6.1 PROCESS OVERVIEW

    STEP 1: RECEIPT AND ACKNOWLEDGMENT
    - Grievance received through any channel
    - Unique ticket number generated
    - Acknowledgment sent within 48 hours
    - Priority assigned based on nature

    STEP 2: INITIAL REVIEW
    - Grievance reviewed by support team
    - Category and subcategory assigned
    - Additional information requested if needed
    - Routed to appropriate department

    STEP 3: INVESTIGATION
    - Relevant team investigates
    - Order/transaction history reviewed
    - Seller contacted if applicable
    - Root cause analysis performed

    STEP 4: RESOLUTION PROPOSAL
    - Resolution options identified
    - Proposal communicated to consumer
    - Consumer acceptance sought
    - Alternative solutions if not accepted

    STEP 5: IMPLEMENTATION
    - Approved resolution implemented
    - Refund/replacement processed
    - Actions taken against violators
    - Consumer notified of completion

    STEP 6: CLOSURE AND FEEDBACK
    - Grievance marked resolved
    - Closure communication sent
    - Feedback requested
    - Record maintained

6.2 PRIORITY LEVELS

    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | PRIORITY     | DESCRIPTION                    | RESPONSE TIME         |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | CRITICAL     | Safety issue, fraud, data      | 4 hours               |
    |              | breach, legal notice           |                       |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | HIGH         | Non-delivery, wrong product,   | 24 hours              |
    |              | payment issue, stuck refund    |                       |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | MEDIUM       | Quality issues, partial        | 48 hours              |
    |              | delivery, service complaints   |                       |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | LOW          | General inquiries,             | 72 hours              |
    |              | suggestions, feedback          |                       |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------

6.3 INVESTIGATION PROCESS

    For product-related grievances:
    (a) Review product listing and seller history;
    (b) Request product photos from consumer;
    (c) Verify delivery and tracking;
    (d) Consult with seller if applicable;
    (e) Expert assessment if needed.

    For payment-related grievances:
    (a) Transaction verification with payment gateway;
    (b) Bank communication if needed;
    (c) Reconciliation of accounts;
    (d) Refund status check.

    For seller-related grievances:
    (a) Seller contacted for response;
    (b) Seller given 48 hours to respond;
    (c) Platform mediation if unresolved;
    (d) Platform decision if no resolution.

                    SECTION 9: CONSUMER RIGHTS
================================================================================

9.1 RIGHTS UNDER CONSUMER PROTECTION ACT, 2019

    As a consumer, you have the right to:

    (a) RIGHT TO SAFETY
        - Protection against hazardous products
        - Products meeting safety standards

    (b) RIGHT TO BE INFORMED
        - Accurate product information
        - Clear pricing and terms
        - No misleading advertisements

    (c) RIGHT TO CHOOSE
        - Access to variety of products
        - Fair pricing without manipulation

    (d) RIGHT TO BE HEARD
        - File complaints
        - Have grievances addressed
        - Representation in consumer forums

    (e) RIGHT TO SEEK REDRESSAL
        - Fair resolution of complaints
        - Compensation for losses
        - Replacement or refund

    (f) RIGHT TO CONSUMER EDUCATION
        - Information about consumer rights
        - Awareness of redressal mechanisms

9.2 RIGHTS UNDER E-COMMERCE RULES, 2020

    As an e-commerce consumer, you have additional rights to:

    (a) Receive contract and order details;
    (b) Cancel orders before dispatch;
    (c) Know seller details;
    (d) Easy returns and refunds;
    (e) Clear grievance mechanism;
    (f) Protection of personal data.

9.3 YOUR RESPONSIBILITIES

    To facilitate smooth grievance resolution:

    (a) Provide accurate and complete information;
    (b) Respond to queries promptly;
    (c) Maintain proof of purchase;
    (d) Follow return and refund policies;
    (e) Communicate respectfully.

                    SECTION 11: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q1: How long does it take to get a response to my grievance?
A:  We acknowledge all grievances within 48 hours and aim to resolve
    them within 30 days.

Q2: How can I track my grievance status?
A:  Use your ticket number to track status on our website or contact
    customer support with the ticket number.

Q3: What if I'm not satisfied with the resolution?
A:  You can escalate to the next level as per our escalation matrix
    or approach external consumer forums.

Q4: Can I file a grievance for a third-party seller product?
A:  Yes. As a marketplace, we facilitate grievance resolution and
    mediate between buyers and sellers.

Q5: Is there a time limit to file a grievance?
A:  We recommend filing within 30 days of the incident. However,
    we accept grievances up to 1 year for most issues.

Q6: What documents do I need to file a grievance?
A:  Order number, invoice, product photos, payment details, and any
    previous correspondence.

Q7: Can I file a grievance anonymously?
A:  For effective resolution, we need your contact details. However,
    we maintain confidentiality of your information.

Q8: Will I be charged for filing a grievance?
A:  No. Filing a grievance with us is completely free.

Q9: What happens if a seller doesn't respond to my grievance?
A:  We will mediate and may take action against the seller including
    penalty or suspension. We may also directly resolve with refund.

Q10: Can I withdraw my grievance?
A:   Yes. Contact us with your ticket number to withdraw.
