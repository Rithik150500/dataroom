                        III. OBJECTS CLAUSE

A. THE MAIN OBJECTS TO BE PURSUED BY THE COMPANY ON ITS INCORPORATION ARE:

   1. To carry on the business of development, designing, programming,
      creation, maintenance, marketing, licensing and sale of computer
      software, applications, mobile applications, web applications,
      cloud-based solutions and Software as a Service (SaaS) products.

   2. To provide information technology enabled services including but not
      limited to software development, system integration, data processing,
      data management, business process outsourcing, and technology consulting.

   3. To develop, own, operate, maintain, and market enterprise software
      solutions, including enterprise resource planning (ERP), customer
      relationship management (CRM), human resource management systems (HRMS),
      and related business automation tools.


B. OBJECTS INCIDENTAL OR ANCILLARY TO THE ATTAINMENT OF THE MAIN OBJECTS:

   1. To acquire by purchase, lease, license, exchange, hire or otherwise,
      and to hold, develop, use, sell, transfer, assign, or otherwise dispose
      of any intellectual property rights including patents, copyrights,
      trademarks, trade secrets, know-how, and technology.

   2. To carry on research and development activities in the field of
      information technology, software development, artificial intelligence,
      machine learning, data analytics, and related technologies.

   3. To establish, maintain, and operate data centers, server farms, cloud
      infrastructure, and other technology infrastructure.

   4. To enter into collaborations, partnerships, joint ventures, and
      strategic alliances with other companies or entities for furthering
      the objects of the Company.

   5. To provide training, consulting, support, and maintenance services
      related to the software and services provided by the Company.

   6. To apply for, obtain, register, purchase, lease, or otherwise acquire,
      and to hold, own, use, operate, develop, license, sell, assign or
      otherwise dispose of any domain names, trade names, brands, and other
      proprietary rights.

   7. To employ and engage professionals, consultants, advisors, and other
      personnel as may be necessary for the conduct of the business.

   8. To open and operate bank accounts, and to borrow money or raise funds
      as may be required for the business of the Company.

   9. To invest the funds of the Company in such manner as may be determined
      by the Board of Directors from time to time.

   10. To acquire and undertake the whole or any part of the business,
       property, assets, and liabilities of any person or company carrying
       on any business which the Company is authorized to carry on.

   11. To apply for and obtain any statutory or regulatory licenses,
       permissions, approvals, registrations, or certifications as may be
       required for carrying on the business of the Company.

   12. To establish branch offices, subsidiaries, or representative offices
       in India or abroad as may be required for the business of the Company.

   13. To engage in export and import of computer software, hardware, and
       related services.

   14. To do all such other lawful things as are incidental or conducive to
       the attainment of the above objects or any of them.
