                        ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

The entity has been recognized as a Startup based on satisfaction of the
following criteria as per the Startup India notification dated 19th February,
2019:

1. INCORPORATION/REGISTRATION
   [✓] Entity is incorporated/registered in India
   [✓] Entity is a Private Limited Company registered under Companies Act, 2013

2. AGE OF ENTITY
   [✓] Period of existence and operations does not exceed 10 years from the
       date of incorporation

3. ANNUAL TURNOVER
   [✓] Annual turnover has not exceeded Rs. 100 Crore in any of the
       previous financial years

4. ORIGINAL ENTITY
   [✓] Entity has not been formed by splitting up or reconstruction of an
       existing business

5. INNOVATION & SCALABILITY
   [✓] Entity is working towards innovation, development or improvement of
       products/processes/services
   [✓] Entity has a scalable business model with high potential of employment
       generation or wealth creation

                        BENEFITS AVAILABLE

As a recognized Startup, the entity is eligible for the following benefits:

1. SELF-CERTIFICATION
   - Compliance under 6 Labour Laws
   - Compliance under 3 Environmental Laws

2. TAX BENEFITS (Subject to eligibility under Section 80IAC)
   - Income tax exemption for 3 consecutive years out of first 10 years
   - Exemption from tax on Long Term Capital Gains under Section 54EE
   - Exemption from Angel Tax under Section 56(2)(viib)

3. EASIER PUBLIC PROCUREMENT
   - Exemption from prior experience/turnover criteria
   - Relaxation of earnest money deposit

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY SUPPORT
   - Fast-track patent examination
   - 80% rebate in patent filing fees
   - 50% rebate in trademark filing fees

5. FUNDING SUPPORT
   - Access to Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS)
   - Eligibility for various government schemes

6. EASIER WINDING UP
   - Fast-track exit within 90 days under Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code

                        SECTION 80IAC CERTIFICATION

Certificate No.:              80IAC/2020/12345
Date of Issue:                15th January, 2020
Valid From:                   FY 2019-20
Valid Until:                  FY 2028-29 (10 years from incorporation)

This is to certify that TechVenture India Private Limited has been approved
for tax benefits under Section 80-IAC of the Income Tax Act, 1961.

The entity is eligible for 100% deduction of profits and gains derived from
an eligible business for a period of 3 consecutive assessment years out of
10 years beginning from the year of incorporation.

Selected Assessment Years for Deduction:
1. Assessment Year 2022-23 (FY 2021-22)
2. Assessment Year 2023-24 (FY 2022-23)
3. Assessment Year 2024-25 (FY 2023-24)

Note: The startup was loss-making in initial years and elected to claim
deduction from AY 2022-23 onwards when it became profitable.
