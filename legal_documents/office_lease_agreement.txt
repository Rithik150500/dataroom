                   FOR OFFICE PREMISES

TECHVENTURE INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED, a company incorporated under the Companies

================================================================================

                          RECITALS

WHEREAS:

A. The Lessor is the owner and in lawful possession of the property described
   in Schedule I hereto (the "Premises").

B. The Lessee is engaged in the business of software development and has
   approached the Lessor for taking the Premises on lease for use as its
   corporate office.

C. The Lessor has agreed to let out and the Lessee has agreed to take on
   lease the Premises on the terms and conditions set forth herein.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the rent and covenants herein contained,
the parties agree as follows:

================================================================================

                   ARTICLE 1: LEASE OF PREMISES

1.1 GRANT OF LEASE
    The Lessor hereby grants to the Lessee, and the Lessee hereby takes from
    the Lessor, a lease of the Premises described in Schedule I, together with
    the right to use common areas, amenities, and parking spaces as specified.

1.2 PURPOSE
    The Premises shall be used by the Lessee solely for the purpose of
    operating its software development and IT services business as a
    corporate office. The Lessee shall not use the Premises for any illegal
    or immoral purpose.

================================================================================

                      ARTICLE 2: TERM

2.1 LEASE TERM
    The lease shall be for a period of 3 (three) years commencing from
    January 1, 2023 and ending on December 31, 2025.

2.2 RENEWAL
    The Lessee shall have the option to renew this lease for an additional
    term of 3 (three) years, subject to mutual agreement on rent and other
    terms. The Lessee shall provide written notice of its intention to renew
    at least 90 days before the expiry of the current term.

2.3 LOCK-IN PERIOD
    The first 2 (two) years of the lease term shall be the lock-in period.
    Neither party may terminate the lease during the lock-in period except
    for breach of this Agreement.

================================================================================

                      ARTICLE 3: RENT

3.1 MONTHLY RENT
    The Lessee shall pay to the Lessor a monthly rent of Rs. 3,00,000
    (Rupees Three Lakhs Only), payable in advance on or before the 5th day
    of each month.

3.2 ESCALATION
    The rent shall be increased by 5% (five percent) at the end of each year
    during the lease term. The rent for subsequent years shall be:
    - Year 1 (2023): Rs. 3,00,000 per month
    - Year 2 (2024): Rs. 3,15,000 per month
    - Year 3 (2025): Rs. 3,30,750 per month

3.3 PAYMENT METHOD
    Rent shall be paid by bank transfer to the Lessor's account:
    - Account Name: Sharma Properties LLP
    - Bank: ICICI Bank, Indiranagar Branch
    - Account No.: 123456789012
    - IFSC: ICIC0001234

3.4 LATE PAYMENT
    If rent is not paid within 7 days of the due date, the Lessee shall pay
    interest at the rate of 18% per annum on the outstanding amount.

3.5 TDS
    The Lessee shall deduct TDS at the applicable rate on the rent and
    provide TDS certificate to the Lessor quarterly.

================================================================================

                  ARTICLE 4: SECURITY DEPOSIT

4.1 AMOUNT
    The Lessee has paid a security deposit of Rs. 9,00,000 (Rupees Nine Lakhs
    Only), equivalent to 3 months' rent, to the Lessor.

4.2 INTEREST
    The security deposit shall be interest-free.

4.3 REFUND
    The security deposit shall be refunded to the Lessee within 30 days of
    the termination of this lease and vacation of the Premises, after
    deducting:
    (a) Any unpaid rent or other charges
    (b) Cost of repairs for damages beyond normal wear and tear
    (c) Utility bills and other dues

4.4 ADJUSTMENT
    The security deposit shall not be adjusted against rent during the lease
    term without the Lessor's written consent.

================================================================================

            ARTICLE 5: MAINTENANCE AND UTILITIES

5.1 MAINTENANCE CHARGES
    The Lessee shall pay common area maintenance charges of Rs. 15,000
    (Rupees Fifteen Thousand Only) per month, which includes:
    - Security services
    - Common area cleaning
    - Lift maintenance
    - Common area electricity
    - Water supply

5.2 UTILITIES
    The Lessee shall pay directly for all utilities consumed in the Premises:
    - Electricity (separate meter installed)
    - Internet and telephone
    - Any other utilities used exclusively by the Lessee

5.3 INTERNAL MAINTENANCE
    The Lessee shall be responsible for internal maintenance and minor
    repairs (up to Rs. 10,000 per instance) of the Premises.

5.4 STRUCTURAL REPAIRS
    The Lessor shall be responsible for structural repairs, major plumbing
    and electrical repairs, and repairs to common areas.

================================================================================

              ARTICLE 6: IMPROVEMENTS AND ALTERATIONS

6.1 PERMITTED ALTERATIONS
    The Lessee may make non-structural interior alterations to the Premises
    with the prior written consent of the Lessor, which shall not be
    unreasonably withheld.

6.2 LESSOR'S PROPERTY
    All permanent improvements and fixtures installed by the Lessee shall
    become the property of the Lessor upon termination of the lease, unless
    the Lessor requires removal.

6.3 RESTORATION
    Upon termination, the Lessee shall restore the Premises to its original
    condition, subject to normal wear and tear, unless otherwise agreed.

================================================================================

                 ARTICLE 7: INSURANCE

7.1 LESSEE'S INSURANCE
    The Lessee shall maintain the following insurance policies:
    (a) Property insurance for all furniture, fixtures, and equipment
    (b) Public liability insurance
    (c) Any other insurance required by law

7.2 LESSOR'S INSURANCE
    The Lessor shall maintain building insurance covering fire and natural
    disasters.

================================================================================

            ARTICLE 8: COMPLIANCE WITH LAWS

8.1 LESSEE'S COMPLIANCE
    The Lessee shall comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and
    building rules, and obtain all necessary licenses and permits for its
    business operations.

8.2 LESSOR'S COMPLIANCE
    The Lessor shall ensure that the building complies with all applicable
    building codes, fire safety regulations, and other legal requirements.

================================================================================

                 ARTICLE 9: ASSIGNMENT AND SUBLETTING

9.1 RESTRICTION
    The Lessee shall not assign this lease or sublet the Premises or any
    part thereof without the prior written consent of the Lessor.

9.2 GROUP COMPANIES
    The Lessor's consent shall not be unreasonably withheld for assignment
    to the Lessee's affiliates or group companies.

================================================================================

                  ARTICLE 10: TERMINATION

10.1 TERMINATION BY NOTICE
     After the lock-in period, either party may terminate this lease by
     giving 90 days' prior written notice to the other party.

10.2 TERMINATION FOR BREACH
     Either party may terminate this lease immediately if the other party:
     (a) Commits a material breach and fails to cure within 30 days of notice
     (b) Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy

10.3 LESSOR'S RIGHT TO TERMINATE
     The Lessor may terminate this lease if the Lessee:
     (a) Fails to pay rent for 2 consecutive months
     (b) Uses the Premises for illegal purposes
     (c) Causes substantial damage to the Premises

10.4 HANDOVER
     Upon termination, the Lessee shall:
     (a) Vacate and hand over the Premises in good condition
     (b) Remove all its property and belongings
     (c) Clear all dues and obtain a no-dues certificate

================================================================================

                  ARTICLE 11: INDEMNITY

11.1 LESSEE'S INDEMNITY
     The Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless the Lessor against all
     claims, losses, and expenses arising from:
     (a) The Lessee's use of the Premises
     (b) Any breach of this Agreement by the Lessee
     (c) Any negligence or willful misconduct of the Lessee

11.2 LESSOR'S INDEMNITY
     The Lessor shall indemnify and hold harmless the Lessee against all
     claims arising from any defect in the Lessor's title to the Premises.

================================================================================

                ARTICLE 12: GENERAL PROVISIONS

12.1 ENTIRE AGREEMENT
     This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

12.2 AMENDMENTS
     This Agreement may be amended only by written agreement signed by
     both parties.

12.3 NOTICES
     All notices shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses
     specified herein.

12.4 GOVERNING LAW
     This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India. Any disputes
     shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of courts in Bangalore.

12.5 REGISTRATION
     This Agreement shall be registered with the Sub-Registrar as required
     under the Registration Act, 1908.
