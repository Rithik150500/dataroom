================================================================================
                TECHVENTURE INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED
                    EMPLOYEE STOCK OPTION PLAN 2020
                      (TechVenture ESOP Scheme 2020)
================================================================================

                         PREAMBLE

TechVenture India Private Limited (the "Company") establishes this Employee
Stock Option Plan 2020 (the "Plan" or "Scheme") with the objective of
attracting, retaining, and motivating talented employees by providing them
an opportunity to participate in the growth and success of the Company.

This Plan has been approved by the Board of Directors of the Company on
March 20, 2020 and by the Shareholders of the Company at the Extraordinary
General Meeting held on April 10, 2020.

================================================================================

                    ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

1.1  "Board" means the Board of Directors of the Company.

1.2  "Compensation Committee" means the committee constituted by the Board
     to administer this Plan, or in the absence of such committee, the Board
     itself.

1.3  "Company" means TechVenture India Private Limited.

1.4  "Employee" means a permanent employee of the Company working in India
     or outside India, including a Director of the Company, whether whole-time
     or part-time.

1.5  "Exercise Period" means the period after vesting within which the
     Grantee should exercise the vested Options.

1.6  "Exercise Price" means the price payable by the Grantee to the Company
     for exercising the Options granted under the Plan.

1.7  "Fair Market Value" means the value of an equity share determined by a
     merchant banker or an independent valuer as required under applicable law.

1.8  "Grant" means the Options granted to an eligible Employee under this Plan.

1.9  "Grant Date" means the date on which the Board or Compensation Committee
     grants Options to an eligible Employee.

1.10 "Grantee" means an Employee who has been granted Options under this Plan.

1.11 "Option" means the right, but not the obligation, granted to an Employee
     to apply for and be allotted equity shares of the Company on satisfaction
     of vesting conditions.

1.12 "Option Agreement" means the agreement between the Company and the
     Grantee setting out the terms and conditions of the Grant.

1.13 "Plan" or "Scheme" means this TechVenture ESOP Scheme 2020.

1.14 "Vesting" means the process by which the Grantee acquires the right to
     exercise the Options granted.

1.15 "Vesting Period" means the period during which vesting occurs as per
     the vesting schedule.

================================================================================

                 ARTICLE 2: ADMINISTRATION

2.1  COMPENSATION COMMITTEE
     This Plan shall be administered by the Compensation Committee appointed
     by the Board. Until such time as the Compensation Committee is constituted,
     the Board shall administer the Plan.

2.2  POWERS OF THE COMPENSATION COMMITTEE
     The Compensation Committee shall have the following powers:
     (a) To grant Options to eligible Employees
     (b) To determine the number of Options to be granted to each Employee
     (c) To determine the Exercise Price
     (d) To determine the Vesting Period and Vesting Schedule
     (e) To determine the Exercise Period
     (f) To interpret the provisions of this Plan
     (g) To prescribe forms and procedures for administration of this Plan
     (h) To accelerate vesting in appropriate circumstances
     (i) To make all other determinations necessary for administration

2.3  DECISIONS BINDING
     All decisions of the Compensation Committee shall be final and binding
     on all parties.

================================================================================

             ARTICLE 3: ELIGIBILITY AND GRANT

3.1  ELIGIBILITY
     The following persons are eligible to participate in this Plan:
     (a) Permanent employees of the Company
     (b) Directors of the Company (whether whole-time or part-time)
     (c) Employees and Directors of subsidiary companies (if any)

     The following persons are NOT eligible:
     (a) An Employee who is a Promoter or belongs to the Promoter Group
     (b) A Director who holds more than 10% of the outstanding equity shares

3.2  CRITERIA FOR GRANT
     The Compensation Committee shall consider the following factors in
     determining Grants:
     (a) Job level and role criticality
     (b) Performance and potential
     (c) Tenure with the Company
     (d) Contribution to the Company's growth
     (e) Market benchmarks and retention requirements

3.3  OPTION POOL
     The maximum number of Options that may be granted under this Plan shall
     not exceed 3,750 (Three Thousand Seven Hundred and Fifty), representing
     15% of the issued and paid-up equity share capital of the Company as on
     the date of approval of this Plan.

3.4  GRANT PROCESS
     (a) The Compensation Committee shall approve each Grant
     (b) The Company shall issue an Option Agreement to the Grantee
     (c) The Grantee shall sign and return the Option Agreement within
         30 days of receipt
     (d) Failure to return the signed Option Agreement shall result in
         automatic cancellation of the Grant

================================================================================

                    ARTICLE 4: EXERCISE PRICE

4.1  DETERMINATION OF EXERCISE PRICE
     The Exercise Price for each Grant shall be determined by the Compensation
     Committee and shall not be less than the face value of the equity shares.

4.2  STANDARD EXERCISE PRICE
     Unless otherwise determined by the Compensation Committee, the Exercise
     Price shall be the face value of the equity shares, i.e., Rs. 100 per share.

4.3  DISCLOSURE
     The Exercise Price shall be clearly stated in the Option Agreement.

================================================================================

                     ARTICLE 5: VESTING

5.1  STANDARD VESTING SCHEDULE
     Unless otherwise specified in the Option Agreement, Options shall vest
     according to the following schedule:

     After Completion of     |  Cumulative Vesting  |  Options Vesting
     ------------------------|----------------------|------------------
     1 year (Cliff)          |       25%            |    Per Grant
     2 years                 |       50%            |    Per Grant
     3 years                 |       75%            |    Per Grant
     4 years                 |      100%            |    Per Grant

     Monthly vesting after the one-year cliff: Options shall vest monthly
     on a pro-rata basis between the end of Year 1 and Year 4.

5.2  VESTING CONDITIONS
     Vesting is subject to the following conditions:
     (a) Continued employment with the Company
     (b) Satisfactory performance as determined by the Company
     (c) Compliance with Company policies and this Plan

5.3  ACCELERATION OF VESTING
     The Compensation Committee may accelerate vesting upon:
     (a) Change of Control of the Company
     (b) Initial Public Offering (IPO)
     (c) Death or permanent disability of the Grantee
     (d) Any other circumstance deemed appropriate by the Committee

5.4  CHANGE OF CONTROL
     "Change of Control" means:
     (a) Sale or transfer of more than 50% of the voting securities
     (b) Sale of substantially all of the assets of the Company
     (c) Merger or consolidation where Company's shareholders own less than
         50% of the surviving entity

================================================================================

                    ARTICLE 6: EXERCISE

6.1  EXERCISE PERIOD
     Vested Options may be exercised:
     (a) During the Exercise Period specified in the Option Agreement
     (b) Standard Exercise Period: 10 years from the Grant Date
     (c) Extended Exercise Period upon termination: as per Article 7

6.2  EXERCISE PROCEDURE
     To exercise Options, the Grantee shall:
     (a) Submit a written Exercise Notice to the Company
     (b) Specify the number of Options being exercised
     (c) Pay the Exercise Price in full by bank transfer or cheque
     (d) Complete any tax withholding requirements

6.3  PARTIAL EXERCISE
     The Grantee may exercise vested Options in whole or in part, subject
     to a minimum of 50 Options per exercise (or such other minimum as
     specified in the Option Agreement).

6.4  ALLOTMENT OF SHARES
     Upon valid exercise and receipt of Exercise Price:
     (a) The Company shall allot equity shares within 15 days
     (b) Share certificates shall be issued within 30 days
     (c) The Company shall update the Register of Members
     (d) The Company shall file necessary forms with the ROC

================================================================================

          ARTICLE 7: TERMINATION OF EMPLOYMENT

7.1  VOLUNTARY RESIGNATION
     If a Grantee voluntarily resigns:
     (a) All unvested Options shall lapse immediately
     (b) Vested but unexercised Options may be exercised within 90 days
         from the last day of employment
     (c) Any unexercised Options after this period shall lapse

7.2  TERMINATION FOR CAUSE
     If employment is terminated for Cause:
     (a) All Options (vested and unvested) shall lapse immediately
     (b) "Cause" includes fraud, dishonesty, breach of confidentiality,
         material breach of employment agreement, or criminal conviction

7.3  TERMINATION WITHOUT CAUSE
     If employment is terminated by the Company without Cause:
     (a) All unvested Options shall lapse immediately
     (b) Vested Options may be exercised within 180 days from termination
     (c) At the discretion of the Committee, additional vesting may be granted

7.4  DEATH OR DISABILITY
     In case of death or permanent disability:
     (a) All unvested Options shall vest immediately (acceleration)
     (b) The legal heirs or the disabled Grantee may exercise within 1 year
     (c) The Exercise Price shall remain unchanged

7.5  RETIREMENT
     Upon retirement at normal retirement age:
     (a) All Options shall continue to vest as per the original schedule
     (b) Vested Options may be exercised within 1 year from retirement

================================================================================

            ARTICLE 8: NON-TRANSFERABILITY

8.1  RESTRICTION ON TRANSFER
     Options granted under this Plan:
     (a) Are personal to the Grantee
     (b) Cannot be sold, transferred, assigned, pledged, or encumbered
     (c) Shall not be subject to attachment or execution
     (d) May be transferred only upon death to legal heirs

8.2  TRANSFER OF SHARES
     Equity shares allotted upon exercise:
     (a) May be subject to lock-in periods as determined by the Committee
     (b) Shall be subject to the Company's transfer restrictions (ROFO, etc.)
     (c) Shall be subject to any applicable shareholder agreements

================================================================================

                     ARTICLE 9: TAXATION

9.1  TAX RESPONSIBILITY
     The Grantee shall be solely responsible for all tax consequences
     arising from:
     (a) Grant of Options
     (b) Vesting of Options
     (c) Exercise of Options
     (d) Sale of shares acquired upon exercise

9.2  TAX WITHHOLDING
     The Company shall:
     (a) Deduct tax at source as required under applicable law
     (b) The Grantee shall provide necessary information for tax compliance
     (c) The Company may require the Grantee to make arrangements for tax
         payment before allotment of shares

9.3  TAX ADVICE
     The Grantee is advised to consult independent tax advisors regarding
     the tax implications of participating in this Plan.

================================================================================

       ARTICLE 10: AMENDMENTS AND TERMINATION

10.1 AMENDMENT
     The Board may amend this Plan at any time, provided that:
     (a) Shareholder approval is obtained for material amendments
     (b) No amendment shall adversely affect the rights of existing Grantees
         without their consent
     (c) Changes to vesting conditions require consent of affected Grantees

10.2 TERMINATION
     The Board may terminate this Plan at any time, provided that:
     (a) Options already granted shall continue to be governed by this Plan
     (b) No new Grants shall be made after termination

================================================================================

              ARTICLE 11: GENERAL PROVISIONS

11.1 NO RIGHT TO EMPLOYMENT
     Nothing in this Plan shall be construed as conferring on the Grantee
     any right to continue in employment with the Company.

11.2 GOVERNING LAW
     This Plan shall be governed by the laws of India and the jurisdiction
     of courts in Bangalore, Karnataka.

11.3 COMPLIANCE
     This Plan is intended to comply with:
     (a) Companies Act, 2013 and rules thereunder
     (b) Income Tax Act, 1961 (for taxation of ESOPs)
     (c) Any other applicable laws

11.4 EFFECTIVE DATE
     This Plan shall be effective from April 10, 2020.
