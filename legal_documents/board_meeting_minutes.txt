ITEM 3: APPROVAL OF RELATED PARTY TRANSACTION - OFFICE LEASE RENEWAL

is due for renewal on December 31, 2022.

market rate.

   approved on the following terms:
   - Monthly rent: Rs. 3,00,000 (Year 1)
   - Annual escalation: 5%
   - Lock-in: 2 years

2. The transaction is in ordinary course and at arm's length.

3. The transaction be placed before shareholders at next AGM.

4. Mr. Rahul Sharma be authorized to execute the lease agreement.
