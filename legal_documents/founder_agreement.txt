================================================================================
                        FOUNDER AGREEMENT
                              OF
                TECHVENTURE INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED
================================================================================

THIS FOUNDER AGREEMENT is made on this 14th day of March, 2019

BETWEEN

(1) MR. RAHUL SHARMA, son of Mr. Vijay Sharma, residing at 123 Palm Grove,
    Indiranagar, Bangalore - 560038 (hereinafter referred to as "Founder 1");

AND

(2) MS. PRIYA MEHTA, daughter of Mr. Suresh Mehta, residing at 456 Brigade
    Road, MG Road, Bangalore - 560001 (hereinafter referred to as "Founder 2");

(Founder 1 and Founder 2 are hereinafter collectively referred to as the
"Founders" and individually as a "Founder")

================================================================================

                            RECITALS

WHEREAS:

A. The Founders are desirous of incorporating a private limited company under
   the Companies Act, 2013 to carry on the business of developing and
   providing Software as a Service (SaaS) solutions.

B. The Founders wish to record their respective roles, responsibilities,
   equity ownership, intellectual property assignments, and other terms
   governing their relationship as co-founders of the Company.

C. The Founders acknowledge that this Agreement is intended to establish a
   clear framework for collaboration, dispute resolution, and orderly
   management of their business relationship.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements
hereinafter set forth, the Founders agree as follows:

================================================================================

                    ARTICLE 1: THE COMPANY

1.1 INCORPORATION
    The Founders agree to incorporate a private limited company under the
    name "TechVenture India Private Limited" with the Registrar of Companies,
    Karnataka, with the following initial share capital:

    Authorized Capital: Rs. 50,00,000 (50,000 equity shares of Rs. 100 each)
    Paid-up Capital:    Rs. 20,00,000 (20,000 equity shares of Rs. 100 each)

1.2 BUSINESS PURPOSE
    The Company shall be engaged in the business of developing, marketing,
    and selling SaaS-based enterprise software solutions, including but not
    limited to:
    (a) Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software
    (b) Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software
    (c) Business process automation tools
    (d) Related consulting and implementation services

1.3 REGISTERED OFFICE
    The registered office of the Company shall initially be located at:
    #42, 3rd Floor, Koramangala 5th Block, Bangalore - 560095, Karnataka

================================================================================

                  ARTICLE 2: EQUITY OWNERSHIP

2.1 INITIAL SHAREHOLDING
    The initial equity ownership of the Company shall be as follows:

    Founder 1 (Rahul Sharma):    12,000 shares (60%)
    Founder 2 (Priya Mehta):      8,000 shares (40%)
    Total:                       20,000 shares (100%)

2.2 CAPITAL CONTRIBUTION
    Each Founder agrees to contribute to the paid-up share capital of the
    Company in proportion to their shareholding:

    Founder 1: Rs. 12,00,000 (Rupees Twelve Lakhs Only)
    Founder 2: Rs.  8,00,000 (Rupees Eight Lakhs Only)

2.3 ESOP RESERVE
    The Founders agree to reserve up to 15% of the fully diluted share capital
    for an Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP) to be established within 12 months
    of incorporation.

================================================================================

              ARTICLE 3: ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

3.1 FOUNDER 1 - RAHUL SHARMA
    Designation: Chief Executive Officer (CEO) and Director

    Primary Responsibilities:
    (a) Overall business strategy and direction
    (b) Product vision and roadmap
    (c) Investor relations and fundraising
    (d) Strategic partnerships and alliances
    (e) Board and stakeholder management
    (f) Public relations and brand building

3.2 FOUNDER 2 - PRIYA MEHTA
    Designation: Chief Technology Officer (CTO) and Director

    Primary Responsibilities:
    (a) Technology strategy and architecture
    (b) Software development and engineering
    (c) Product development and quality assurance
    (d) Technology team building and management
    (e) Technical partnerships and vendor management
    (f) Information security and data protection

3.3 SHARED RESPONSIBILITIES
    The Founders shall jointly be responsible for:
    (a) Major hiring decisions
    (b) Budget approvals above Rs. 5 lakhs
    (c) Strategic decisions affecting company direction
    (d) Legal and compliance matters

================================================================================

              ARTICLE 4: DECISION MAKING

4.1 DEADLOCK MATTERS
    The following matters shall require unanimous consent of both Founders:
    (a) Changes to the business model or core product offering
    (b) Raising external funding or debt above Rs. 25 lakhs
    (c) Sale, merger, or acquisition of the Company
    (d) Issuance of new shares (except under approved ESOP)
    (e) Entry into any related party transactions
    (f) Appointment of key executives (CXO level)
    (g) Amendment to this Agreement

4.2 RESOLUTION OF DEADLOCKS
    In the event of a deadlock on any matter requiring unanimous consent:
    (a) The Founders shall first attempt good faith negotiation for 15 days
    (b) If unresolved, the matter shall be referred to a mutually agreed
        advisor or mentor for non-binding mediation
    (c) If still unresolved, the matter shall be referred to arbitration
        as per Article 10 of this Agreement

================================================================================

           ARTICLE 5: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1 ASSIGNMENT OF PRIOR IP
    Each Founder agrees to assign to the Company all intellectual property
    rights in any work, invention, development, or creation that:
    (a) Was created by the Founder prior to the incorporation of the Company
    (b) Is related to or useful for the business of the Company
    (c) Has been incorporated or will be incorporated into the Company's
        products or services

5.2 OWNERSHIP OF COMPANY IP
    All intellectual property created by the Founders during their association
    with the Company and related to the Company's business shall be the sole
    and exclusive property of the Company.

5.3 IP ASSIGNMENT DEED
    Each Founder shall execute a separate IP Assignment Deed substantially
    in the form attached as Schedule A to this Agreement.

================================================================================

                  ARTICLE 6: COMPENSATION

6.1 INITIAL COMPENSATION
    The Founders agree that for the first 12 months of the Company's
    operations, or until the Company raises external funding of at least
    Rs. 50 lakhs, whichever is earlier:

    Founder 1: Rs. 50,000 per month
    Founder 2: Rs. 50,000 per month

6.2 REVIEW OF COMPENSATION
    The Founders' compensation shall be reviewed and adjusted by the Board
    of Directors upon:
    (a) Completion of 12 months of operations
    (b) Successful fundraising
    (c) Achievement of key business milestones

6.3 ESOP GRANTS
    Each Founder shall be entitled to ESOP grants as approved by the Board
    from time to time, subject to the terms of the ESOP Scheme.

================================================================================

               ARTICLE 7: TIME COMMITMENT

7.1 FULL-TIME COMMITMENT
    Each Founder agrees to devote substantially all of their professional
    time and efforts to the business of the Company. "Substantially all"
    shall mean at least 40 hours per week.

7.2 NO COMPETING ACTIVITIES
    During the term of this Agreement and for a period of 2 years thereafter,
    no Founder shall:
    (a) Engage in any business that competes with the Company
    (b) Provide services to any competitor of the Company
    (c) Hold any position in any competitor of the Company
    (d) Own any interest (except passive investment of less than 2%) in any
        competitor of the Company

7.3 EXCEPTIONS
    The following activities are permitted:
    (a) Passive investments in publicly traded companies
    (b) Personal investments in real estate
    (c) Advisory roles in non-competing startups (with Board approval)
    (d) Teaching, speaking, and writing engagements

================================================================================

          ARTICLE 8: DEPARTURE OF FOUNDER

8.1 VOLUNTARY DEPARTURE
    If a Founder voluntarily chooses to leave the Company:
    (a) Such Founder shall provide at least 90 days' prior written notice
    (b) During the notice period, the departing Founder shall cooperate in
        transitioning responsibilities
    (c) The departing Founder's unvested shares (if any) shall be forfeited
    (d) The remaining Founder shall have the right of first refusal to
        purchase the departing Founder's vested shares at fair market value

8.2 INVOLUNTARY DEPARTURE
    A Founder may be asked to leave the Company by the Board of Directors if:
    (a) The Founder commits a material breach of this Agreement
    (b) The Founder commits fraud, dishonesty, or willful misconduct
    (c) The Founder is convicted of a criminal offense (other than minor
        traffic violations)
    (d) The Founder becomes incapacitated for more than 90 consecutive days

8.3 FOUNDER VESTING
    Upon the Company raising external funding of at least Rs. 25 lakhs, the
    Founders' shares shall be subject to vesting as follows:
    (a) 25% shall vest immediately
    (b) The remaining 75% shall vest monthly over the next 36 months
    (c) Acceleration upon change of control or IPO

================================================================================

                ARTICLE 9: CONFIDENTIALITY

9.1 CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
    Each Founder agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary
    and confidential information of the Company, including but not limited to:
    (a) Business plans and strategies
    (b) Financial information
    (c) Customer and vendor information
    (d) Technical designs and source code
    (e) Marketing and sales data

9.2 SURVIVAL
    The confidentiality obligations under this Article shall survive the
    termination of this Agreement and any Founder's departure from the
    Company for a period of 5 years.

================================================================================

             ARTICLE 10: DISPUTE RESOLUTION

10.1 GOVERNING LAW
     This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with
     the laws of India.

10.2 ARBITRATION
     Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall
     be finally settled by arbitration in accordance with the Arbitration
     and Conciliation Act, 1996.

10.3 ARBITRATION PROCEDURE
     (a) The arbitration shall be conducted by a sole arbitrator mutually
         appointed by the Founders
     (b) If the Founders cannot agree on an arbitrator within 15 days, the
         arbitrator shall be appointed by the High Court of Karnataka
     (c) The seat of arbitration shall be Bangalore, Karnataka
     (d) The proceedings shall be conducted in English

================================================================================

             ARTICLE 11: GENERAL PROVISIONS

11.1 ENTIRE AGREEMENT
     This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Founders
     with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior
     agreements and understandings.

11.2 AMENDMENTS
     This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by
     both Founders.

11.3 SEVERABILITY
     If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable,
     the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

11.4 WAIVER
     No waiver of any breach of this Agreement shall constitute a waiver of
     any other or subsequent breach.

11.5 NOTICES
     All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered by
     hand, courier, or email to the addresses specified above.
