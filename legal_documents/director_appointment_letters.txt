Mr. Rahul Sharma

Subject: Appointment as Director of TechVenture India Private Limited

Dear Mr. Sharma,

We are pleased to inform you of your appointment as a Director of TechVenture
India Private Limited (the "Company") with effect from March 15, 2019, being
the date of incorporation of the Company.

DETAILS OF APPOINTMENT:

1. DESIGNATION
   Whole-time Director and Chief Executive Officer

2. DIN (Director Identification Number)
   08123456

3. TERM
   Your appointment is for a term of 5 (five) years from the date of
   appointment, subject to retirement by rotation as per the Articles of
   Association.

4. CATEGORY
   Executive Director (Whole-time)

5. REMUNERATION
   As per terms approved by the Board from time to time and disclosed to
   shareholders.

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

As a Director, you are expected to:

(a) Attend Board meetings regularly and participate in decision-making;

(b) Exercise duties with due diligence, care, and skill;

(c) Act in good faith and in the best interests of the Company;

(d) Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose any potential conflicts;

(e) Maintain confidentiality of Company information;

(f) Comply with the Companies Act, 2013 and applicable laws;

(g) Ensure compliance with the Company's Code of Conduct;

(h) Provide strategic direction and oversight to the management.

STATUTORY OBLIGATIONS:

You are required to comply with the following statutory obligations:

(a) Obtain and maintain a valid Director Identification Number (DIN);

(b) File Form DIR-3 KYC annually with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs;

(c) Disclose your interest in any other entity as required;

(d) Attend at least one Board meeting in each financial year;

(e) Comply with Section 184 (Disclosure of Interest) and Section 188
    (Related Party Transactions) of the Companies Act, 2013.

COMMITTEES:

You may be appointed as a member of one or more Board Committees as
determined by the Board from time to time.

INSURANCE:

The Company maintains Directors & Officers (D&O) Liability Insurance
coverage of Rs. 5 Crores for the benefit of Directors.

CONFIDENTIALITY:

You agree to maintain confidentiality of all Company information and not
to disclose any Confidential Information without prior Board approval.

CONFLICT OF INTEREST:

You agree to disclose any actual or potential conflict of interest and
to recuse yourself from decisions where such conflict exists.

Please confirm your acceptance of this appointment by signing and returning
the duplicate copy of this letter.

We look forward to your valuable contribution to the Company's growth.


For TECHVENTURE INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED


_________________________
Priya Mehta
Director


ACCEPTANCE

I, Rahul Sharma, hereby accept the appointment as Director of TechVenture
India Private Limited on the terms stated above.


_________________________
Rahul Sharma
Date: March 15, 2019

To,
Mr. Suresh Kumar
234 Sadashivanagar
Bangalore - 560080

Subject: Appointment as Independent Director of TechVenture India Private
         Limited

Dear Mr. Kumar,

Pursuant to the resolution passed by the Board of Directors at its meeting
held on January 10, 2021, and subject to approval of the shareholders, we
are pleased to inform you of your appointment as an Independent Director
of TechVenture India Private Limited (the "Company").

DETAILS OF APPOINTMENT:

1. DESIGNATION
   Independent Director

2. DIN (Director Identification Number)
   07456789

3. TERM
   Your appointment is for a term of 3 (three) years from January 10, 2021
   to January 9, 2024, not subject to retirement by rotation.

4. CATEGORY
   Independent Director

5. REMUNERATION
   Sitting fee of Rs. 10,000 per Board meeting and Rs. 5,000 per Committee
   meeting attended.

INDEPENDENCE CRITERIA:

You have confirmed that you meet the independence criteria specified in
Section 149(6) of the Companies Act, 2013, including:

(a) No material pecuniary relationship with the Company;
(b) Not related to Promoters or Directors;
(c) Not held key managerial position in the Company in preceding years;
(d) Not a partner or employee of statutory auditor or internal auditor.

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

As an Independent Director, you are expected to:

(a) Bring independent judgment on issues of strategy and performance;

(b) Monitor and provide oversight on Company performance;

(c) Safeguard the interests of minority shareholders;

(d) Balance the conflicting interests of stakeholders;

(e) Chair or participate in Committee meetings as appointed;

(f) Meet separately with management and auditors as required.

SEPARATE MEETING:

You shall attend at least one meeting with other Independent Directors
(without management) in each financial year.

[Other terms same as previous letters]
