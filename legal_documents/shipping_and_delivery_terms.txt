                    SECTION 8: DELIVERY ACCEPTANCE AND INSPECTION
================================================================================

8.1 INSPECTION AT DELIVERY

    When receiving your package:

    (a) Check outer packaging for visible damage;
    (b) Verify package weight seems appropriate;
    (c) Ensure seal tape is intact;
    (d) Count number of packages against shipment details.

8.2 OPEN DELIVERY (OTP-PROTECTED)

    For select high-value items:

    (a) Open package in front of delivery executive;
    (b) Verify product and accessories;
    (c) Check for damage or defects;
    (d) Refuse delivery if issues found;
    (e) Report immediately if damage noticed after executive leaves.

8.3 DAMAGED PACKAGE

    If package appears damaged:

    (a) Photograph/video the damaged package before opening;
    (b) Open carefully and document product condition;
    (c) Report damage within 24 hours;
    (d) Keep all packaging materials;
    (e) Contact customer support for resolution.

8.4 REFUSAL OF DELIVERY

    You may refuse delivery if:

    (a) Package is severely damaged;
    (b) Product does not match order;
    (c) Delivery is significantly late (beyond acceptable window);
    (d) You no longer need the product (return shipping may apply).

    Refused packages return to warehouse for refund processing.

================================================================================
                    SECTION 9: FAILED DELIVERIES
================================================================================

9.1 DELIVERY ATTEMPTS

    (a) Standard: 3 delivery attempts;
    (b) Premium: Up to 5 attempts;
    (c) Attempts made on consecutive business days;
    (d) Contact attempted before each retry.

9.2 REASONS FOR FAILED DELIVERY

    (a) Recipient not available;
    (b) Incorrect or incomplete address;
    (c) Access restrictions (gated community, office);
    (d) Refused by recipient;
    (e) Address not found;
    (f) Natural disasters or emergencies.

9.3 AFTER FAILED ATTEMPTS

    If all delivery attempts fail:

    (a) Package held at local delivery center for 7 days;
    (b) Notification sent for self-pickup option;
    (c) After 7 days, package returned to warehouse;
    (d) Refund processed after return (minus shipping if applicable).

9.4 RESHIPMENT

    To reship a returned package:

    (a) Contact customer support;
    (b) Confirm/update delivery address;
    (c) Pay reshipping charges (Rs. 50-150);
    (d) New estimated delivery provided.

9.5 CUSTOMER RESPONSIBILITY

    To avoid failed deliveries:

    (a) Provide complete and accurate address;
    (b) Include landmarks and contact number;
    (c) Ensure someone is available to receive;
    (d) Update delivery preferences if needed;
    (e) Respond to delivery executive calls.

                    SECTION 14: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q1: How long will delivery take?
A:  Delivery time depends on your location and shipping method.
    Standard delivery is 5-10 business days. Check estimated date
    at checkout.

Q2: Do you deliver on Sundays?
A:  Sunday delivery is available in select metro cities. Most
    deliveries are Monday to Saturday.

Q3: Can I change my delivery address after ordering?
A:  Address can be changed before dispatch. After dispatch, contact
    delivery partner directly for address update requests.

Q4: Why is shipping not free for my order?
A:  Free shipping applies to orders above Rs. 499. Smaller orders
    have a nominal shipping fee.

Q5: What if I'm not home during delivery?
A:  Delivery executive will call before arriving. You can reschedule
    or ask a family member to receive with OTP.

Q6: How do I track my international order?
A:  International tracking available through our website and partner
    websites. Tracking updates may be delayed at customs.

Q7: Can I get same-day delivery?
A:  Same-day delivery is available in select metros for orders placed
    before 12 PM. Check availability at checkout.

Q8: What happens if my package is lost?
A:  Lost packages are investigated with shipping partner. If
    unrecoverable, full refund or replacement provided.

Q9: Why is my order shipping in multiple packages?
A:  Items may be stored in different warehouses and ship separately
    for faster delivery. Each package can be tracked individually.

Q10: Do you ship to P.O. Boxes?
A:   Yes, we ship to P.O. Boxes via India Post where available.
     Express delivery not available for P.O. Boxes.
