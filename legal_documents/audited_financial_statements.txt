NOTE 1: SIGNIFICANT ACCOUNTING POLICIES

(a) Basis of Preparation
    The financial statements are prepared in accordance with Indian Accounting
    Standards (Ind AS) as prescribed under Section 133 of the Companies Act,
    2013.

(b) Revenue Recognition
    - SaaS subscription revenue is recognized ratably over the subscription
      period
    - Implementation revenue is recognized on completion
    - Support revenue is recognized over the service period

(c) Property, Plant and Equipment
    Depreciation is provided on Written Down Value method at rates prescribed
    under the Companies Act, 2013.

(d) Intangible Assets
    Software development costs are capitalized and amortized over 5 years.

(e) Employee Benefits
    - Provident Fund: Defined contribution plan
    - Gratuity: Defined benefit plan, provision made as per actuarial valuation
    - Leave encashment: Provision made based on actuarial valuation
