                    SECTION 1: INTRODUCTION AND SCOPE
================================================================================

1.1 PURPOSE

    This User Generated Content Policy ("Policy") governs all content
    submitted, posted, or shared by users on TechVenture India Private
    Limited's e-commerce platform ("Platform"). This Policy ensures
    our community remains helpful, trustworthy, and respectful.

1.2 APPLICABILITY

    This Policy applies to:

    (a) All registered users and guests;
    (b) Buyers posting reviews and content;
    (c) Sellers posting product content;
    (d) Any person submitting content to the Platform.

1.3 AGREEMENT

    By submitting any content to our Platform, you agree to:

    (a) Comply with this Policy;
    (b) Grant us the licenses described herein;
    (c) Accept responsibility for your content;
    (d) Allow moderation and removal of content.

1.4 CONTENT DEFINITION

    "User Generated Content" or "UGC" includes but is not limited to:

    (a) Product reviews and ratings;
    (b) Questions and answers;
    (c) Photos and videos;
    (d) Comments and replies;
    (e) Profile information;
    (f) Wish lists and collections;
    (g) Any other user-submitted content.

================================================================================
                    SECTION 3: CONTENT GUIDELINES
================================================================================

3.1 GENERAL GUIDELINES

    All content should be:

    (a) AUTHENTIC: Based on genuine experience with the product
    (b) HELPFUL: Provide useful information to other shoppers
    (c) RELEVANT: Related to the product or topic
    (d) RESPECTFUL: Courteous and constructive
    (e) HONEST: Truthful and unbiased
    (f) ORIGINAL: Your own words and media

3.2 CONTENT QUALITY STANDARDS

    Good content includes:

    (a) Specific details about features and performance;
    (b) Context about your use case and needs;
    (c) Comparison with similar products (factual);
    (d) Pros and cons balanced fairly;
    (e) Clear and readable writing;
    (f) High-quality images and videos.

3.3 REVIEW GUIDELINES

    Helpful reviews:

    (a) Focus on the product, not the service;
    (b) Describe your actual experience;
    (c) Mention specific features you liked/disliked;
    (d) Indicate how long you've used the product;
    (e) Update reviews if experience changes;
    (f) Avoid emotional language.

3.4 LANGUAGE REQUIREMENTS

    (a) Content accepted in English and major Indian languages;
    (b) Avoid excessive slang or abbreviations;
    (c) Use appropriate grammar and punctuation;
    (d) No excessive caps or special characters.

================================================================================
                    SECTION 4: PROHIBITED CONTENT
================================================================================

4.1 STRICTLY PROHIBITED

    The following content is NOT allowed:

    (a) OFFENSIVE CONTENT
        - Hate speech or discrimination
        - Threats or harassment
        - Profanity and vulgar language
        - Adult or sexually explicit content
        - Violence or graphic imagery

    (b) ILLEGAL CONTENT
        - Promotes illegal activities
        - Violates intellectual property rights
        - Defamatory statements
        - Privacy violations
        - Regulated product promotion

    (c) SPAM AND MANIPULATION
        - Fake or paid reviews
        - Repetitive or duplicate content
        - Promotional or advertising content
        - Links to external websites
        - SEO keyword stuffing

    (d) MISLEADING CONTENT
        - False or inaccurate information
        - Impersonation of others
        - Fake credentials or expertise
        - Manipulated images
        - Misleading claims

    (e) PRIVACY VIOLATIONS
        - Personal information of others
        - Order numbers or transaction details
        - Phone numbers or email addresses
        - Photos of people without consent

    (f) IRRELEVANT CONTENT
        - Not about the product
        - About shipping or seller service
        - Political or religious content
        - Off-topic discussions

4.2 CONFLICTS OF INTEREST

    You may NOT review products if you:

    (a) Are the seller or manufacturer;
    (b) Are employed by the seller or competitor;
    (c) Received payment or free product for review;
    (d) Have family relationship with seller;
    (e) Are reviewing a competing product to harm it.

4.3 INCENTIVIZED REVIEWS

    (a) Paid reviews are strictly prohibited;
    (b) Reviews in exchange for products must be disclosed;
    (c) Discount in exchange for review is prohibited;
    (d) Incentivized reviews will be removed;
    (e) Accounts may be banned.

                    SECTION 5: REVIEWS AND RATINGS
================================================================================

5.1 WHO CAN REVIEW

    (a) Only verified purchasers can review (preferred);
    (b) Non-purchasers may review with clear indication;
    (c) One review per product per customer;
    (d) Reviews can be updated after initial submission.

5.2 RATING SCALE

    Star ratings explained:

    (a) 5 Stars - Excellent, highly recommend
    (b) 4 Stars - Good, minor issues
    (c) 3 Stars - Average, meets expectations
    (d) 2 Stars - Below expectations, issues
    (e) 1 Star - Poor, significant problems

5.3 REVIEW ELEMENTS

    (a) Overall rating (required);
    (b) Written review (optional but encouraged);
    (c) Title/headline (optional);
    (d) Photos/videos (optional);
    (e) Pros and cons (optional).

5.4 REVIEW AUTHENTICITY

    We verify reviews through:

    (a) Purchase verification;
    (b) Account history analysis;
    (c) Content pattern detection;
    (d) Reviewer credibility scoring;
    (e) Machine learning algorithms.

5.5 FAKE REVIEW DETECTION

    We detect and remove:

    (a) Reviews from same IP/device for different accounts;
    (b) Reviews submitted too quickly;
    (c) Reviews with similar language patterns;
    (d) Reviews from accounts with suspicious activity;
    (e) Reviews solicited through prohibited means.
