                    SECTION 1: OVERVIEW AND SCOPE
================================================================================

1.1 PURPOSE

    This Refund and Return Policy ("Policy") outlines the terms and
    conditions under which products purchased from TechVenture India
    Private Limited ("Company," "we," "our," "us") through our e-commerce
    platform may be returned for refund, exchange, or replacement.

1.2 APPLICABILITY

    This Policy applies to:

    (a) All products sold directly by TechVenture;
    (b) Products sold by third-party sellers on our marketplace
        (subject to seller-specific terms);
    (c) All customers regardless of payment method;
    (d) Both domestic and international orders (with specific conditions).

1.3 COMPLIANCE

    This Policy complies with:

    (a) Consumer Protection Act, 2019;
    (b) Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules, 2020;
    (c) Information Technology Act, 2000;
    (d) Applicable state laws and regulations.

1.4 CUSTOMER RIGHTS

    Your statutory rights as a consumer are not affected by this Policy.
    You may have additional rights under local consumer protection laws.

                    SECTION 2: ELIGIBILITY FOR RETURNS
================================================================================

2.1 GENERAL ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

    Products are eligible for return if:

    (a) Return is initiated within the specified return window;
    (b) Product is unused and in original condition;
    (c) All original tags, labels, and packaging are intact;
    (d) Product is not in the non-returnable category;
    (e) Invoice/proof of purchase is available;
    (f) Return reason is valid as per this Policy.

2.2 VALID RETURN REASONS

    (a) Product different from description or images;
    (b) Defective or damaged product;
    (c) Wrong product delivered;
    (d) Missing parts or accessories;
    (e) Quality not as expected;
    (f) Size or fit issues (for applicable categories);
    (g) Changed mind (within return window for eligible products);
    (h) Duplicate order placed by mistake.

2.3 CONDITIONS THAT MAY AFFECT ELIGIBILITY

    Return eligibility may be rejected if:

    (a) Product shows signs of use or wear;
    (b) Packaging is damaged or missing;
    (c) Tags or labels are removed or tampered;
    (d) Product is altered or modified;
    (e) Hygiene seal is broken (for applicable products);
    (f) Serial number or IMEI is mismatched;
    (g) Free gift or accessory is missing.

                    SECTION 3: NON-RETURNABLE PRODUCTS
================================================================================

3.1 ABSOLUTE NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS

    The following products CANNOT be returned under any circumstances
    (except if defective):

    (a) Perishable goods (food items, flowers, plants);
    (b) Personal hygiene products (undergarments, swimwear, socks);
    (c) Beauty products (opened cosmetics, skincare, perfumes);
    (d) Health and wellness products (supplements, medicines);
    (e) Digital products (e-books, software downloads, subscriptions);
    (f) Gift cards and store credits;
    (g) Customized or personalized products;
    (h) Products with broken hygiene seals;
    (i) Hazardous materials and flammable products;
    (j) Mattresses (if packaging is opened);
    (k) Jewelry above Rs. 10,000 (without original certification).

3.2 CONDITIONALLY NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS

    The following can only be returned if defective or not as described:

    (a) Electronics with registration/activation (once activated);
    (b) Software with broken seal;
    (c) Inkjet cartridges and toners;
    (d) Assembled furniture;
    (e) Vehicle parts once installed;
    (f) Large appliances once installed;
    (g) Products with tampered warranty seals.

3.3 CATEGORY-SPECIFIC RESTRICTIONS

    (a) Books: Only returnable if damaged or wrong edition;
    (b) Mobile Phones: Must include all accessories, box, and documents;
    (c) Watches: Must have warranty card and original packaging;
    (d) Precious Items: Must have certification and secure packaging.

                    SECTION 5: RETURN CONDITIONS AND REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

5.1 PRODUCT CONDITION

    For successful returns, products must be:

    (a) Unused: No signs of use, wear, or damage from customer;
    (b) Complete: All parts, accessories, and freebies included;
    (c) Original Packaging: Product in original box with all inserts;
    (d) Tags Intact: All price tags, brand tags, and labels attached;
    (e) Documentation: Invoice, warranty cards, and manuals included.

5.2 PACKAGING REQUIREMENTS

    (a) Use original packaging wherever possible;
    (b) Secure product properly to prevent transit damage;
    (c) Include all original materials (plastic covers, foam, etc.);
    (d) Place return authorization clearly on outer box;
    (e) Do not write on or damage original product packaging.

5.3 ELECTRONICS-SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS

    (a) Factory reset device and remove personal data;
    (b) Sign out of all accounts (iCloud, Google, etc.);
    (c) Remove screen protectors if applied;
    (d) Include all cables, chargers, and earphones;
    (e) Match serial/IMEI numbers with invoice.

5.4 FASHION-SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS

    (a) Try products without removing tags;
    (b) Do not wash, alter, or wear products;
    (c) Keep shoeboxes and dust bags intact;
    (d) Include all buttons, belts, and accessories;
    (e) Keep fragrance bottles sealed.

                    SECTION 8: QUALITY CHECK PROCESS
================================================================================

8.1 QC PROCESS OVERVIEW

    All returns undergo quality check (QC) at our warehouse:

    STEP 1: Package receipt and RAN verification (Day 1)

    STEP 2: Product inspection against return reason (Day 1-2)

    STEP 3: Verification of completeness and condition (Day 2)

    STEP 4: QC decision - Approved or Rejected (Day 2-3)

    STEP 5: Refund initiation if approved (Day 3)

8.2 QC CRITERIA

    (a) Product matches what was sold (serial/IMEI check);
    (b) Product is in returnable condition;
    (c) All accessories and freebies included;
    (d) Packaging is adequate;
    (e) Return reason is accurate.

8.3 QC APPROVAL

    If QC is PASSED:

    (a) Refund is initiated immediately;
    (b) Email confirmation sent;
    (c) Estimated refund date provided;
    (d) No further action required from customer.

8.4 QC REJECTION

    If QC FAILS, return may be rejected due to:

    (a) Product used, damaged, or altered by customer;
    (b) Missing parts, accessories, or packaging;
    (c) Wrong product returned;
    (d) Return reason does not match product condition;
    (e) Product tampered or warranty seal broken.

    In case of rejection:

    (a) Customer notified within 24 hours;
    (b) Photos/evidence of QC failure provided;
    (c) Product shipped back at customer's cost;
    (d) Option to dispute decision available;
    (e) No refund processed.

8.5 DISPUTED QC DECISIONS

    To dispute a QC rejection:

    (a) Contact customer support within 7 days;
    (b) Provide evidence supporting your claim;
    (c) Case escalated to senior review team;
    (d) Decision provided within 5 business days;
    (e) Decision of review team is final.

9.1 REFUND TO ORIGINAL PAYMENT METHOD

    Default refund method is to original payment source:

    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | PAYMENT METHOD           | REFUND METHOD                              |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | Credit Card              | Credit to same card                        |
    | Debit Card               | Credit to same card                        |
    | Net Banking              | Credit to source bank account              |
    | UPI                      | Credit to linked bank account              |
    | Digital Wallet           | Credit to same wallet                      |
    | EMI                      | Credit to card (EMI cancellation)          |
    | BNPL                     | Loan cancellation/adjustment               |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------

9.2 ALTERNATIVE REFUND METHODS

    In certain cases, alternative refund methods may apply:

    (a) BANK TRANSFER (NEFT/IMPS)
        - When original payment method unavailable
        - Requires bank account details verification
        - Processing time: 5-7 business days

    (b) STORE CREDIT
        - Instant credit to TechVenture account
        - Valid for 1 year from issue date
        - Can be used for future purchases
        - Non-transferable and non-refundable

    (c) CHEQUE
        - For amounts above Rs. 50,000
        - When electronic transfer not possible
        - Mailed to registered address
        - Processing time: 10-15 business days

9.3 CASH ON DELIVERY (COD) REFUNDS

    For COD orders, refunds are processed as:

    (a) PREFERRED: Bank account transfer (NEFT/IMPS)
        - Provide bank details via secure form
        - Verify account ownership
        - Processing time: 5-7 business days

    (b) ALTERNATIVE: Store credit
        - If bank details not provided within 7 days
        - Instant credit to account
        - 1-year validity

9.4 PARTIAL REFUNDS

    Partial refunds may be issued when:

    (a) Only some items from order are returned;
    (b) Product has minor damage affecting value;
    (c) Accessories or freebies are missing;
    (d) Return shipping is customer-paid.

    Deductions will be clearly communicated before finalizing return.

                    SECTION 17: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q1: How long do I have to return a product?
A:  Return windows vary by category from 7-30 days. Check your order
    details for specific return window.

Q2: Can I return a product without the original box?
A:  Original packaging is preferred. Returns without original packaging
    may be accepted with reduced refund value.

Q3: Who pays for return shipping?
A:  Return shipping is free for defective items or our errors. For
    other returns, shipping may be deducted from refund.

Q4: How will I receive my refund?
A:  Refunds are processed to your original payment method. COD orders
    are refunded via bank transfer or store credit.

Q5: Can I return a used product?
A:  No. Products must be unused and in original condition for return.
    Only defective items can be returned after use.

Q6: What if I miss the pickup?
A:  We attempt pickup up to 3 times. After that, you can self-ship or
    drop off at partner location.

Q7: Can I cancel a return request?
A:  Yes, if pickup has not been scheduled. Contact customer support
    to cancel.

Q8: Why was my return rejected?
A:  Returns may be rejected if product is used, damaged, missing parts,
    or does not match return reason. See Section 8.4.

Q9: How do I track my refund?
A:  Track in "My Returns" section or contact customer support with
    your return ID.

Q10: Can I exchange for a different product?
A:   Exchanges are only for same product variants (size, color). For
     different products, return and place new order.
