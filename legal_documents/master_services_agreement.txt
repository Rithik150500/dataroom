                    ARTICLE 2: SERVICES

2.1 SCOPE OF SERVICES
    The Service Provider agrees to provide the Services as described in
    the Statement of Work attached as Schedule A.

2.2 ACCESS TO PLATFORM
    Upon execution of this Agreement and payment of applicable Fees, the
    Service Provider shall provide the Client with access to the Platform
    for the number of Authorized Users specified in the SOW.

2.3 IMPLEMENTATION
    The Service Provider shall implement the Services in accordance with
    the implementation plan set out in the SOW, including:
    (a) Configuration and setup of the Platform
    (b) Data migration (if applicable)
    (c) User training
    (d) Go-live support

2.4 SUPPORT AND MAINTENANCE
    The Service Provider shall provide support and maintenance services
    as set out in Schedule B (Service Level Agreement).

2.5 UPDATES AND ENHANCEMENTS
    The Service Provider may, at its discretion, provide updates,
    enhancements, and new features to the Platform. Major updates that
    materially change functionality shall be communicated in advance.

    (c) Provide decisions and approvals within agreed timelines
    (d) Ensure Authorized Users comply with this Agreement

3.2 USE RESTRICTIONS
    The Client shall not, and shall ensure that Authorized Users do not:
    (a) Use the Services in violation of any applicable law
    (b) Transmit any harmful, illegal, or offensive content
    (c) Attempt to gain unauthorized access to the Platform
    (d) Reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble the Platform
    (e) Copy, modify, or create derivative works
    (f) Resell, sublicense, or transfer access to the Services
    (g) Exceed the number of licensed Authorized Users
    (h) Use the Services to compete with the Service Provider

3.3 SECURITY
    The Client shall:
    (a) Maintain the confidentiality of access credentials
    (b) Notify the Service Provider of any security breach
    (c) Implement appropriate security controls on Client systems

3.4 DATA ACCURACY
    The Client is responsible for the accuracy and legality of Data
    uploaded to the Platform.

              ARTICLE 6: CONFIDENTIALITY

6.1 CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS
    Each Party agrees to:
    (a) Maintain the confidentiality of the other Party's Confidential
        Information
    (b) Use Confidential Information only for the purposes of this
        Agreement
    (c) Protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree
        of care used for its own confidential information
    (d) Not disclose Confidential Information to third parties without
        prior written consent

6.2 EXCEPTIONS
    Confidential Information does not include information that is:
    (a) Publicly available
    (b) Already known to the receiving Party
    (c) Independently developed
    (d) Received from a third party without restriction

6.3 REQUIRED DISCLOSURE
    A Party may disclose Confidential Information if required by law,
    provided it gives prompt notice to enable the other Party to seek
    a protective order.

               ARTICLE 7: DATA PROTECTION

7.1 DATA PROCESSING
    The Service Provider shall process Personal Data only:
    (a) As necessary to provide the Services
    (b) In accordance with the Client's instructions
    (c) In compliance with applicable data protection laws

7.2 SECURITY MEASURES
    The Service Provider shall implement appropriate technical and
    organizational measures to protect Personal Data, including:
    (a) Encryption of data in transit and at rest
    (b) Access controls and authentication
    (c) Regular security assessments
    (d) Incident response procedures

7.3 DATA LOCATION
    Data shall be stored and processed in India unless otherwise agreed.

7.4 SUB-PROCESSORS
    The Service Provider may engage sub-processors for data processing
    subject to appropriate contractual protections.

7.5 DATA BREACH
    In case of a data breach, the Service Provider shall:
    (a) Notify the Client within 72 hours
    (b) Take immediate remedial action
    (c) Cooperate with the Client's investigation

7.6 DATA RETURN AND DELETION
    Upon termination, the Service Provider shall:
    (a) Return or provide export of Client Data within 30 days
    (b) Delete Client Data within 90 days (except as required by law)

            ARTICLE 10: TERM AND TERMINATION

10.1 TERM
     This Agreement shall commence on the date first written above and
     continue for an initial term of [1/2/3] year(s) (the "Initial Term").

10.2 RENEWAL
     The Agreement shall automatically renew for successive periods of
     one year each unless either Party provides written notice of
     non-renewal at least 60 days prior to the end of the then-current term.

10.3 TERMINATION FOR CONVENIENCE
     Either Party may terminate this Agreement with 90 days' prior
     written notice.

10.4 TERMINATION FOR CAUSE
     Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately if:
     (a) The other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to
         cure within 30 days of notice
     (b) The other Party becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, or
         ceases operations

10.5 EFFECT OF TERMINATION
     Upon termination:
     (a) Client's access to the Platform shall cease
     (b) All outstanding Fees become immediately due
     (c) Each Party shall return Confidential Information
     (d) Client Data shall be returned/deleted per Article 7.6
     (e) Provisions that by nature should survive shall survive

11.2 BY CLIENT

     (c) Client's breach of applicable laws

11.3 PROCEDURE
     The indemnified Party shall:
     (a) Promptly notify the indemnifying Party
     (b) Provide reasonable cooperation
     (c) Allow the indemnifying Party to control the defense
