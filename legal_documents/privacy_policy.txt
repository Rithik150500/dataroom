We retain personal data for as long as necessary for the purposes described

                9. YOUR RIGHTS

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

9.1 RIGHT TO ACCESS:
    You can request a copy of your personal data.

9.2 RIGHT TO CORRECTION:
    You can request correction of inaccurate data.

9.3 RIGHT TO ERASURE:
    You can request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).

9.4 RIGHT TO WITHDRAW CONSENT:
    You can withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior processing).

9.5 RIGHT TO GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL:
    You can raise concerns with our Data Protection Officer or the Data
    Protection Board of India.

HOW TO EXERCISE YOUR RIGHTS:

- Platform settings: Account > Privacy

identity before processing requests.
