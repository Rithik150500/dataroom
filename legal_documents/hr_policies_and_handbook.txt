                    2. CODE OF CONDUCT

2.1 PROFESSIONAL BEHAVIOR
All employees shall:
- Treat colleagues with respect and dignity
- Maintain professional relationships
- Communicate openly and honestly
- Avoid conflicts of interest

2.2 CONFIDENTIALITY
Employees must protect company confidential information including:
- Customer data and contracts
- Financial information
- Product roadmaps
- Employee information

2.3 CONFLICTS OF INTEREST
Employees must:
- Disclose potential conflicts to HR
- Avoid personal gain from company relationships
- Not engage in competing businesses

2.4 USE OF COMPANY RESOURCES
Company resources (laptops, email, etc.) are for business purposes.
Limited personal use is permitted.

2.5 SOCIAL MEDIA
Employees must not:
- Disclose confidential information
- Make statements on behalf of company without authorization
- Post content that damages company reputation

                  3. EMPLOYMENT POLICIES

3.1 EQUAL OPPORTUNITY
TechVenture is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based
on race, religion, gender, age, disability, or any other protected category.

3.2 PROBATION
- Duration: 3-6 months depending on role
- Notice period during probation: 15 days
- Confirmation subject to performance

3.3 WORKING HOURS
- Standard hours: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (with flexibility)
- Work week: Monday to Friday
- Core hours (mandatory): 11:00 AM - 4:00 PM

3.4 ATTENDANCE
- Employees must mark attendance daily
- Inform manager of any absence
- Medical certificate required for sick leave > 2 days

3.5 DRESS CODE
Business casual attire. Professional dress for client meetings.

          6. PREVENTION OF SEXUAL HARASSMENT (POSH) POLICY

6.1 POLICY STATEMENT
TechVenture is committed to providing a safe workplace free from sexual
harassment as per the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention,
Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013.

6.2 DEFINITION OF SEXUAL HARASSMENT
Sexual harassment includes:
- Physical contact and advances
- Demand or request for sexual favors
- Sexually colored remarks
- Showing pornography
- Any unwelcome conduct of sexual nature

6.3 INTERNAL COMPLAINTS COMMITTEE (ICC)

Presiding Officer: Ms. Priya Mehta (Director)
Members:
- Ms. Sneha Kapoor (HR Manager)
- Mr. Arun Kumar (Senior Developer)
- External Member: Dr. Lakshmi Rao (Advocate)

6.4 COMPLAINT PROCEDURE
1. Written complaint to ICC within 3 months of incident
2. ICC to complete inquiry within 90 days
3. Employer to act on recommendations within 60 days

6.5 CONFIDENTIALITY
All complaints and proceedings are strictly confidential.

6.6 PROTECTION AGAINST RETALIATION
No employee shall be retaliated against for filing a complaint in good faith.

6.7 PENALTIES
- Warning
- Written apology
- Withholding promotion/increment
- Termination
- Deduction from salary for compensation

6.8 AWARENESS
- Training for all employees
- Policy displayed at workplace
- Annual report filed with District Officer

              8. INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY

8.1 PURPOSE
Protect company and customer data from unauthorized access.

8.2 PASSWORD POLICY
- Minimum 12 characters
- Mix of upper, lower, numbers, special characters
- Change every 90 days
- No reuse of last 5 passwords

8.3 DATA CLASSIFICATION
- Public: General information
- Internal: Employee-only information
- Confidential: Sensitive business data
- Restricted: Customer data, credentials

8.4 DEVICE SECURITY
- Screen lock when away
- Encrypt hard drives
- Install company security software
- Report lost/stolen devices immediately

8.5 EMAIL AND INTERNET
- No confidential data to personal email
- No downloading unauthorized software
- Report suspicious emails to IT

8.6 CLEAN DESK
- Lock sensitive documents
- Clear desk at end of day
- Shred confidential papers

                 9. GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL

9.1 PURPOSE
Provide fair and transparent mechanism for employee concerns.

9.2 SCOPE
Covers workplace issues including policies, treatment, working conditions.

9.3 PROCEDURE

Level 1: Immediate Manager
- Raise concern verbally or in writing
- Manager to respond within 3 working days

Level 2: HR Department
- If unresolved, escalate to HR
- HR to respond within 5 working days

Level 3: Grievance Committee
- Committee: HR Head, Department Head, Employee Representative
- Hearing within 10 days
- Decision within 5 days of hearing

9.4 CONFIDENTIALITY
All grievances handled confidentially.

9.5 NO RETALIATION
No adverse action for raising genuine concerns.

                  10. SEPARATION POLICY

10.1 NOTICE PERIOD
- During probation: 15 days
- After confirmation: 60 days
- Recovery of notice period shortfall

10.2 RESIGNATION PROCESS
1. Submit written resignation to manager
2. Handover duties and knowledge transfer
3. Return company assets
4. Exit interview with HR
5. Final settlement within 30 days

10.3 TERMINATION
Company may terminate for:
- Performance issues (after PIP)
- Misconduct
- Violation of policies
- Redundancy

10.4 FULL AND FINAL SETTLEMENT
Includes:
- Salary till last working day
- Leave encashment
- Gratuity (if eligible)
- Reimbursements
- Deductions (notice recovery, loans, etc.)
