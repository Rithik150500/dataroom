================================================================================
                     ARTICLES OF ASSOCIATION
                              OF
                TECHVENTURE INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED
================================================================================

(A Company Limited by Shares under the Companies Act, 2013)

================================================================================

                        PRELIMINARY

1.  In these Articles, unless there is something repugnant in the subject or
    context:

    (a) "Act" means the Companies Act, 2013, or any statutory modification or
        re-enactment thereof for the time being in force;

    (b) "Articles" means these Articles of Association as originally framed
        or as altered from time to time;

    (c) "Board" or "Board of Directors" means the Board of Directors of the
        Company;

    (d) "Company" means TechVenture India Private Limited;

    (e) "Director" means a Director for the time being of the Company;

    (f) "Dividend" includes bonus;

    (g) "Member" means a member of the Company;

    (h) "Memorandum" means the Memorandum of Association of the Company;

    (i) "Registrar" means the Registrar of Companies, Karnataka at Bangalore;

    (j) "Seal" means the Common Seal of the Company;

    (k) "Secretary" means a Company Secretary of the Company;

    (l) Words importing the singular number include the plural number and
        vice-versa; and words importing the masculine gender include the
        feminine gender and words importing persons shall include body
        corporate.

================================================================================

                         SHARE CAPITAL

2.  The Authorized Share Capital of the Company is Rs. 50,00,000/- (Rupees
    Fifty Lakhs Only) divided into 50,000 (Fifty Thousand) Equity Shares of
    Rs. 100/- (Rupees One Hundred Only) each, with power to increase, reduce,
    subdivide, or consolidate the share capital and to divide the shares in
    the capital for the time being into different classes and to attach
    thereto respectively such preferential, deferred, qualified or other
    special rights, privileges or conditions as may be determined by or in
    accordance with the Articles of the Company and to vary, modify or
    abrogate any such rights, privileges or conditions in such manner as may
    be permitted by the Act.

3.  The shares shall be under the control of the Board of Directors who may
    allot or otherwise dispose of the same to such persons, on such terms
    and conditions and at such times as the Directors think fit.

================================================================================

                        PRIVATE COMPANY

4.  The Company is a private company within the meaning of Section 2(68) of
    the Companies Act, 2013 and accordingly:

    (a) The right to transfer shares is restricted in the manner hereinafter
        provided;

    (b) The number of members of the Company (exclusive of persons who are
        in the employment of the Company and persons who having been formerly
        in the employment of the Company were members of the Company while in
        that employment and have continued to be members after the employment
        ceased) shall be limited to 200;

    (c) Any invitation or acceptance of deposits from persons other than its
        members, directors, or their relatives is prohibited.

================================================================================

                      TRANSFER OF SHARES

5.  No share shall be transferred to any person who is not a member of the
    Company so long as any member or any person selected by the Board as one
    whom it shall be desirable in the interest of the Company to admit to
    membership is willing to purchase the same at fair value.

6.  The instrument of transfer of any share in the Company shall be executed
    both by the transferor and transferee, and the transferor shall be deemed
    to remain a holder of the share until the name of the transferee is
    entered in the Register of Members in respect thereof.

7.  The Board may, at its sole discretion and without assigning any reason,
    decline to register any transfer of any share to a person of whom they
    do not approve.

8.  If at any time the Board declines to register a transfer, the Company
    shall within thirty days from the date on which the instrument of transfer
    was lodged with the Company, send notice of refusal to the transferee and
    the transferor, giving reasons for such refusal.

================================================================================

                      PRE-EMPTION RIGHTS

9.  A member who intends to transfer any share (the "Transferring Member")
    shall give notice in writing (the "Transfer Notice") to the Company
    stating the number of shares proposed to be transferred, the price at
    which transfer is proposed, and the particulars of the proposed
    transferee.

10. Upon receipt of Transfer Notice, the Company shall offer such shares to
    the other members in proportion to their existing shareholding at the
    price stated in the Transfer Notice.

11. If the other members do not purchase all the shares within 30 days, the
    Transferring Member shall be at liberty to transfer the unpurchased
    shares to the proposed transferee.

================================================================================

                     GENERAL MEETINGS

12. The Company shall in each year hold a General Meeting as its Annual
    General Meeting in addition to any other meetings in that year. All
    General Meetings other than Annual General Meetings shall be called
    Extra-ordinary General Meetings.

13. The Board may, whenever it thinks fit, call an Extra-ordinary General
    Meeting. If at any time there are not sufficient Directors capable of
    acting to form a quorum, any Director or any two members of the Company
    may call an Extra-ordinary General Meeting.

14. Notice of every General Meeting shall be given in accordance with Section
    101 of the Act.

================================================================================

                     QUORUM FOR MEETINGS

15. The quorum for a General Meeting shall be two members personally present.

================================================================================

                         VOTING

16. Subject to any rights or restrictions attached to any class of shares:

    (a) On a show of hands, every member present in person shall have one vote;

    (b) On a poll, the voting rights of members shall be in proportion to
        their share of the paid-up equity share capital.

17. In case of an equality of votes, the Chairman of the meeting shall have
    a second or casting vote.

================================================================================

                    BOARD OF DIRECTORS

18. The number of Directors shall not be less than two and not more than
    fifteen.

19. The first Directors of the Company shall be:
    (a) Rahul Sharma
    (b) Priya Mehta

20. The Board shall be entitled to appoint additional Directors provided the
    total number of Directors shall not exceed fifteen.

21. A Director need not hold any qualification shares.

================================================================================

                  POWERS OF THE BOARD

22. Subject to the provisions of the Act and these Articles, the business of
    the Company shall be managed by the Board who may exercise all such powers
    of the Company and do all such acts and things as are not required to be
    exercised or done by the Company in General Meeting.

23. Without prejudice to the general powers conferred by the preceding
    Article, and the other powers conferred by these Articles, the Board
    shall have the following powers:

    (a) To pay costs, charges and expenses preliminary and incidental to the
        promotion, formation, establishment and registration of the Company;

    (b) To purchase or otherwise acquire any property, rights or privileges
        which the Company is authorized to acquire;

    (c) To pay for any property or rights acquired by the Company either in
        cash or in shares;

    (d) To secure the fulfilment of any contracts or engagements entered
        into by the Company;

    (e) To appoint and remove managers, secretaries, officers, clerks,
        agents and servants;

    (f) To provide for the management of the affairs of the Company abroad;

    (g) To enter into all such negotiations, contracts and agreements;

    (h) To make and give receipts, releases and other discharges;

    (i) To delegate any of their powers to committees;

    (j) From time to time to make, vary and repeal bye-laws for the
        regulation of the business of the Company.

================================================================================

                   BOARD MEETINGS

24. The Board shall meet at least once in every quarter and at least four
    meetings shall be held in each year.

25. Notice of every Board Meeting shall be given in writing to every Director
    for the time being in India.

26. The quorum for a Board Meeting shall be one-third of the total strength
    or two Directors, whichever is higher.

27. Questions arising at any meeting shall be decided by a majority of votes.
    In case of an equality of votes, the Chairman shall have a second or
    casting vote.

================================================================================

                        CHAIRMAN

28. The Board may elect a Chairman of the Board and determine the period for
    which he is to hold office. If no such Chairman is elected, or if at any
    meeting the Chairman is not present within fifteen minutes after the time
    appointed for holding the meeting, the Directors present may choose one
    of their number to be Chairman of the meeting.

================================================================================

                         DIVIDENDS

29. The Company in General Meeting may declare dividends, but no dividend
    shall exceed the amount recommended by the Board.

30. The Board may from time to time pay to the members such interim dividends
    as appear to the Board to be justified by the profits of the Company.

31. No dividend shall be payable except out of profits of the Company.

================================================================================

                      ACCOUNTS & AUDIT

32. The Company shall keep proper books of account with respect to all sums
    of money received and expended by the Company and the matters in respect
    of which the receipt and expenditure takes place.

33. The books of account shall be kept at the registered office of the
    Company or at such other place in India as the Board may decide.

34. Accounts shall be prepared in accordance with the provisions of Section
    129 of the Act.

================================================================================

                        AUDITORS

35. Auditors shall be appointed and their duties regulated in accordance with
    Sections 139 to 148 of the Act.

================================================================================

                     WINDING UP

36. Subject to the provisions of the Act, if the Company is wound up, the
    assets available for distribution among the members shall be applied first
    in repayment to the members of the amounts paid up on their shares
    respectively and then the balance (if any) shall be distributed amongst
    the members in proportion to the number of shares held by them.

================================================================================

                     INDEMNITY

37. Subject to the provisions of Section 197 of the Act, every Director,
    Manager, Secretary and other Officer of the Company shall be indemnified
    by the Company against all costs, losses and expenses which any such
    Director, Manager, Secretary or Officer may incur or become liable to
    by reason of any contract entered into or act or deed done by him as
    such Director, Manager, Secretary or Officer in any way in the discharge
    of his duties.
