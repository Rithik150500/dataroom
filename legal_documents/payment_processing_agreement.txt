                    ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION
================================================================================

1.1 DEFINITIONS

    In this Payment Processing Agreement, unless the context otherwise
    requires:

    (a) "Authorization" means approval from the Card Issuer or Payment
        Method provider to proceed with a transaction;

    (b) "Card Issuer" means the bank or financial institution that
        issued the payment card to the Customer;

    (c) "Chargeback" means a reversal of a payment initiated by the
        Card Issuer due to a dispute;

    (d) "Payment Gateway" means the third-party service provider that
        processes payment transactions on behalf of the Company;

    (e) "Payment Method" means any method accepted by the Company for
        payment including cards, net banking, UPI, wallets, and COD;

    (f) "PCI-DSS" means Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard;

    (g) "RBI" means Reserve Bank of India;

    (h) "Transaction" means any financial operation including payments,
        refunds, and adjustments;

    (i) "UPI" means Unified Payments Interface as regulated by NPCI.

                    ARTICLE 3: PAYMENT PROCESSING
================================================================================

3.1 PAYMENT FLOW

    (a) CHECKOUT: Select payment method and enter details
    (b) VALIDATION: Payment details verified for format and completeness
    (c) AUTHORIZATION: Request sent to payment gateway/card issuer
    (d) AUTHENTICATION: 3D Secure/OTP verification completed
    (e) CONFIRMATION: Payment authorized and order confirmed
    (f) SETTLEMENT: Funds transferred to merchant (T+2 days typical)

3.2 AUTHORIZATION HOLD

    (a) Authorization hold placed on card for order value;
    (b) Hold released after settlement or cancellation;
    (c) Hold may appear as pending charge on statement;
    (d) Release time depends on card issuer (1-7 days).

3.3 PAYMENT CONFIRMATION

    Upon successful payment:

    (a) Order confirmation displayed on screen;
    (b) Confirmation email sent to registered email;
    (c) SMS notification with order details;
    (d) Payment reference number provided;
    (e) Invoice generated and available in account.

3.4 PAYMENT RECEIPT

    (a) Digital receipt available in "My Orders";
    (b) Includes payment method, amount, and reference;
    (c) GST invoice provided separately;
    (d) Can be downloaded or printed.

3.5 PAYMENT GATEWAY PARTNERS

    We use PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways:

    (a) Razorpay
    (b) PayU
    (c) CCAvenue
    (d) Paytm Payment Gateway
    (e) BillDesk

    Gateway assigned based on transaction type and availability.

                    ARTICLE 4: PAYMENT SECURITY
================================================================================

4.1 DATA SECURITY STANDARDS

    We maintain strict security standards:

    (a) PCI-DSS Level 1 Compliance;
    (b) 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption;
    (c) Tokenization of card data;
    (d) No storage of CVV/PIN;
    (e) Regular security audits.

4.2 3D SECURE AUTHENTICATION

    All card transactions require 3D Secure:

    (a) Verified by Visa (VbV);
    (b) MasterCard SecureCode;
    (c) American Express SafeKey;
    (d) RuPay PaySecure.

    This adds an additional authentication layer with OTP or password.

4.3 TOKENIZATION

    As per RBI guidelines:

    (a) Card details tokenized by card networks;
    (b) Only token stored for future transactions;
    (c) Full card number not stored by merchant;
    (d) Secure token valid only for our platform.

4.4 FRAUD PREVENTION

    We employ multiple fraud detection measures:

    (a) AI-based fraud scoring;
    (b) Device fingerprinting;
    (c) IP geolocation analysis;
    (d) Velocity checks;
    (e) Address Verification Service (AVS);
    (f) Manual review for high-risk transactions.

4.5 CUSTOMER SECURITY RESPONSIBILITY

    To protect yourself:

    (a) Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords;
    (b) Use secure and private network;
    (c) Verify website URL before payment;
    (d) Log out after completing transaction;
    (e) Monitor bank statements regularly;
    (f) Report suspicious activity immediately.

4.6 SECURITY INCIDENT REPORTING

    Report security concerns to:

    Email:      security@techventure.in

7.1 COMMON PAYMENT FAILURE REASONS

    (b) Incorrect card details;
    (c) Card not enabled for online transactions;
    (d) 3D Secure authentication failed;
    (e) Bank server unavailable;
    (f) Transaction timeout;
    (g) Card expired or blocked;
    (h) Daily/monthly limit exceeded;
    (i) Suspected fraud by bank.

    If payment fails:

    (a) Error message displayed;
    (b) Order not placed;
    (c) No charges applied;
    (d) Retry with same or different payment method.

    If amount debited but order not confirmed:

    (a) Do not retry payment immediately;
    (b) Check email for order confirmation;
    (c) Check "My Orders" section;
    (d) Wait for 30 minutes for system reconciliation;
    (e) If not confirmed, amount auto-reversed within 5-7 days.

7.4 DOUBLE CHARGES

    If charged twice for same order:

    (a) Contact customer support immediately;
    (b) Provide order number and transaction IDs;
    (c) We will verify and process refund;
    (d) Duplicate charge refunded within 5-7 days.
