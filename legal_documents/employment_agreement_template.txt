        ARTICLE 6: CONFIDENTIALITY AND INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

6.1 CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
    The Employee acknowledges and agrees that during employment, the Employee
    will have access to Confidential Information of the Company, including
    but not limited to:

    (a) Business plans, strategies, and projections
    (b) Financial information and data
    (c) Customer and vendor information
    (d) Technical designs, source code, and algorithms
    (e) Product roadmaps and development plans
    (f) Marketing and sales strategies
    (g) Employee and compensation information
    (h) Any other proprietary information

6.2 CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS
    The Employee agrees to:
    (a) Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential
    (b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party
    (c) Use Confidential Information only for the purposes of employment
    (d) Return all Confidential Information upon termination

6.3 INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
    All intellectual property created by the Employee during employment
    that relates to the Company's business shall be the exclusive property
    of the Company. This includes:
    (a) Inventions, discoveries, and improvements
    (b) Software, code, and technical designs
    (c) Documentation, manuals, and training materials
    (d) Marketing materials and content

6.4 ASSIGNMENT OF IP
    The Employee hereby assigns to the Company all rights, title, and
    interest in any intellectual property created during employment.
    The Employee agrees to execute any additional documents required
    to perfect this assignment.

6.5 MORAL RIGHTS
    To the extent permitted by law, the Employee waives all moral rights
    in any work created during employment.

6.6 SURVIVAL
    The obligations under this Article shall survive the termination of
    this Agreement.

      ARTICLE 7: NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

7.1 NON-COMPETE DURING EMPLOYMENT
    During employment, the Employee shall not:
    (a) Engage in any competing business
    (b) Provide services to any competitor
    (c) Hold any position in any competing entity

7.2 NON-COMPETE AFTER EMPLOYMENT
    For a period of [6/12] months following termination of employment,
    the Employee shall not engage in any business that directly competes
    with the Company's business in the same geographic market.

    [Note: Enforceability of post-employment non-compete varies under
    Indian law and may be subject to limitations.]

7.3 NON-SOLICITATION
    For a period of 12 months following termination, the Employee shall not:
    (a) Solicit or attempt to solicit any employee of the Company
    (b) Solicit or attempt to solicit any customer of the Company
    (c) Interfere with the Company's relationships with vendors or partners

================================================================================

                  ARTICLE 8: TERMINATION

8.1 TERMINATION BY COMPANY WITH NOTICE
    The Company may terminate this Agreement by providing:
    (a) During probation: 15 days' written notice or salary in lieu
    (b) After confirmation: 30 days' written notice or salary in lieu

8.2 TERMINATION BY EMPLOYEE WITH NOTICE
    The Employee may terminate this Agreement by providing:
    (a) During probation: 15 days' written notice or salary in lieu
    (b) After confirmation: 60 days' written notice or salary in lieu

8.3 TERMINATION FOR CAUSE
    The Company may terminate employment immediately without notice for
    Cause, including:
    (a) Material breach of this Agreement
    (b) Gross misconduct or negligence
    (c) Fraud, dishonesty, or theft
    (d) Harassment or discrimination
    (e) Breach of confidentiality
    (f) Criminal conviction (other than minor offenses)
    (g) Intoxication affecting work performance
    (h) Willful insubordination

8.4 CONSEQUENCES OF TERMINATION
    Upon termination:
    (a) The Employee shall return all Company property, documents, and data
    (b) The Employee shall complete all handover formalities
    (c) Final settlement shall be made within 30 days of last working day
    (d) All unused earned leave shall be encashed as per policy
    (e) The Employee shall sign a release and no-dues certificate

9.1 ENTIRE AGREEMENT
    This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties
    and supersedes all prior agreements, whether written or oral.

9.2 AMENDMENTS
    This Agreement may be amended only by written agreement signed by both

    the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

9.4 WAIVER
    No waiver of any breach shall constitute a waiver of any subsequent
    breach or of any other provision.

9.5 NOTICES
    All notices shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses
    mentioned in this Agreement or such other addresses as notified.

9.6 GOVERNING LAW
    This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India. Any disputes

9.7 BACKGROUND VERIFICATION
    This offer is subject to satisfactory completion of background
    verification as per Company policy.

9.8 COMPANY POLICIES
    The Employee agrees to comply with all Company policies, including but
    not limited to:
    (a) Employee Handbook
    (b) Code of Conduct
    (c) Anti-Harassment and POSH Policy
    (d) Information Security Policy
    (e) Leave Policy
