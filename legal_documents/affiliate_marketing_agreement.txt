                    ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

1.1 In this Agreement, unless the context otherwise requires:

    (a) "Affiliate Link" means the unique tracking URL provided to
        Affiliate for tracking referrals to the Company's website;

    (b) "Affiliate Network" means any third-party platform managing
        the affiliate program on behalf of the Company;

    (c) "Click" means a user clicking on an Affiliate Link;

    (d) "Commission" means the payment earned by Affiliate for
        qualifying transactions;

    (e) "Conversion" means a completed qualifying action (purchase)
        resulting from an Affiliate referral;

    (f) "Cookie Duration" means the period during which an Affiliate
        receives credit for a referral after initial click;

    (g) "Creative Materials" means banners, text links, images, and
        other promotional materials provided by the Company;

    (h) "Effective Date" means the date this Agreement comes into force;

    (i) "Net Sale" means gross sale value less returns, refunds,
        cancellations, taxes, and shipping charges;

    (j) "Qualifying Transaction" means a valid purchase that meets
        all requirements for commission payment;

    (k) "Referral" means a visitor directed to Company's website
        through Affiliate's Link.

                    ARTICLE 4: COMMISSION STRUCTURE
================================================================================

4.1 BASE COMMISSION RATES

    Standard commission rates by category:

    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | PRODUCT CATEGORY            | COMMISSION RATE                         |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | Electronics & Gadgets       | 2% - 5%                                 |
    | Mobile Phones               | 1% - 3%                                 |
    | Fashion & Apparel           | 8% - 12%                                |
    | Home & Kitchen              | 5% - 8%                                 |
    | Beauty & Personal Care      | 6% - 10%                                |
    | Books & Media               | 4% - 6%                                 |
    | Sports & Fitness            | 6% - 10%                                |
    | Baby Products               | 5% - 8%                                 |
    | Furniture                   | 4% - 8%                                 |
    | Grocery                     | 1% - 4%                                 |
    | Large Appliances            | 2% - 4%                                 |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Current rates available in Affiliate Dashboard.

4.2 COMMISSION CALCULATION

    Commission = Net Sale Value x Commission Rate

    Net Sale = Product Price - Returns - Refunds - Cancellations
               - Taxes - Shipping (if applicable)

4.3 COMMISSION CAP

    (a) Per product commission cap may apply;
    (b) Example: Maximum Rs. 500 per item;
    (c) Caps vary by category;
    (d) See rate card for details.

4.4 SPECIAL COMMISSION RATES

    (a) PROMOTIONAL RATES: Temporary increased rates for campaigns
    (b) PERFORMANCE BONUS: Additional commission for top affiliates
    (c) NEW CUSTOMER BONUS: Higher rate for first-time buyers
    (d) VOLUME BONUS: Increased rate for high sales volume

4.5 COMMISSION ADJUSTMENT

    Commissions may be:

    (a) Pending: Order placed, awaiting delivery
    (b) Approved: Order delivered, return window passed
    (c) Rejected: Order cancelled, returned, or invalid
    (d) Adjusted: Partial return or price adjustment

4.6 NON-COMMISSIONABLE ITEMS

    No commission earned on:

    (a) Gift cards and store credit;
    (b) Shipping and handling charges;
    (c) Taxes;
    (d) Warranty and service plans;
    (e) Items purchased by Affiliate;
    (f) Fraudulent or invalid transactions.

                    ARTICLE 5: TRACKING AND ATTRIBUTION
================================================================================

5.1 TRACKING METHOD

    (a) Cookie-based tracking primary method;
    (b) Affiliate Links contain unique tracking code;
    (c) Cookie placed on user's browser upon click;
    (d) Order attributed to Affiliate if cookie present.

5.2 COOKIE DURATION

    (a) Standard cookie duration: 24-30 days;
    (b) Cookie overwrites previous affiliate cookie;
    (c) Last-click attribution model used;
    (d) Duration may vary by promotion.

5.3 ATTRIBUTION RULES

    (a) LAST-CLICK: Last affiliate before purchase gets credit
    (b) DIRECT TRAFFIC: No affiliate credit if user types URL
    (c) COOKIE OVERRIDE: Later affiliate overwrites earlier

5.4 CROSS-DEVICE TRACKING

    (a) Limited cross-device tracking supported;
    (b) Best effort to track across devices;
    (c) User must be logged in for cross-device;
    (d) Not guaranteed in all cases.

5.5 REPORTING AND ANALYTICS

    Affiliate Dashboard provides:

    (a) Real-time clicks and conversions;
    (b) Sales reports by product and date;
    (c) Commission earned and pending;
    (d) Conversion rates and performance;
    (e) Downloadable reports.

5.6 TRACKING DISPUTES

    (a) Report disputes within 30 days of transaction;
    (b) Provide evidence (screenshots, etc.);
    (c) Company investigates within 14 days;
    (d) Company decision is final.

                    ARTICLE 8: PROHIBITED PRACTICES
================================================================================

8.1 STRICTLY PROHIBITED ACTIONS

    Affiliates must NOT:

    (a) PAID SEARCH BIDDING
        - Bid on Company's trademark terms
        - Use brand name in ad copy
        - Direct link in PPC ads

    (b) COOKIE STUFFING
        - Force cookies without user knowledge
        - Use pop-ups, pop-unders, or frames
        - Automatic redirect scripts

    (c) TRADEMARK MISUSE
        - Register domains with Company trademarks
        - Use trademarks in social media handles
        - Mislead users about official status

    (d) FALSE ADVERTISING
        - Make false or unsubstantiated claims
        - Create fake urgency or scarcity
        - Mislead about products or prices
        - Use bait-and-switch tactics

    (e) SPAM
        - Send unsolicited emails
        - Post spam comments or messages
        - Use automated posting tools
        - Engage in any spam activity

    (f) INCENTIVIZED TRAFFIC
        - Pay users to click links
        - Offer rewards for purchases
        - Use loyalty cashback sites without approval

    (g) TRADEMARK INFRINGEMENT
        - Create look-alike websites
        - Impersonate Company
        - Use confusing similar names

    (h) ADWARE/MALWARE
        - Distribute software with affiliate links
        - Use browser toolbars
        - Any malicious tracking

    (i) SELF-REFERRAL
        - Purchase through own affiliate links
        - Refer immediate family members
        - Use multiple accounts

    (j) CONTENT VIOLATIONS
        - Host on illegal or adult sites
        - Promote on sites with prohibited content
        - Use hateful or offensive content

8.2 CONSEQUENCES OF VIOLATIONS

    (a) Warning and request to remedy;
    (b) Commission forfeiture;
    (c) Account suspension;
    (d) Permanent termination;
    (e) Withholding of payments;
    (f) Legal action.
