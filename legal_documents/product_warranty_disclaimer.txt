                    SECTION 5: WARRANTY CLAIM PROCESS
================================================================================

5.1 HOW TO CLAIM WARRANTY

    STEP 1: VERIFY WARRANTY
    - Check warranty period and coverage
    - Confirm issue is covered under warranty
    - Gather purchase documents

    STEP 2: CONTACT APPROPRIATE PARTY
    - Manufacturer (for manufacturer warranty)
    - TechVenture (for TechVenture-sold products)
    - Seller (for third-party seller products)

    STEP 3: SUBMIT CLAIM
    - Provide proof of purchase (invoice)
    - Describe the issue in detail
    - Share photos/videos of defect
    - Provide serial/IMEI number

    STEP 4: ASSESSMENT
    - Product may be collected for inspection
    - Manufacturer/seller assesses claim
    - Decision communicated (repair/replace/reject)

    STEP 5: RESOLUTION
    - Repair at service center
    - Replacement if unrepairable
    - Refund if replacement unavailable

5.2 DOCUMENTS REQUIRED

    (a) Original invoice from TechVenture;
    (b) Warranty card (if applicable);
    (c) Product with original packaging (preferred);
    (d) All accessories and components;
    (e) Description of defect/issue;
    (f) Photos/videos showing problem.

5.3 SERVICE CENTER PROCESS

    For manufacturer warranty claims:

    (a) Locate authorized service center;
    (b) Schedule appointment if required;
    (c) Carry product with documents;
    (d) Get job sheet/acknowledgment;
    (e) Track repair status;
    (f) Collect after repair.

5.4 WARRANTY CLAIM THROUGH TECHVENTURE

    For TechVenture-sold products:

    (a) Initiate claim via "My Orders" section;
    (b) Select "Warranty Claim";
    (c) Describe issue and upload photos;

        - Power surges, lightning
