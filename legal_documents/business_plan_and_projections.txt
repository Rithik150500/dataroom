                  1. COMPANY OVERVIEW

1.1 BUSINESS DESCRIPTION

TechVenture provides cloud-based enterprise software solutions that help
mid-market companies automate business processes, improve efficiency, and
make data-driven decisions.

Core Products:
- VentureFlow: Workflow automation platform
- VentureERP: Enterprise resource planning
- VentureBI: Business intelligence & analytics

1.2 PROBLEM STATEMENT

Mid-market companies (Rs. 50 Cr - 500 Cr revenue) face challenges:
- Legacy systems are expensive and inflexible
- Enterprise solutions (SAP, Oracle) are too complex and costly
- Small business tools lack scalability
- Data silos prevent informed decision-making

1.3 SOLUTION

TechVenture offers:
- Modern, cloud-native SaaS platform
- Affordable pricing (1/10th of enterprise alternatives)
- Quick implementation (weeks vs. months)
- Intuitive user interface
- Strong integration capabilities

1.4 TARGET MARKET

Primary: Indian mid-market companies
- Revenue: Rs. 50 Cr - 500 Cr
- Employees: 100-1,000
- Industries: Manufacturing, Services, BFSI, Healthcare
- Geography: Tier 1 and Tier 2 cities

TAM/SAM/SOM:

TAM (India Enterprise Software): Rs. 50,000 Cr
SAM (Mid-market ERP/Workflow): Rs. 5,000 Cr
SOM (Achievable in 5 years): Rs. 500 Cr (10% of SAM)
