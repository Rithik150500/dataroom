                    ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

1.1 In this Agreement, unless the context otherwise requires:

    (a) "Agreement" means this Seller Marketplace Agreement including all
        schedules, annexures, and policies referenced herein;

    (b) "Buyer" or "Customer" means any person who purchases Products
        from the Seller through the Platform;

    (c) "Commission" means the fee charged by Company as percentage of
        each sale made by Seller through the Platform;

    (d) "Effective Date" means the date this Agreement comes into force;

    (e) "Fulfillment" means the process of receiving, processing, packing,
        and shipping orders to Customers;

    (f) "Intellectual Property" means trademarks, copyrights, patents,
        trade secrets, and other proprietary rights;

    (g) "Listing" means a Product displayed for sale on the Platform;

    (h) "Net Sales" means gross sales less returns, refunds, cancellations,
        and applicable taxes;

    (i) "Order" means a Customer's purchase request for Seller's Product;

    (j) "Platform" means the Company's e-commerce website, mobile
        applications, and associated services;

    (k) "Product" means goods or merchandise listed by Seller for sale;

    (l) "Seller Account" means the account created by Seller to access
        Platform services;

    (m) "Settlement" means the transfer of funds from Company to Seller
        for completed sales.

                    ARTICLE 3: PRODUCT LISTING REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

3.1 LISTING STANDARDS

    All product listings must:

    (a) Comply with Platform listing guidelines;
    (b) Contain accurate and complete information;
    (c) Include clear, high-quality images;
    (d) State correct pricing and availability;
    (e) Include all relevant product attributes;
    (f) Not infringe any third-party rights.

3.2 PRODUCT INFORMATION

    Required information for each listing:

    (a) Product title and description;
    (b) Brand name and model number;
    (c) Category and subcategory;
    (d) Price (MRP and selling price);
    (e) Stock quantity;
    (f) Product specifications;
    (g) HSN code;
    (h) Country of origin;
    (i) Warranty information (where applicable);
    (j) Package contents.

3.3 PRODUCT IMAGES

    Image requirements:

    (a) Minimum resolution: 1000 x 1000 pixels;
    (b) White background preferred;
    (c) Product must fill 85% of frame;
    (d) No watermarks or promotional text;
    (e) Multiple angles recommended;
    (f) Lifestyle images encouraged.

3.4 PRICING REQUIREMENTS

    (a) MRP must be accurate and match product packaging;
    (b) Selling price cannot exceed MRP;
    (c) Prices inclusive of all taxes;
    (d) Price changes reflected immediately;
    (e) Historical lowest price disclosure as per guidelines.

3.5 CATALOG QUALITY

    Company may:

    (a) Edit listings for quality and compliance;
    (b) Merge duplicate listings;
    (c) Remove non-compliant listings;
    (d) Suppress listings with poor performance.

                    ARTICLE 4: PRICING AND FEES
================================================================================

4.1 COMMISSION STRUCTURE

    Commission rates vary by category:

    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | CATEGORY                        | COMMISSION RATE                     |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | Electronics & Mobiles           | 5% - 12%                            |
    | Fashion & Apparel               | 10% - 25%                           |
    | Home & Kitchen                  | 8% - 18%                            |
    | Beauty & Personal Care          | 5% - 15%                            |
    | Books & Media                   | 3% - 10%                            |
    | Sports & Fitness                | 10% - 15%                           |
    | Baby Products                   | 8% - 15%                            |
    | Grocery & Gourmet               | 3% - 12%                            |
    | Automotive                      | 6% - 12%                            |
    | Industrial & Scientific         | 8% - 15%                            |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Detailed commission chart available in Seller Dashboard.

4.2 ADDITIONAL FEES

    (a) FIXED FEE: Rs. 10-30 per order (category dependent)

    (b) SHIPPING FEE: Based on weight, dimensions, and zone
        - Deducted from settlement for Company-shipped orders

    (c) COLLECTION FEE: 2% of order value
        - Payment gateway and processing costs

    (d) PICK AND PACK FEE: Rs. 15-40 per unit
        - For Fulfilled by TechVenture (FBT) orders

4.3 PROMOTIONAL FEES

    Optional fees for increased visibility:

    (a) Sponsored listings;
    (b) Deal of the day participation;
    (c) Category promotions;
    (d) Search boost.

4.4 FEE CALCULATION

    Net Settlement = Gross Sale - Commission - Fees - Taxes

    Example:
    Product Price: Rs. 1,000
    Commission (15%): Rs. 150
    Fixed Fee: Rs. 20
    Collection Fee (2%): Rs. 20
    Net Settlement: Rs. 810

4.5 TAX ON FEES

    (a) All fees are exclusive of GST;
    (b) GST (18%) charged on commission and fees;
    (c) Tax invoice provided for all deductions.

                    ARTICLE 5: ORDER FULFILLMENT
================================================================================

5.1 ORDER NOTIFICATION

    (a) Orders received via Seller Dashboard and email;
    (b) Seller must acknowledge within 24 hours;
    (c) Non-acknowledgment may result in auto-cancellation.

5.2 FULFILLMENT OPTIONS

    (a) SELLER FULFILLMENT (Self-Ship)
        - Seller handles packing and shipping
        - Uses own or Platform's shipping partners

    (b) FULFILLED BY TECHVENTURE (FBT)
        - Inventory stored in Company warehouses
        - Company handles packing and shipping
        - Higher fees but better performance metrics

    (c) EASY SHIP
        - Seller packs the order
        - Platform arranges pickup and delivery

5.3 FULFILLMENT TIMELINES

    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | FULFILLMENT TYPE          | PACK BY        | SHIP BY                  |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------
    | Self-Ship (Standard)      | 24 hours       | 48 hours                 |
    | Self-Ship (Express)       | 12 hours       | 24 hours                 |
    | Easy Ship                 | 24 hours       | Pickup scheduled         |
    | FBT                       | N/A            | Same/Next day            |
    -------------------------------------------------------------------------

5.4 PACKAGING REQUIREMENTS

    (a) Secure packaging to prevent damage;
    (b) No seller branding on outer package;
    (c) Platform's shipping label clearly visible;
    (d) Invoice and warranty card inside;
    (e) Tamper-evident seal where required.

5.5 ORDER CANCELLATION BY SELLER

    Seller-initiated cancellations should be avoided:

    (a) Cancel only if genuinely out of stock;
    (b) Impacts seller metrics and visibility;
    (c) Excessive cancellations lead to penalties;
    (d) Customer must be notified promptly.

================================================================================
                    ARTICLE 6: SHIPPING AND DELIVERY
================================================================================

6.1 SHIPPING RESPONSIBILITY

    (a) Self-Ship: Seller responsible for delivery
    (b) Easy Ship/FBT: Platform manages delivery

6.2 SHIPPING PARTNERS

    Approved shipping partners:

    (a) Platform logistics partners;
    (b) Pre-approved third-party couriers;
    (c) Seller's couriers (with tracking).

6.3 DELIVERY TIMELINES

    Seller must meet promised delivery dates:

    (a) Standard: 5-10 business days;
    (b) Express: 2-3 business days;
    (c) Same/Next day: As committed.

    Late deliveries impact seller rating.

6.4 SHIPPING CHARGES

    (a) Self-Ship: Seller bears cost or charges customer
    (b) Easy Ship: Deducted from settlement per rate card
    (c) FBT: Included in fulfillment fees

6.5 TRACKING REQUIREMENTS

    (a) Valid tracking ID for every shipment;
    (b) Update tracking status promptly;
    (c) Customer must be able to track order.

6.6 DELIVERY FAILURES

    (a) Coordinate with shipping partner on failures;
    (b) Reattempt delivery or arrange pickup;
    (c) Return to seller if undeliverable;
    (d) Refund customer promptly.

                    ARTICLE 7: PAYMENT TERMS
================================================================================

7.1 SETTLEMENT CYCLE

    (a) Standard settlement: 7 days after delivery
    (b) New sellers: 14 days (first 3 months)
    (c) Express settlement: Available for qualified sellers

7.2 PAYMENT METHOD

    (a) Bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS) to registered account;
    (b) Payments on scheduled settlement days;
    (c) Minimum settlement threshold: Rs. 100.

7.3 SETTLEMENT STATEMENT

    Detailed statement provided showing:

    (a) Gross sales;
    (b) Commission and fees;
    (c) Taxes deducted;
    (d) Refunds and returns;
    (e) Net payable amount.

7.4 TAX DEDUCTION

    (a) TCS (Tax Collected at Source) as per Income Tax Act;
    (b) Currently 1% of net taxable sales;
    (c) TCS certificate provided quarterly;
    (d) GST on fees deducted.

7.5 DISPUTED SETTLEMENTS

    (a) Raise dispute within 30 days of settlement;
    (b) Provide supporting documentation;
    (c) Resolution within 14 business days.

7.6 PAYMENT HOLDS

    Company may hold payments for:

    (a) Suspected fraud or policy violations;
    (b) High return/refund rates;
    (c) Customer complaints under investigation;
    (d) Account under review.

================================================================================
                    ARTICLE 8: RETURNS AND REFUNDS
================================================================================

8.1 RETURN POLICY

    (a) Seller must accept returns as per Platform policy;
    (b) Return window varies by category (7-30 days);
    (c) Customer-initiated returns as per eligibility;
    (d) Damaged/defective returns are mandatory.

8.2 RETURN REASONS

    Returns to be accepted for:

    (a) Product not as described;
    (b) Defective or damaged;
    (c) Wrong product shipped;
    (d) Size/fit issues (fashion);
    (e) Customer changed mind (select categories).

8.3 RETURN PROCESSING

    (a) Returns shipped back to seller or FBT warehouse;
    (b) Seller must confirm receipt within 2 business days;
    (c) QC check by seller on returned product;
    (d) Refund initiated within 2 days of QC.

8.4 RETURN SHIPPING COSTS

    (a) Seller bears cost for seller-fault returns;
    (b) Customer bears cost for change-of-mind returns;
    (c) Platform may recover from seller if applicable.

8.5 REFUND DEDUCTIONS

    Refund amount deducted from seller settlement:

    (a) Full product value refunded to customer;
    (b) Commission on cancelled order reversed;
    (c) Shipping and handling costs may apply.

8.6 RETURNLESS REFUNDS

    For low-value items, Platform may:

    (a) Refund customer without physical return;
    (b) Amount deducted from seller;
    (c) Decision based on cost-benefit analysis.
