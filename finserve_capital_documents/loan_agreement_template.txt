================================================================================
                    ARTICLE 1 - THE LOAN
================================================================================

1.1 LOAN AMOUNT

    The Lender agrees to lend and the Borrower agrees to borrow the Loan
    Amount specified in the Schedule.

1.2 PURPOSE

    The Loan shall be used solely for the purpose specified in the Schedule.
    The Borrower shall not divert the Loan for any other purpose.

1.3 DISBURSEMENT

    (a) The Loan shall be disbursed after:
        - Execution of this Agreement and security documents
        - Completion of all documentation
        - Payment of processing fee and charges
        - Satisfaction of all conditions precedent

    (b) Disbursement shall be made by credit to the Borrower's bank account
        or directly to vendors/suppliers as the Lender may decide.

================================================================================
                   ARTICLE 2 - INTEREST
================================================================================

2.1 INTEREST RATE

    The Borrower shall pay interest on the outstanding principal at the
    Interest Rate specified in the Schedule.

    Effective Annual Interest Rate (APR): _____% per annum

    The APR includes the impact of reducing balance, processing fees, and
    other charges on the effective cost of borrowing.

2.2 INTEREST CALCULATION

    Interest shall be calculated on daily reducing balance basis.

2.3 INTEREST RESET (FOR FLOATING RATE)

    For floating rate loans, the interest rate shall be reset annually
    based on the Lender's Base Rate prevailing at the time of reset.

2.4 INTEREST ON DEFAULT

    If any amount payable is not paid on the due date, the Borrower shall
    pay additional interest at 2% per month (24% per annum) on the overdue
    amount for the period of default.

================================================================================
                  ARTICLE 3 - REPAYMENT
================================================================================

3.1 REPAYMENT SCHEDULE

    The Borrower shall repay the Loan along with interest in Equated Monthly
    Installments (EMIs) as per the repayment schedule annexed hereto.

3.2 EMI PAYMENT

    (a) EMI shall be payable on the due date specified in the Schedule.

    (b) Payment shall be made through NACH/ECS/Standing Instruction/PDCs.

    (c) The Borrower shall ensure sufficient funds in the bank account on
        the due date.

3.3 PREPAYMENT

    (a) The Borrower may prepay the Loan in full or in part subject to
        prepayment charges as below:

        Prepayment during          Prepayment Charges
        -------------------------------------------------
        0-6 months                 4% of prepaid amount
        6-12 months                3% of prepaid amount
        12-24 months               2% of prepaid amount
        After 24 months            1% of prepaid amount

    (b) For floating rate loans to individuals, no prepayment charges shall
        be levied as per RBI guidelines.

    (c) Partial prepayment shall be in multiples of Rs. 10,000.

3.4 APPLICATION OF PAYMENTS

    Payments received shall be applied in the following order:
    1. Costs and expenses
    2. Late payment charges
    3. Interest due
    4. Principal due

================================================================================
                  ARTICLE 5 - GUARANTEE
================================================================================

5.1 GUARANTEE

    The Guarantor unconditionally and irrevocably guarantees to the Lender
    the due payment and performance by the Borrower of all obligations under
    this Agreement.

5.2 CONTINUING GUARANTEE

    This guarantee shall be a continuing guarantee and shall remain in force

5.3 PRIMARY OBLIGATION

    The Guarantor's liability is as a primary obligor and not merely as a

================================================================================
            ARTICLE 6 - REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES
================================================================================

6.1 The Borrower represents and warrants that:

    (a) The Borrower has legal capacity and authority to enter into this
        Agreement.

    (b) All information provided in the loan application is true and correct.

    (c) There is no litigation or proceeding pending against the Borrower
        that may materially affect ability to repay.

    (d) The Borrower is not a wilful defaulter with any bank or NBFC.

    (e) The security is free from encumbrance (except as disclosed).

    (f) The Borrower has not obtained this loan by misrepresentation.

================================================================================
               ARTICLE 7 - BORROWER'S COVENANTS
================================================================================

7.1 The Borrower shall:

    (a) Pay all EMIs and other amounts on due dates.

    (b) Use the Loan only for the stated purpose.

    (c) Maintain books of account and records.

    (d) Provide financial statements annually (or as required).

    (e) Inform the Lender of any material change in business or financial
        condition.

    (f) Maintain the security in good condition.

    (g) Pay all taxes, duties, and other charges on the security.

    (h) Permit inspection of the security and records by the Lender.

    (i) Not create any other charge on the security without consent.

    (j) Not change constitution, management, or business without consent.

7.2 The Borrower shall NOT:

    (a) Divert the Loan for any purpose other than stated.

    (b) Dispose of or encumber the security without consent.

    (c) Enter into any transaction that may prejudice the Lender's interest.

================================================================================
              ARTICLE 8 - EVENTS OF DEFAULT
================================================================================

8.1 EVENTS OF DEFAULT

    Each of the following is an Event of Default:

    (a) Non-payment of EMI or any other amount within 7 days of due date.

    (b) Three or more EMI bounces in a year.

    (c) Breach of any representation, warranty, or covenant.

    (d) Cross-default under any other loan agreement.

    (e) Any security becoming impaired or inadequate.

    (f) The Borrower becoming insolvent or bankrupt.

    (g) Any litigation or proceeding materially affecting the Borrower.

    (h) Death of individual Borrower or dissolution of firm.

    (i) Diversion of Loan proceeds.

    (j) Misrepresentation or fraud.

8.2 CONSEQUENCES OF DEFAULT

    Upon Event of Default, the Lender may:

    (a) Declare all outstanding amounts immediately due and payable.

    (b) Charge default interest and penal charges.

    (c) Enforce the security.

    (d) Take possession of secured assets.

    (e) Appoint receiver for the security.

    (f) File suit for recovery.

    (g) Report default to credit bureaus.

8.3 ACCELERATION

    The Lender may recall the entire Loan by giving 60 days notice if:
    (a) There is material adverse change in Borrower's condition
    (b) The security becomes inadequate
    (c) The Borrower breaches any material covenant

================================================================================
               ARTICLE 9 - ENFORCEMENT
================================================================================

9.1 The Lender may enforce the security through:

    (a) SARFAESI Act, 2002 (for secured loans above Rs. 1 Lakh)
    (b) Debt Recovery Tribunal (for debts above Rs. 20 Lakhs)
    (c) Civil Court
    (d) Arbitration

9.2 The Borrower waives any defense of:
    (a) Lis pendens
    (b) Res judicata (if not adjudicated on merits)
    (c) Limitation (subject to law)
