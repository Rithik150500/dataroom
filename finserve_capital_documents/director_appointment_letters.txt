Dear Ms. Krishnan,

Re: Appointment as Independent Director

We are pleased to inform you that the Board of Directors at its meeting held
on October 10, 2020 has appointed you as an Independent Director of the
Company for a term of 5 years with effect from October 15, 2020, subject to
approval of shareholders at the ensuing Annual General Meeting.

The shareholders at the AGM held on September 28, 2021 approved your
appointment as Independent Director.

TERMS OF APPOINTMENT:

1. TENURE:
   First Term: October 15, 2020 to October 14, 2025 (5 years)
   (Eligible for re-appointment for second term of 5 years)

2. INDEPENDENCE:
   You have confirmed that you meet the criteria of independence as
   prescribed under Section 149(6) of the Companies Act, 2013 and
   Regulation 16(1)(b) of SEBI LODR Regulations (as applicable).

   You shall inform the Company immediately if there is any change in
   circumstances which may affect your independence.

3. SITTING FEES:
   - Board Meeting: Rs. 50,000 per meeting attended
   - Committee Meeting: Rs. 30,000 per meeting attended

   Note: You are not entitled to any remuneration other than sitting fees
   as you are an Independent Director.

4. COMMITTEE MEMBERSHIPS:
   - Audit Committee: Chairperson
   - Nomination and Remuneration Committee: Member

5. DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
   As an Independent Director, you shall:

   (a) Bring independent judgment on issues of strategy, performance,
       risk management, and standards of conduct

   (b) Safeguard interests of all stakeholders, particularly minority
       shareholders

   (c) Monitor and oversee management performance

   (d) Satisfy yourself that financial controls and systems of risk
       management are robust

   (e) Determine appropriate levels of remuneration for executive directors

   (f) Have a prime role in recommending appointment/removal of directors

   (g) Moderate and arbitrate in the interest of the Company as a whole in
       situations of conflict

6. LIABILITIES:
   As per Section 149(12) of the Companies Act, 2013, an Independent
   Director shall be held liable only for acts of omission or commission
   by the Company which had occurred with his knowledge, attributable
   through Board processes, and with his consent or connivance, or where
   he had not acted diligently.

7. PROFESSIONAL INDEMNITY:
   The Company has D&O Insurance policy which provides coverage to Directors.

8. RBI COMPLIANCE (NBFC SPECIFIC):
   As the Company is an RBI-regulated NBFC, you shall:
   - Meet "fit and proper" criteria prescribed by RBI
   - Ensure proper oversight of regulatory compliance
   - Not be associated with any company whose NBFC registration was cancelled

9. CONFLICT OF INTEREST:
   You shall disclose any potential conflict of interest and abstain from
   related discussions and voting.

10. CODE OF CONDUCT:
    You shall abide by the Code for Independent Directors as prescribed
    in Schedule IV to the Companies Act, 2013.

11. RESIGNATION:
    You may resign by giving written notice to the Company. The resignation
    shall be effective from the date of Board taking note or 30 days from
    receipt of notice, whichever is earlier.

12. OTHER DIRECTORSHIPS:
    Your other directorships are noted. You shall ensure compliance with
    limits under Companies Act (max 20 companies, max 10 public companies).
