--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
              CONDITIONS ATTACHED TO THIS REGISTRATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This Certificate of Registration is subject to the following conditions:

1.  CAPITAL REQUIREMENTS

    (a) The Company shall maintain Net Owned Funds (NOF) of not less than
        Rs. 2 Crores at all times. (Current NOF: Rs. 142 Crores - Compliant)

    (b) The Company shall maintain CRAR of not less than 15% at all times.
        (Current CRAR: 18.2% - Compliant)

    (c) Tier I Capital shall not be less than 10% of risk-weighted assets.
        (Current Tier I: 13.8% - Compliant)

2.  BUSINESS RESTRICTIONS

    (a) The Company shall NOT accept public deposits without specific
        permission from the Reserve Bank of India.

    (b) The Company shall carry on business of NBFC only and no other
        business.

    (c) The Company shall comply with all provisions of the RBI Act, 1934
        and regulations, directions, guidelines, notifications and circulars
        issued by RBI from time to time.

3.  EXPOSURE NORMS

    (a) Single borrower exposure shall not exceed 25% of Owned Funds.

    (b) Group borrower exposure shall not exceed 40% of Owned Funds.

    (c) Investment in shares shall not exceed the limits prescribed by RBI.

4.  ASSET CLASSIFICATION AND PROVISIONING

    The Company shall classify its loan assets and make provisions in
    accordance with the Income Recognition, Asset Classification and
    Provisioning (IRACP) norms prescribed by RBI.

5.  FAIR PRACTICES CODE

    The Company shall adopt a Board-approved Fair Practices Code in compliance
    with RBI guidelines.

6.  KYC/AML COMPLIANCE

    The Company shall comply with Know Your Customer (KYC) guidelines and
    Anti-Money Laundering (AML) standards as prescribed by RBI and PMLA.

7.  CORPORATE GOVERNANCE

    (a) The Company shall constitute Board committees as required by RBI
        for NBFCs including Audit Committee, Risk Management Committee,
        and Nomination & Remuneration Committee.

    (b) Directors and Key Management Personnel shall meet "fit and proper"
        criteria prescribed by RBI.

    (c) The Company shall appoint Chief Risk Officer, Chief Compliance
        Officer, and Principal Officer as required.

8.  REPORTING AND RETURNS

    The Company shall submit to RBI all returns, statements, and information
    as prescribed within the specified time limits including:

    (a) Quarterly returns on asset classification and provisioning (NBS-7)
    (b) Half-yearly prudential norms return (NBS-8)
    (c) Monthly ALM returns
    (d) Annual statutory auditor's certificate on compliance
    (e) Any other returns as may be prescribed

9.  PRIOR APPROVAL REQUIREMENTS

    The Company shall obtain prior approval of RBI for:

    (a) Change in name of the Company
    (b) Shifting of registered office outside the state
    (c) Change in management or Managing Director/CEO
    (d) Any person acquiring more than 26% of paid-up capital
    (e) Any amalgamation, merger, or change in shareholding pattern
    (f) Any other matter requiring prior RBI approval

10. INSPECTION

    The Company and its books shall be open to inspection by RBI officers
    at any time. The Company shall cooperate fully with any inspection and
    shall address all observations raised by RBI.

11. CYBER SECURITY

    The Company shall implement cyber security framework as prescribed by
    RBI and shall report all cyber security incidents.

12. INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

    The Company shall comply with IT framework prescribed by RBI for NBFCs
    and shall undergo IS Audit by empanelled auditors.

13. GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL

    The Company shall put in place a proper grievance redressal mechanism
    and shall appoint a Nodal Officer for customer complaints.
