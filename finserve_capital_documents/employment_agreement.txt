================================================================================

================================================================================
             ARTICLE 5 - CONFIDENTIALITY
================================================================================

5.1 CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

    "Confidential Information" includes:

    (a) Customer data and borrower information
    (b) Loan portfolio and NPA details
    (c) Credit assessment models and algorithms
    (d) Business strategies and plans
    (e) Financial information and projections
    (f) Trade secrets and proprietary information
    (g) Employee and salary information
    (h) Any information marked as confidential

5.2 CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATION

    The Employee shall:

    (a) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party
    (b) Use Confidential Information only for Company's purposes
    (c) Take all reasonable measures to protect confidentiality
    (d) Return all materials containing Confidential Information upon
        termination

5.3 SURVIVAL

    The confidentiality obligation shall survive termination of employment
    and continue for a period of 5 years thereafter.

5.4 STATUTORY EXCEPTIONS

    Confidentiality obligations shall not apply to:
    (a) Information in the public domain
    (b) Disclosure required by law or regulatory authorities
    (c) Disclosure with written consent of the Company

================================================================================
            ARTICLE 6 - INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
================================================================================

6.1 WORK PRODUCT

    All work product created by the Employee during employment, including
    but not limited to:

    (a) Credit scoring models
    (b) Software and applications
    (c) Processes and procedures
    (d) Reports and presentations
    (e) Inventions and innovations

    shall be the exclusive property of the Company.

6.2 ASSIGNMENT

    The Employee hereby assigns to the Company all intellectual property
    rights in the work product created during employment.

6.3 ASSISTANCE

    The Employee shall provide all assistance required for protecting the
    Company's intellectual property rights, including signing documents
    and providing testimony.

================================================================================
             ARTICLE 7 - NON-COMPETITION
================================================================================

7.1 DURING EMPLOYMENT

    During the term of employment, the Employee shall not:

    (a) Engage in any other employment or business without prior written
        consent
    (b) Provide services to any competitor
    (c) Have any financial interest in any competitor (except as a passive
        investor with less than 5% holding)

7.2 POST-EMPLOYMENT

    For a period of 12 months after termination, the Employee shall not:

    (a) Solicit or service any customer of the Company with whom the
        Employee had dealings during last 12 months
    (b) Solicit or hire any employee of the Company
    (c) Disparage the Company or its products/services

    Note: Non-compete clauses restricting employment after termination may
    not be enforceable under Indian law. This clause is limited to non-
    solicitation of customers and employees.

================================================================================
             ARTICLE 8 - TERMINATION
================================================================================

8.1 TERMINATION BY COMPANY

    The Company may terminate this Agreement:

    (a) WITH NOTICE: By giving 3 months written notice or payment in lieu
        of notice

    (b) WITHOUT NOTICE: Immediately in case of:
        - Misconduct or dishonesty
        - Breach of confidentiality
        - Gross negligence
        - Violation of Company policies
        - Conviction for criminal offense
        - Failure to meet performance standards after warnings
        - Any act detrimental to Company's interests

8.2 TERMINATION BY EMPLOYEE

    The Employee may terminate this Agreement by giving 3 months written
    notice.

8.3 NOTICE PERIOD

    During the notice period:
    (a) Employee shall continue to perform duties
    (b) Employee shall hand over responsibilities
    (c) Company may relieve Employee earlier

8.4 PAYMENT ON TERMINATION

    Upon termination, the Employee shall be entitled to:
    (a) Salary up to last working day
    (b) Leave encashment for unused earned leave
    (c) Reimbursement of approved expenses
    (d) Pro-rata bonus (if applicable and as per policy)

8.5 RETURN OF PROPERTY

    Upon termination, the Employee shall return:
    (a) All Company property (laptop, mobile, ID card, etc.)
    (b) All documents and materials
    (c) All Confidential Information
    (d) Complete knowledge transfer

================================================================================
            ARTICLE 9 - REGULATORY COMPLIANCE
================================================================================

9.1 RBI COMPLIANCE

    The Employee acknowledges that the Company is regulated by RBI and shall:

    (a) Comply with all RBI regulations applicable to NBFCs
    (b) Follow Fair Practices Code in dealings with customers
    (c) Maintain confidentiality of customer information
    (d) Not engage in any act that may attract regulatory action

9.2 FIT AND PROPER (FOR SENIOR POSITIONS)

    If the Employee is in a senior management position, the Employee shall:

    (a) Meet "fit and proper" criteria prescribed by RBI
    (b) Provide declaration and undertaking as required
    (c) Inform the Company of any change affecting the criteria

9.3 TRAINING

    The Employee shall attend mandatory compliance training including:
    (a) KYC/AML training
    (b) Fair Practices Code training
    (c) Cyber security awareness
    (d) POSH (Prevention of Sexual Harassment) training

================================================================================
             ARTICLE 10 - GENERAL PROVISIONS
================================================================================

10.1 PROBATION

     The Employee shall be on probation for a period of 6 months from the
     Commencement Date. During probation:
     (a) Notice period shall be 1 month
     (b) Company may terminate without cause with 1 month notice
     (c) Confirmation shall be subject to satisfactory performance

10.2 BACKGROUND VERIFICATION

     This employment is subject to satisfactory background verification
     including:
     (a) Education verification
     (b) Employment history verification
     (c) Criminal record check
     (d) Reference check

10.3 ENTIRE AGREEMENT

     This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties
     and supersedes all prior negotiations and agreements.

10.4 AMENDMENTS

     This Agreement may be amended only by written instrument signed by
     both Parties.

10.5 SEVERABILITY

     If any provision is held invalid, the remaining provisions shall
     continue in full force and effect.

10.6 GOVERNING LAW

     This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.

10.7 JURISDICTION

     The courts at Mumbai shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

10.8 COMPANY POLICIES

     The Employee shall comply with all Company policies including:
     (a) Code of Conduct
     (b) HR Policy
     (c) IT and Data Security Policy
     (d) POSH Policy
     (e) Whistleblower Policy
