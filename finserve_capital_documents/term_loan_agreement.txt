================================================================================
                         RECITALS
================================================================================

WHEREAS:

A. The Borrower is a Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC) registered with
   the Reserve Bank of India under Registration Number N-14.03268, engaged
   in the business of providing loans to SMEs, vehicle finance, and MSME
   working capital.

B. The Borrower has approached the Bank for a term loan facility to meet
   its business requirements for onward lending to its customers.

C. The Bank has agreed to provide the term loan facility to the Borrower
   subject to the terms and conditions set out in this Agreement.

NOW THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements
herein contained, the Parties agree as follows:

================================================================================
                    ARTICLE 1 - DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

1.1 In this Agreement, unless the context otherwise requires:

    "Availability Period" means the period of 3 months from the date of
    this Agreement during which the Borrower may draw down the Facility.

    "Business Day" means a day on which banks are open for business in Mumbai.

    "Event of Default" has the meaning given in Article 8.

    "Facility" means the term loan facility of Rs. 50,00,00,000 (Rupees
    Fifty Crores Only) granted by the Bank to the Borrower.

    "Facility Documents" means this Agreement and all other documents
    executed in connection with this Facility.

    "Final Repayment Date" means June 15, 2025.

    "Interest Payment Date" means the last Business Day of each month.

    "Interest Rate" means MCLR plus Spread.

    "Loan Receivables" means all present and future book debts, loan
    receivables, and other amounts due to the Borrower from its borrowers.

    "MCLR" means the Marginal Cost of Funds based Lending Rate of the Bank.

    "Repayment Date" means each date on which a Repayment Installment is due.

    "Security" has the meaning given in Article 5.

    "Spread" means 1.50% (150 basis points) per annum.

================================================================================
                  ARTICLE 2 - THE FACILITY
================================================================================

2.1 FACILITY AMOUNT

    The Bank agrees to make available to the Borrower a term loan facility
    in an aggregate amount not exceeding Rs. 50,00,00,000 (Rupees Fifty
    Crores Only).

2.2 PURPOSE

    The Facility shall be used solely for:
    (a) Onward lending to borrowers in the ordinary course of the
        Borrower's NBFC business
    (b) General corporate purposes

    The Borrower shall not use the Facility for:
    (a) Investment in capital market
    (b) Real estate activities
    (c) Any speculative purpose
    (d) Any purpose prohibited by RBI for NBFCs

2.3 AVAILABILITY

    Subject to the conditions precedent being satisfied, the Facility shall
    be available for drawing during the Availability Period.

2.4 DRAWDOWN

    (a) The Borrower may draw down the Facility in minimum amounts of
        Rs. 10,00,00,000 (Rupees Ten Crores Only).

    (b) Each drawdown request shall be submitted at least 3 Business Days
        before the proposed drawdown date.

    (c) Any amount not drawn during the Availability Period shall stand
        cancelled.

================================================================================
                  ARTICLE 3 - INTEREST
================================================================================

3.1 INTEREST RATE

    The Borrower shall pay interest on the outstanding principal amount at
    the Interest Rate.

    Current Interest Rate:
    - MCLR (1 Year): 8.00% p.a.
    - Spread: 1.50% p.a.
    - Total Interest Rate: 9.50% p.a.

3.2 INTEREST RESET

    The Interest Rate shall be reset on every anniversary of the first
    drawdown based on the then prevailing MCLR.

3.3 INTEREST CALCULATION

    Interest shall be calculated on the basis of a year of 365 days and the
    actual number of days elapsed.

3.4 INTEREST PAYMENT

    Interest accrued during each month shall be payable on the Interest
    Payment Date of that month.

3.5 DEFAULT INTEREST

    If the Borrower fails to pay any amount due under this Agreement on its
    due date, the Borrower shall pay default interest on the overdue amount
    at the rate of 2% per annum above the Interest Rate.

================================================================================
                   ARTICLE 4 - REPAYMENT
================================================================================

4.1 REPAYMENT SCHEDULE

    The Borrower shall repay the Facility in 20 equal quarterly installments
    of Rs. 2,50,00,000 (Rupees Two Crores Fifty Lakhs Only) each, commencing
    from the expiry of the moratorium period.

    Moratorium Period: 3 months from first drawdown

    Repayment Schedule:
    Installment    Due Date         Amount (Rs. Cr)
    -----------------------------------------------
    1              30/09/2020       2.50
    2              31/12/2020       2.50
    3              31/03/2021       2.50
    ...            ...              ...
    20             15/06/2025       2.50
    -----------------------------------------------
    Total                          50.00

4.2 PREPAYMENT

    (a) The Borrower may prepay all or part of the Facility subject to
        giving at least 15 Business Days prior written notice.

    (b) Prepayment charges:
        - Within 1 year: 2% of prepaid amount
        - After 1 year: 1% of prepaid amount

    (c) Partial prepayments shall be in minimum amounts of Rs. 5,00,00,000
        (Rupees Five Crores Only) and shall be applied against installments
        in inverse order of maturity.

4.3 MANDATORY PREPAYMENT

    The Borrower shall prepay the Facility upon:
    (a) Change in control of the Borrower
    (b) Cancellation of RBI registration
    (c) Any event which in the Bank's opinion materially adversely affects
        the Borrower's ability to repay

================================================================================
                   ARTICLE 5 - SECURITY
================================================================================

5.1 SECURITY DOCUMENTS

    The Facility is secured by:

    (a) PRIMARY SECURITY:
        First pari passu charge on the Borrower's present and future Loan
        Receivables with minimum asset cover of 1.10 times.

    (b) ADDITIONAL SECURITY:
        (i)  Personal Guarantee of Mr. Rajesh Kumar Mehta (Promoter)
        (ii) Corporate Guarantee of FinServe Holdings Pvt Ltd

5.2 CREATION OF SECURITY

    (a) The Borrower shall execute a Deed of Hypothecation in favor of the
        Bank for creation of first pari passu charge on Loan Receivables.

    (b) The charge shall be registered with the Registrar of Companies within
        30 days of creation.

    (c) The charge shall also be registered with CERSAI within 30 days.

5.3 ASSET COVER

    The Borrower shall ensure that the value of the charged assets is at
    all times at least 1.10 times the outstanding Facility amount. If the
    asset cover falls below 1.10x, the Borrower shall:
    (a) Provide additional security, or
    (b) Prepay the Facility to restore the asset cover

5.4 SHARING OF SECURITY

    The Bank agrees to share the security on pari passu basis with other
    lenders to the Borrower, subject to execution of an inter-creditor
    agreement.

================================================================================
             ARTICLE 6 - CONDITIONS PRECEDENT
================================================================================

6.1 CONDITIONS TO FIRST DRAWDOWN

    The Bank shall not be obliged to make the first drawdown unless:

    (a) CORPORATE DOCUMENTS:
        - Certified copies of MOA, AOA, Certificate of Incorporation
        - Board Resolution authorizing the borrowing
        - KYC documents of the Company and Directors

    (b) REGULATORY:
        - RBI Certificate of Registration
        - Certificate that borrowing is within limits permitted by RBI

    (c) SECURITY DOCUMENTS:
        - Executed Deed of Hypothecation
        - Personal Guarantee of Promoter
        - Corporate Guarantee

    (d) FINANCIAL:
        - Audited financial statements for last 3 years
        - Satisfactory credit report from credit bureaus
        - No Event of Default or potential Event of Default

    (e) LEGAL:
        - Legal opinion on enforceability of documents
        - No litigation materially affecting the Borrower

    (f) OTHER:
        - Payment of all fees and expenses
        - Any other document reasonably required by the Bank

================================================================================
                 ARTICLE 7 - COVENANTS
================================================================================

7.1 AFFIRMATIVE COVENANTS

    The Borrower shall:

    (a) FINANCIAL COVENANTS:
        - Maintain CRAR of at least 15%
        - Maintain Debt to Equity ratio below 4:1
        - Maintain asset cover of 1.10x
        - Maintain Net NPA below 6%

    (b) REPORTING:
        - Submit quarterly unaudited financial statements within 45 days
        - Submit annual audited financial statements within 90 days
        - Submit monthly stock statements
        - Inform of any Event of Default within 5 days

    (c) COMPLIANCE:
        - Maintain RBI registration
        - Comply with all applicable laws
        - File all returns with RBI on time

    (d) OTHER:
        - Maintain adequate insurance
        - Permit inspection by Bank officials
        - Use the Facility for stated purpose

7.2 NEGATIVE COVENANTS

    Without the Bank's prior written consent, the Borrower shall not:

    (a) Change its constitution or management
    (b) Declare dividend if financial covenants are breached
    (c) Create any security over its assets ranking pari passu or prior
    (d) Make any material change in business
    (e) Dispose of substantial assets
    (f) Undertake amalgamation or reconstruction
    (g) Make loans to directors or group companies

================================================================================
                ARTICLE 8 - EVENTS OF DEFAULT
================================================================================

8.1 EVENTS OF DEFAULT

    Each of the following is an Event of Default:

    (a) The Borrower fails to pay any amount due under the Facility Documents
        on its due date.

    (b) Any representation or warranty made is incorrect or misleading.

    (c) The Borrower fails to comply with any covenant for 15 days after
        notice from the Bank.

    (d) Any cross-default occurs (default under any other loan agreement
        with any creditor for amount exceeding Rs. 1 Crore).

    (e) The Borrower's RBI registration is cancelled or suspended.

    (f) Any insolvency or winding up proceedings are initiated.

    (g) Any judgment or award against the Borrower is not satisfied within
        30 days.

    (h) Any event occurs which in the Bank's opinion materially adversely
        affects the Borrower's ability to perform its obligations.

8.2 CONSEQUENCES OF EVENT OF DEFAULT

    Upon occurrence of an Event of Default:

    (a) The Bank may declare the entire outstanding amount immediately due
        and payable.

    (b) The Bank may cancel any undrawn commitment.

    (c) The Bank may enforce the Security.

    (d) The Bank may exercise any other rights available under law.

================================================================================
                 ARTICLE 9 - FEES AND EXPENSES
================================================================================

9.1 PROCESSING FEE

    The Borrower shall pay a non-refundable processing fee of 0.50% of the
    Facility amount (Rs. 25,00,000) upon execution of this Agreement.

9.2 COMMITMENT FEE

    The Borrower shall pay a commitment fee of 0.25% per annum on the undrawn
    portion of the Facility during the Availability Period.

9.3 EXPENSES

    The Borrower shall pay all costs and expenses incurred by the Bank in
    connection with the Facility including legal fees, stamp duty, and
    registration charges.
