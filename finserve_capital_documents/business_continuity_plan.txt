================================================================================
                    EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

This Business Continuity Plan (BCP) establishes the framework to ensure
continuity of critical business operations of FinServe Capital Limited in
the event of any disruption. The plan covers:

- Identification of critical business functions
- Recovery Time Objectives (RTO) and Recovery Point Objectives (RPO)
- Disaster Recovery procedures
- Communication protocols
- Testing and maintenance

================================================================================
                  I. POLICY STATEMENT
================================================================================

FinServe Capital Limited is committed to ensuring continuity of critical
operations and minimizing the impact of any disruption on:
- Customers and borrowers
- Employees
- Regulatory compliance
- Business operations
- Reputation

This BCP is aligned with:
- RBI Master Direction on IT Framework for NBFC Sector
- ISO 22301 Business Continuity Management Standard
- Company's Risk Management Policy

================================================================================
              II. SCOPE AND OBJECTIVES
================================================================================

2.1 SCOPE

This BCP covers:
- All critical business functions

2.2 OBJECTIVES

           V. DISASTER RECOVERY (DR) STRATEGY
================================================================================

5.1 DR SITE

Primary Site:     Peninsula Business Park, Lower Parel, Mumbai
DR Site:          Data Center, Mahape, Navi Mumbai
Distance:         25 km from primary site
Mode:             Active-Passive with real-time replication
Provider:         XYZ Data Center Services Pvt Ltd

5.2 DR INFRASTRUCTURE

The DR site maintains:
- Replica of all production servers
- Network connectivity with redundancy
- Power backup (UPS + DG)
- Fire suppression systems
- Physical security

5.3 DATA BACKUP

Backup Type         Frequency       Retention     Storage
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Full Backup         Weekly          4 weeks       DR Site + Cloud
Incremental         Daily           7 days        DR Site
Transaction Logs    Real-time       72 hours      DR Site
Database            Every 1 hour    7 days        DR Site

5.4 RECOVERY PROCEDURES

Level 1: Hardware Failure (Single Component)
- Auto-failover to redundant component
- RTO: < 30 minutes
- No data loss expected

Level 2: Server Failure
- Restore from latest backup on standby server
- RTO: 2-4 hours
- Data loss: < 1 hour (as per backup frequency)

Level 3: Site Disaster
- Activate DR site
- Redirect network traffic
- RTO: 4-8 hours
- Data loss: < 1 hour (real-time replication)

              VII. PANDEMIC RESPONSE
================================================================================

(Enhanced after COVID-19 experience)

7.1 WORK FROM HOME CAPABILITY

- All employees have laptops with VPN access
- Cloud-based collaboration tools (MS Teams, Zoom)
- Digital signature capability
- Remote access to core systems

7.2 PANDEMIC TRIGGERS

Level 1 (Awareness): WHO advisory, cases reported in country
Level 2 (Preparedness): Cases in state, government advisory
Level 3 (Activation): Lockdown, cases in office location

7.3 PANDEMIC ACTIONS

- Activate work from home for all non-essential staff
- Rotate essential staff to reduce exposure
- Enhanced hygiene at office
- Suspend non-essential travel
- Daily health monitoring
