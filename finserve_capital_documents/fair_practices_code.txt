                         PREAMBLE
================================================================================

This Fair Practices Code ("Code") has been framed in accordance with the
guidelines issued by the Reserve Bank of India vide Master Direction -
Reserve Bank of India (Non-Banking Financial Company - Scale Based Regulation)
Directions, 2023 dated October 19, 2023, as amended from time to time.

The objective of this Code is to:
1. Promote good and fair practices by setting minimum standards
2. Increase transparency for the benefit of customers
3. Encourage market forces through fair competition
4. Foster confidence in the NBFC system

================================================================================
               I. APPLICATIONS FOR LOANS AND PROCESSING
================================================================================

1.1 LOAN APPLICATION FORM

    (a) The Company shall provide a loan application form that includes:
        - Necessary information affecting the interest of the borrower
        - Terms and conditions clearly stated
        - Information required from borrower

    (b) The application form shall be in vernacular language or a language
        understood by the borrower.

    (c) The Company shall provide acknowledgment of receipt of all loan
        applications within 3 working days.

1.2 INFORMATION TO BE PROVIDED TO BORROWER

    At the time of sanction, the Company shall provide a sanction letter
    containing the following information:

    (a) Amount of loan sanctioned and disbursed
    (b) Tenure of the loan
    (c) Rate of interest (ROI) - whether fixed or floating
    (d) Effective annualized rate of interest
    (e) Method of interest application (monthly/quarterly/yearly rest)
    (f) Amount of EMI and due date
    (g) Processing fees and other charges
    (h) Pre-payment/foreclosure charges
    (i) Penal interest/charges for delayed payment
    (j) Security/collateral required
    (k) Documents required
    (l) Terms and conditions attached to sanction

1.3 LOAN APPRAISAL AND TERMS

    (a) Loan appraisal shall be based on prudent credit assessment
    (b) Terms and conditions shall be transparent and fair
    (c) The Company shall not interfere in the affairs of the borrower
        except for the purposes provided in the terms and conditions

1.4 TIME FRAME FOR LOAN PROCESSING

    Product Type                      Processing Time
    -----------------------------------------------
    Vehicle Finance                   5 working days
    SME Term Loan                     10 working days
    MSME Working Capital              7 working days
    Loan Against Property             15 working days

================================================================================
                II. DISBURSEMENT OF LOANS
================================================================================

2.1 LOAN AGREEMENT

    (a) The loan agreement shall be executed on non-judicial stamp paper
        of appropriate value

    (b) A copy of the loan agreement along with all enclosures shall be
        provided to the borrower free of cost

    (c) The agreement shall contain:
        - All terms and conditions as per sanction letter
        - Right of pre-payment and charges thereof
        - Acceleration clause clearly explained
        - Personal guarantee requirements
        - Security documentation

2.2 DISBURSEMENT

    (a) Disbursement shall be made only after execution of all documents

    (b) Disbursement shall be through account payee cheque / RTGS / NEFT
        to the borrower's bank account

    (c) In case of vehicle/equipment finance, disbursement may be made
        directly to the dealer/vendor

2.3 POST-DISBURSEMENT

    Within 7 days of disbursement, the Company shall provide:
    - Repayment schedule showing EMI amount, principal, interest breakup
    - List of documents executed
    - Welcome letter with contact details

================================================================================
                 III. INTEREST RATES
================================================================================

3.1 INTEREST RATE MODEL

    The Company follows an interest rate model based on:
    - Cost of funds
    - Operating costs
    - Risk premium based on borrower profile
    - Market conditions

3.2 INTEREST RATE RANGE

    Product                     Interest Rate Range (per annum)
    -----------------------------------------------------------
    Vehicle Finance             12% - 18%
    SME Term Loans              14% - 20%
    MSME Working Capital        13% - 19%
    Loan Against Property       11% - 16%

    Note: Actual rate depends on individual borrower assessment.

3.3 ANNUALIZED INTEREST RATE

    The Company shall quote the annualized rate of interest so that the
    borrower can compare the rates charged by different lenders.

3.4 INTEREST RATE CHANGES

    (a) Changes in interest rates due to reset clause shall be communicated
        to the borrower in advance (at least 30 days)

    (b) Borrowers shall be given the option to prepay without penalty in
        case of interest rate reset (for floating rate loans)

3.5 INTEREST ON LOANS

    The Company shall ensure that interest is not levied on any component
    other than the principal outstanding.

================================================================================
                  IV. PROCESSING AND OTHER CHARGES
================================================================================

4.1 SCHEDULE OF CHARGES

    Charge Type                       Amount/Rate
    ---------------------------------------------
    Processing Fee                    0.5% - 2% of loan amount
    Documentation Charges             Rs. 500 - Rs. 5,000
    Legal/Valuation Charges           At actuals
    Stamp Duty                        As per State laws
    CERSAI Registration               Rs. 100 - Rs. 500
    Insurance (if applicable)         As per insurance company

    Pre-closure Charges:
    - Within 6 months                 4% of principal outstanding
    - 6-12 months                     3% of principal outstanding
    - 12-24 months                    2% of principal outstanding
    - After 24 months                 1% of principal outstanding

    Individual borrowers taking floating rate term loans shall NOT be
    charged pre-closure charges (as per RBI guidelines).

    Late Payment Charges              2% per month on overdue amount
    Cheque/ECS Bounce Charges         Rs. 500 per instance
    Part Payment Charges              As per pre-closure charges
    Duplicate Statement               Rs. 100 per copy
    Document Retrieval                Rs. 500 per document
    NOC/No Dues Certificate           Free (within 30 days of closure)

4.2 DISCLOSURE OF CHARGES

    (a) The schedule of charges shall be displayed prominently at all
        branches and on the Company's website

    (b) All charges shall be disclosed upfront in the sanction letter

    (c) No hidden charges shall be levied

================================================================================
              V. GENERAL GUIDELINES FOR LENDING
================================================================================

5.1 TRANSPARENCY IN DEALINGS

    (a) The Company shall not discriminate against any applicant on grounds
        of sex, caste, religion, or region

    (b) Decisions shall be based solely on creditworthiness and financial
        parameters

    (c) Reasons for rejection shall be provided in writing if requested

5.2 NON-INTERFERENCE

    The Company shall not interfere in the affairs of the borrower except:
    - For the purposes stated in the loan agreement
    - As provided by law
    - In case of default by borrower

5.3 NO HARASSMENT

    (a) The Company shall not resort to undue harassment of borrowers

    (b) Staff and recovery agents shall be instructed to use decent
        language and behavior

    (c) Calling time shall be restricted to 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM

5.4 TAKEOVER OF LOANS

    While taking over loans from other banks/NBFCs, the Company shall:
    - Verify outstanding balance and lien status
    - Not demand additional collateral unless justified
    - Provide foreclosure statement within 30 days

================================================================================
                   VI. SECURITY AND DOCUMENTATION
================================================================================

6.1 SECURITY CREATION

    (a) Security creation shall be proportionate to the loan amount

    (b) Exclusive charge/security shall not be obtained for loans below
        Rs. 1 Crore where shared charge is available

    (c) Third-party security shall require written consent of third party

6.2 RETURN OF SECURITY

    (a) Upon repayment of all dues, the Company shall release all securities
        within 30 days

    (b) Original documents shall be returned within 30 days of closure

    (c) NOC/No Dues Certificate shall be provided within 30 days of closure

    (d) CERSAI registration shall be marked as satisfied within 30 days

6.3 LOSS OF SECURITY DOCUMENTS

    In case of loss of security documents in the Company's custody, the
    Company shall:
    - Assist the borrower in obtaining duplicate documents
    - Bear the costs of obtaining duplicates
    - Compensate the borrower for any loss caused

================================================================================
                  VII. DEFAULT AND CONSEQUENCES
================================================================================

7.1 CLASSIFICATION OF DEFAULT

    Category        Definition
    --------------------------
    SMA-0           Principal/interest payment overdue 1-30 days
    SMA-1           Principal/interest payment overdue 31-60 days
    SMA-2           Principal/interest payment overdue 61-90 days
    NPA             Principal/interest payment overdue > 90 days

7.2 COMMUNICATION ON DEFAULT

    (a) The Company shall communicate to the borrower at each stage of default

    (b) Reminder notices shall be sent at 7, 30, 60, and 90 days overdue

    (c) Consequences of continued default shall be clearly communicated

7.3 ACCELERATION OF PAYMENT

    (a) Acceleration clause shall be exercised only as per terms of agreement

    (b) Adequate notice (at least 60 days) shall be given before recall

    (c) Recall shall be made only for genuine default, not minor breaches

7.4 SALE OF SECURED ASSETS

    (a) Before sale of secured assets, the Company shall give notice of
        at least 30 days

    (b) The notice shall specify the dues and consequences

    (c) Sale shall be conducted in a fair and transparent manner

    (d) Surplus amount shall be returned to the borrower

================================================================================
              VIII. COLLECTION OF DUES AND RECOVERY
================================================================================

8.1 RECOVERY POLICY

    (a) Recovery agents shall be properly trained before deployment

    (b) Recovery agents shall carry identity cards and authorization letters

    (c) Borrower shall be informed of the names and contact numbers of
        recovery agents

8.2 CODE OF CONDUCT FOR RECOVERY

    The following practices are strictly prohibited:

    ❌ Use of abusive language or physical force
    ❌ Harassment of family members, neighbors, or colleagues
    ❌ Visits at odd hours (before 8 AM or after 7 PM)
    ❌ Sending recovery agents on public holidays
    ❌ Removal of borrower's belongings without due process
    ❌ Publishing names of defaulters in media
    ❌ Threats or intimidation
    ❌ Repeated calls causing inconvenience

8.3 GRIEVANCE AGAINST RECOVERY AGENTS

    (a) Borrowers can complain about recovery agent behavior

    (b) Complaints shall be investigated within 7 days

    (c) Action shall be taken against erring agents including termination

    (d) Feedback to borrower shall be provided within 15 days

================================================================================
               X. CREDIT REPORTING TO CREDIT BUREAUS
================================================================================

10.1 REPORTING MECHANISM

    The Company reports credit information to the following Credit
    Information Companies (CICs):
    - TransUnion CIBIL
    - Experian
    - Equifax
    - CRIF High Mark

10.2 BORROWER'S RIGHTS

    (a) Borrower shall be informed about reporting of credit information

    (b) Borrower has the right to obtain their credit report from CICs

    (c) If borrower finds any error, they may request correction

    (d) The Company shall rectify errors within 21 days of intimation

10.3 CONSENT

    Consent for accessing and reporting credit information shall be obtained
    from the borrower in the loan application form.

================================================================================
                XI. DIGITAL LENDING GUIDELINES
================================================================================

11.1 TRANSPARENCY IN DIGITAL LENDING

    For loans originated through digital channels:

    (a) The Company shall display Key Fact Statement (KFS) before execution
        of loan agreement

    (b) All fees and charges shall be clearly disclosed on app/website

    (c) Consent for data access shall be clearly explained and obtained

11.2 KEY FACT STATEMENT (KFS)

    The KFS shall contain:
    - Annual Percentage Rate (APR)
    - Recovery mechanism
    - Grievance redressal mechanism
    - Cooling-off period details

11.3 COOLING-OFF PERIOD

    The borrower shall be given a cooling-off period of at least 3 days
    during which they can exit the loan without any charges.

================================================================================
                  XII. LANGUAGE AND COMMUNICATION
================================================================================

12.1 LANGUAGE

    All communications shall be in a language understood by the borrower
    (English, Hindi, or regional language).

12.2 KEY DOCUMENTS IN VERNACULAR

    The following documents shall be available in vernacular languages:
    - Loan application form
    - Sanction letter
    - Most Important Terms and Conditions (MITC)
    - Schedule of charges
