MEMBERS PRESENT:

IN ATTENDANCE:

- Mr. Vikram Joshi       - CFO

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ITEM 1: REVIEW OF CREDIT RISK

Portfolio at Risk Analysis:
- PAR 0-30: 8.2%
- PAR 31-60: 3.5%
- PAR 61-90: 2.1%
- PAR 90+: 9.3%

Concentration Analysis:
- Top 20 borrowers: 28% of portfolio
- Maharashtra exposure: 38% (recommend diversification)
- Sector concentration within limits

RESOLVED that management present geographic diversification plan at next meeting.

ITEM 2: LIQUIDITY RISK REVIEW

- LCR: 152% (comfortable)
- No negative gaps in critical buckets (1-7 days, 8-14 days)
- Unutilized bank lines: Rs. 45 Crores

RESOLVED that liquidity position be noted as satisfactory.

ITEM 3: STRESS TESTING RESULTS

Stress scenarios tested:
1. NPA increase by 50%: CRAR drops to 14.8% (below threshold)
2. Interest rate increase by 200 bps: NIM drops by 80 bps
3. Combination scenario: Capital injection of Rs. 30 Cr required

RESOLVED that stress testing results be presented to Board with capital
contingency plan.

ITEM 4: OPERATIONAL RISK INCIDENTS

Q2 Incidents:
- 3 operational risk events (total loss Rs. 2.5 Lakhs)
- No fraud detected
- All incidents resolved

RESOLVED that operational risk report be noted.

ITEM 5: CYBER SECURITY UPDATE

- 2 minor incidents in Q2 (phishing attempts, blocked)
- VAPT remediation: 5 of 7 medium vulnerabilities closed
- Cyber insurance claim for forensic costs: Rs. 2 Lakhs

RESOLVED that cyber security status be noted with direction to close
remaining vulnerabilities by December 2024.

Meeting concluded at 12:30 PM.
