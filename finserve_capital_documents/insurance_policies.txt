================================================================================

POLICY DETAILS

Sum Insured:                    Rs. 10,00,00,000 (Rupees Ten Crores Only)
Premium:                        Rs. 12,50,000 + GST
Deductible:                     Rs. 1,00,000 per claim

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

COVERAGE

This policy provides coverage to the Directors and Officers of the Company
for claims made against them for wrongful acts committed in their capacity
as Directors and Officers.

INSURED PERSONS:
- All past, present, and future Directors
- All past, present, and future Officers
- Company Secretary
- Key Management Personnel

WRONGFUL ACTS COVERED:
- Breach of duty
- Breach of trust
- Neglect
- Error
- Misleading statement
- Omission
- Other act done or alleged to have been done in insured capacity

COVERAGE INCLUDES:
(a) Directors and Officers Liability
(b) Company Reimbursement
(c) Company Securities Liability
(d) Employment Practices Liability
(e) Prosecution Costs
(f) Extradition Costs
(g) Public Relations Expenses

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

EXCLUSIONS:
- Fraud, dishonesty, or criminal acts
- Personal profit or advantage
- Insured vs. Insured claims
- Prior or pending litigation
- Pollution liability
- ERISA claims
- Bodily injury or property damage

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

KEY TERMS:
- Claims-made policy (claims must be made during policy period)
- Retroactive date: August 12, 2015 (date of incorporation)
- Extended reporting period: 60 days (automatic), up to 3 years (optional)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

CLAIMS HISTORY:
- FY 2024-25: No claims
- FY 2023-24: No claims
- FY 2022-23: 1 claim settled (Rs. 12 Lakhs) - Customer allegation against
  officer, settled without admission of liability
