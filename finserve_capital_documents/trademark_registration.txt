          IV. PROPRIETARY CREDIT SCORING MODELS
================================================================================

The Company has developed proprietary credit scoring models which are
maintained as Trade Secrets.

MODEL 1: "FinScore SME" - SME Credit Scoring Model

Description:    Machine learning based credit scoring model for SME borrowers
Developed:      2018, enhanced in 2021
Key Features:   - Incorporates 150+ variables
                - Bureau data + financial data + behavioral data
                - Industry-specific risk factors
                - Geographic risk adjustments

Protection:     Maintained as Trade Secret
Access:         Restricted to Credit team only
NDA:            All employees with access have signed NDAs

MODEL 2: "FinScore Auto" - Vehicle Finance Scoring Model

Description:    Credit scoring model for vehicle finance
Developed:      2019
Key Features:   - Asset quality assessment
                - Borrower creditworthiness
                - Loan-to-value optimization

Protection:     Maintained as Trade Secret

MODEL 3: Early Warning System (EWS)

Description:    AI-based early warning system for NPAs
Developed:      2023
Key Features:   - Predictive analytics for defaults
                - Behavioral pattern recognition
                - 60-day advance warning

Protection:     Maintained as Trade Secret
