================================================================================
                         RECITALS
================================================================================

A. The Lessor is the owner of the property described in Schedule I hereto
   and is entitled to lease out the same.

B. The Lessee is a Non-Banking Financial Company desirous of taking on
   lease the said property for use as its corporate office.

C. The Lessor has agreed to lease and the Lessee has agreed to take on
   lease the said property on the terms and conditions set out herein.

NOW THEREFORE, in consideration of the rent and covenants herein, the
Parties agree as follows:

================================================================================
                ARTICLE 1 - LEASED PREMISES
================================================================================

1.1 DESCRIPTION OF PREMISES

    The Lessor hereby leases to the Lessee and the Lessee hereby takes on
    lease from the Lessor the following premises:

    Unit Numbers:           501 and 502
    Floor:                  5th Floor
    Building Name:          Peninsula Business Park
    Address:                Senapati Bapat Marg, Lower Parel
                            Mumbai - 400013, Maharashtra
    Carpet Area:            5,200 sq. ft. (approximately)
    Built-up Area:          6,500 sq. ft. (approximately)

    (hereinafter referred to as the "Leased Premises")

1.2 FIXTURES AND FITTINGS

    The Leased Premises shall include:
    (a) Centralized air-conditioning
    (b) Common area maintenance
    (c) Lift access
    (d) 24x7 security
    (e) DG power backup
    (f) Firefighting systems
    (g) Parking spaces (4 car + 10 two-wheelers)

================================================================================
                    ARTICLE 2 - TERM
================================================================================

2.1 INITIAL TERM

    The lease shall be for an initial term of 5 (Five) years commencing from
    April 1, 2019 and ending on March 31, 2024 ("Initial Term").

2.2 RENEWAL

    The Lessee shall have the option to renew this lease for a further term
    of 5 (Five) years upon:
    (a) Giving written notice of renewal at least 6 months before expiry
    (b) Agreement on revised rent and other terms
    (c) Execution of a fresh lease agreement

    The lease has been renewed for a second term of 5 years from April 1,
    2024 to March 31, 2029.

2.3 LOCK-IN PERIOD

    The initial lock-in period is 3 (Three) years from the commencement date.
    During this period, neither party shall terminate this Agreement except
    as provided in Article 10.

3.2 ESCALATION

    The rent shall escalate by 5% per annum on each anniversary of the
    commencement date.

3.3 PAYMENT

    (a) Rent shall be payable monthly in advance on or before the 5th day
        of each month.

    (b) Payment shall be made by cheque/NEFT/RTGS to the Lessor's bank account:
        Bank: HDFC Bank
        Account No: [XXXX]
        IFSC: HDFC0000XXX

    (c) The Lessee shall deduct TDS at applicable rates and provide TDS
        certificate quarterly.

3.4 LATE PAYMENT

    If rent is not paid within 7 days of the due date, the Lessee shall pay
    interest at 18% per annum for the period of delay.

================================================================================
                  ARTICLE 4 - SECURITY DEPOSIT
================================================================================

4.1 AMOUNT

    The Lessee has paid to the Lessor a security deposit of Rs. 72,00,000
    (Rupees Seventy Two Lakhs Only), equivalent to 6 months' rent.

4.2 INTEREST

    The security deposit shall be interest-free.

4.3 REFUND

    The security deposit shall be refunded within 30 days of vacating the
    premises, after deducting:
    (a) Any unpaid rent or charges
    (b) Cost of repairs for damage beyond normal wear and tear
    (c) Any other outstanding dues

4.4 ADJUSTMENT

    Upon rent escalation, the security deposit shall be proportionately
    adjusted to maintain 6 months' rent equivalent.

================================================================================
              ARTICLE 5 - MAINTENANCE CHARGES
================================================================================

5.1 COMMON AREA MAINTENANCE (CAM)

    CAM charges: Rs. 25 per sq. ft. of built-up area per month
    Current CAM: Rs. 1,62,500 per month

    CAM includes:
    - Common area cleaning and maintenance
    - Security services
    - Lift maintenance
    - Common utilities

5.2 ESCALATION

    CAM charges may be escalated annually based on actual cost increase,
    subject to a maximum of 10% per annum.

5.3 ELECTRICITY

    Electricity charges for the Leased Premises shall be paid by the Lessee
    directly to the electricity provider or as per sub-meter readings.

================================================================================
                ARTICLE 6 - USE OF PREMISES
================================================================================

6.1 PERMITTED USE

    The Leased Premises shall be used solely for:
    (a) Corporate office of the Lessee
    (b) Conduct of NBFC business activities
    (c) Any other lawful purpose with prior consent of Lessor

6.2 PROHIBITED USE

    The Lessee shall not:
    (a) Use the premises for any unlawful purpose
    (b) Cause nuisance to other occupants
    (c) Store hazardous materials
    (d) Make structural alterations without consent
    (e) Sub-let or assign without consent

================================================================================
             ARTICLE 7 - LESSEE'S OBLIGATIONS
================================================================================

7.1 PAYMENT

    (a) Pay rent and other charges on time
    (b) Pay all utility bills
    (c) Pay property tax if billed separately

7.2 MAINTENANCE

    (a) Keep the premises in good condition
    (b) Carry out day-to-day repairs
    (c) Maintain interior fitouts installed by Lessee

7.3 COMPLIANCE

    (a) Comply with all applicable laws
    (b) Obtain necessary licenses (Shop & Establishment, etc.)
    (c) Not violate building rules

7.4 INSURANCE

    (a) Insure contents against fire and theft
    (b) Maintain public liability insurance

7.5 ACCESS

    Allow Lessor access for inspection with reasonable prior notice

================================================================================
             ARTICLE 8 - LESSOR'S OBLIGATIONS
================================================================================

8.1 QUIET ENJOYMENT

    Ensure the Lessee's peaceful enjoyment of the premises without
    interference.

8.2 STRUCTURAL MAINTENANCE

    (a) Maintain the structure of the building
    (b) Maintain common areas
    (c) Repair structural defects

8.3 STATUTORY COMPLIANCE

    (a) Maintain building completion/occupancy certificate
    (b) Comply with fire safety norms
    (c) Maintain building insurance

8.4 SERVICES

    Ensure continuous provision of:
    (a) Water supply
    (b) Lift service
    (c) DG backup (as per building norms)
    (d) Security at building level

================================================================================
          ARTICLE 9 - ALTERATIONS AND IMPROVEMENTS
================================================================================

9.1 INTERIOR FITOUTS

    The Lessee may, at its own cost:
    (a) Install interior partitions
    (b) Install false ceiling and flooring
    (c) Install electrical and networking
    (d) Install CCTV and access control

9.2 STRUCTURAL CHANGES

    No structural changes shall be made without the Lessor's prior written
    consent.

9.3 REMOVAL

    Upon termination:
    (a) Lessee may remove all movable fitouts
    (b) Premises shall be restored to original condition (fair wear and
        tear excepted)
    (c) Any permanent improvement shall remain with the premises

================================================================================
                 ARTICLE 10 - TERMINATION
================================================================================

10.1 TERMINATION BY LESSEE

     After the lock-in period, the Lessee may terminate by giving 6 months
     written notice.

10.2 TERMINATION BY LESSOR

     The Lessor may terminate:
     (a) After lock-in period with 6 months notice
     (b) Immediately if:
         - Rent is unpaid for 3 consecutive months
         - Lessee breaches material terms
         - Lessee becomes insolvent
         - Premises used for illegal purpose

10.3 CONSEQUENCES OF TERMINATION

     Upon termination:
     (a) Lessee shall vacate within 30 days
     (b) Lessee shall remove all belongings
     (c) Lessee shall clear all dues
     (d) Lessor shall refund security deposit (after deductions)
