================================================================================

This Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on this 1st day of
July, 2018 ("Effective Date")

BY AND BETWEEN:

SIPCOT INDUSTRIAL GROWTH CENTRE, a body corporate established under the State
Industries Promotion Corporation of Tamil Nadu Act, having its registered office
at 19-A, Rukmini Lakshmipathi Road, Egmore, Chennai - 600008
(hereinafter referred to as the "Lessor")

AND

PRECISION ENGINEERING WORKS PRIVATE LIMITED, a company incorporated under the
Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at Plot No. 45, SIPCOT
Industrial Estate, Hosur - 635126, Tamil Nadu (CIN: U28999TN2018PTC125847)
(hereinafter referred to as the "Lessee")

(The Lessor and the Lessee are hereinafter individually referred to as a "Party"
and collectively as the "Parties")

================================================================================

RECITALS

WHEREAS:

A. The Lessor is the owner of industrial plots in SIPCOT Industrial Estate,
   Hosur, Krishnagiri District, Tamil Nadu;

B. The Lessee is engaged in the business of precision engineering and
   manufacturing and requires industrial premises for its operations;

C. The Lessor has agreed to lease the Demised Premises to the Lessee for
   industrial use.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein and
the rent to be paid by the Lessee, the Parties agree as follows:

================================================================================

ARTICLE 1: DEMISED PREMISES

1.1 DESCRIPTION

    The Lessor hereby leases to the Lessee the following premises:

    Plot No.        : 45
    Location        : SIPCOT Industrial Estate, Hosur - 635126
    District        : Krishnagiri
    State           : Tamil Nadu
    Total Area      : 10,000 square feet
    Covered Area    : 8,000 square feet
    Open Area       : 2,000 square feet

1.2 BOUNDARIES

    North           : Plot No. 44
    South           : Internal Road
    East            : Plot No. 46
    West            : Main Road

1.3 SCHEDULE OF PREMISES

    The Demised Premises consists of:
    - Factory building: 6,000 sq. ft.
    - Office space: 1,500 sq. ft.
    - Store room: 500 sq. ft.
    - Parking area: 2,000 sq. ft.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 2: TERM

2.1 LEASE TERM

    The lease shall be for a period of 99 (Ninety-Nine) years commencing from
    1st July, 2018 and ending on 30th June, 2117.

2.2 RENEWAL

    The lease may be renewed upon expiry by mutual agreement, subject to the
    prevailing terms and conditions of SIPCOT.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 3: RENT AND DEPOSITS

3.1 ANNUAL LEASE RENT

    The Lessee shall pay to the Lessor an annual lease rent as follows:

    Initial Period (Years 1-10)  : Rs. 50,000/- per annum
    Second Period (Years 11-20)  : Rs. 60,000/- per annum
    Third Period (Years 21-30)   : Rs. 75,000/- per annum
    Subsequent Periods           : As revised by SIPCOT

3.2 PAYMENT SCHEDULE

    The annual rent shall be payable in advance on or before April 1st of each
    financial year.

3.3 SECURITY DEPOSIT

    The Lessee has paid a refundable security deposit of Rs. 5,00,000/- (Rupees
    Five Lakhs Only) upon execution of this Agreement.

3.4 LATE PAYMENT

    Late payment of rent shall attract interest at 12% per annum.

3.5 COMMON AREA MAINTENANCE

    The Lessee shall pay common area maintenance charges as determined by SIPCOT
    from time to time (currently Rs. 1/- per sq. ft. per month).

================================================================================

ARTICLE 4: PERMITTED USE

4.1 USE OF PREMISES

    The Demised Premises shall be used only for:
    - Precision engineering and manufacturing
    - CNC machining operations
    - Related industrial activities

4.2 PROHIBITED USES

    The Lessee shall not use the premises for:
    - Residential purposes
    - Storage of hazardous materials (except as permitted)
    - Activities prohibited by law or SIPCOT regulations
    - Sub-leasing without prior approval

================================================================================

ARTICLE 5: LESSEE'S OBLIGATIONS

5.1 The Lessee shall:

    (a) Pay rent and other charges on time;
    (b) Use the premises only for the permitted purpose;
    (c) Comply with all applicable laws and SIPCOT regulations;
    (d) Obtain all necessary licenses and permits;
    (e) Maintain the premises in good condition;
    (f) Not make structural alterations without prior approval;
    (g) Keep the premises insured against fire and other risks;
    (h) Allow Lessor's representatives to inspect the premises;
    (i) Comply with environmental and pollution control norms;
    (j) Commence commercial production within 2 years;
    (k) Maintain employment as committed in the project report.

5.2 CONSTRUCTION

    The Lessee may construct buildings on the plot subject to:
    - Obtaining prior approval from SIPCOT
    - Compliance with building regulations
    - Floor Area Ratio (FAR) restrictions
    - Setback requirements

================================================================================

ARTICLE 6: LESSOR'S OBLIGATIONS

6.1 The Lessor shall:

    (a) Provide peaceful possession of the Demised Premises;
    (b) Maintain common areas and infrastructure;
    (c) Provide power, water, and road connectivity;
    (d) Not disturb the Lessee's possession during the lease term;
    (e) Provide NOC for obtaining licenses and permits.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 7: UTILITIES

7.1 ELECTRICITY

    - Connection: The Lessee shall obtain power connection from TANGEDCO
    - Sanctioned Load: 250 KVA
    - Charges: To be paid directly to TANGEDCO

7.2 WATER

    - Connection: SIPCOT industrial water supply
    - Allocation: 10,000 liters per day
    - Charges: As per SIPCOT tariff

7.3 OTHER UTILITIES

    - Effluent disposal: Through SIPCOT CETP
    - Solid waste: Lessee's responsibility

================================================================================

ARTICLE 8: INSURANCE

8.1 The Lessee shall maintain the following insurance:

    (a) Property Insurance: Covering buildings, machinery, and stock
    (b) Public Liability Insurance: Minimum Rs. 1 Crore
    (c) Workers' Compensation Insurance: As per law

8.2 The Lessor shall be named as an additional insured in the property
    insurance policy.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 9: TERMINATION

9.1 TERMINATION BY LESSOR

    The Lessor may terminate this Agreement if the Lessee:

    (a) Fails to pay rent for 3 consecutive months;
    (b) Uses the premises for unauthorized purposes;
    (c) Commits material breach of the Agreement;
    (d) Fails to commence production within 2 years;
    (e) Becomes insolvent or is wound up.

9.2 TERMINATION BY LESSEE

    The Lessee may surrender the premises by giving 6 months' notice, subject
    to forfeiture of security deposit.

9.3 CONSEQUENCES OF TERMINATION

    Upon termination:
    (a) The Lessee shall vacate the premises within 30 days;
    (b) Structures constructed by Lessee shall vest in Lessor;
    (c) Security deposit may be forfeited (at Lessor's discretion).

================================================================================

ARTICLE 10: ASSIGNMENT AND SUB-LEASE

10.1 The Lessee shall not assign, transfer, or sub-lease the Demised Premises
     or any part thereof without prior written consent of the Lessor.

10.2 Transfer of controlling interest in the Lessee company shall be deemed
     an assignment requiring Lessor's consent.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 11: DEFAULT AND REMEDIES

11.1 Upon default by the Lessee, the Lessor may:

     (a) Issue a notice requiring cure within 30 days;
     (b) Terminate the Agreement if default is not cured;
     (c) Re-enter and take possession of the premises;
     (d) Recover damages for any loss suffered.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 12: INDEMNITY

12.1 The Lessee shall indemnify and hold harmless the Lessor against any
     claims, damages, or liabilities arising from:

     (a) The Lessee's use of the premises;
     (b) Breach of this Agreement by the Lessee;
     (c) Negligence or misconduct of the Lessee;
     (d) Violation of any law by the Lessee.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 13: GENERAL PROVISIONS

13.1 GOVERNING LAW

     This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.

13.2 DISPUTE RESOLUTION

     Any dispute shall be resolved through arbitration in Chennai under the
     Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

13.3 REGISTRATION

     This Agreement shall be registered with the Sub-Registrar's office at
     Hosur within 4 months of execution.

13.4 ENTIRE AGREEMENT

     This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties.

13.5 AMENDMENT

     This Agreement may be amended only in writing signed by both Parties.

13.6 NOTICES

     All notices shall be sent to the addresses specified herein.

================================================================================
