                    APPOINTMENT LETTER - MANAGING DIRECTOR

================================================================================

Date: 15th July, 2018

To,
Mr. Vikram Rajan
No. 12, Anna Nagar East
Chennai - 600102
Tamil Nadu

Dear Mr. Vikram Rajan,

Sub: Appointment as Managing Director

We are pleased to inform you that the Board of Directors of Precision
Engineering Works Private Limited ("Company") in its meeting held on 15th July,
2018 has approved your appointment as Managing Director of the Company with
effect from 15th July, 2018.

The terms and conditions of your appointment are as follows:

1. DESIGNATION AND TERM

   (a) Designation: Managing Director
   (b) Term: Five (5) years from 15th July, 2018 to 14th July, 2023
   (c) The term may be renewed by the Board subject to applicable laws

2. DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

   You shall:
   (a) Manage the day-to-day affairs of the Company;
   (b) Implement the decisions of the Board;
   (c) Develop and execute business strategy;
   (d) Oversee manufacturing operations and quality control;
   (e) Lead technology and product development;
   (f) Build and maintain customer relationships;
   (g) Represent the Company before third parties;
   (h) Perform such other duties as the Board may assign.

3. REMUNERATION

   (a) Basic Salary: Rs. 75,000/- per month
   (b) Allowances: Rs. 50,000/- per month
   (c) Perquisites: Rs. 25,000/- per month
   (d) Total Monthly Remuneration: Rs. 1,50,000/- per month
   (e) Annual Remuneration: Rs. 18,00,000/- per annum

4. OTHER BENEFITS

   (a) Company-maintained vehicle or car allowance;
   (b) Mobile phone and internet expenses;
   (c) Medical insurance for self and family;
   (d) Annual leave of 30 days;
   (e) Reimbursement of business expenses;
   (f) Director & Officer (D&O) liability insurance.

5. STATUTORY COMPLIANCES

   You shall comply with all applicable provisions of the Companies Act, 2013
   and other relevant laws. You shall:

   (a) Obtain Director Identification Number (DIN);
   (b) Give consent in Form DIR-2;
   (c) File declaration of non-disqualification;
   (d) Disclose interest in other companies;
   (e) Attend Board meetings regularly.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

   You shall maintain confidentiality of all Company information during and
   after your term as Director.

7. NON-COMPETE

   During your term and for one year thereafter, you shall not engage in any
   business competitive with the Company.

8. TERMINATION

   Your appointment may be terminated:
   (a) By giving three months' notice by either party;
   (b) By the Board for cause (misconduct, breach of duty, etc.);
   (c) Upon disqualification under the Companies Act.

9. GOVERNING LAW

   This appointment shall be governed by the laws of India and the provisions
   of the Companies Act, 2013.

Please sign and return the duplicate copy of this letter as acceptance of the
appointment.
