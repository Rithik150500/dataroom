The Chairman informed the Board that the Company was incorporated on 15th July,

RESOLVED THAT the appointment of the First Directors be confirmed.

SIPCOT Industrial Estate, Hosur - 635126, Tamil Nadu.

ITEM NO. 5: APPOINTMENT OF MANAGING DIRECTOR

RESOLVED THAT Mr. Vikram Rajan (DIN: 08567234) be and is hereby appointed as

ITEM NO. 6: APPOINTMENT OF WHOLE-TIME DIRECTOR

RESOLVED THAT Mr. Anand Kumar (DIN: 08567235) be and is hereby appointed as the

RESOLVED THAT M/s. Kumar & Associates, Chartered Accountants, Chennai

ITEM NO. 8: COMMON SEAL

RESOLVED THAT the Common Seal, an impression of which is affixed in the margin

RESOLVED THAT Mr. Vikram Rajan and Mr. Anand Kumar, Directors, be and are hereby

                    MINUTES OF THE MEETING OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS
                    HELD ON 25TH SEPTEMBER, 2023

================================================================================

Minutes of the Meeting of the Board of Directors of Precision Engineering Works
Private Limited held on Monday, the 25th day of September, 2023 at 4:00 PM at
the Registered Office at Plot No. 45, SIPCOT Industrial Estate, Hosur - 635126,
Tamil Nadu.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

PRESENT:

1. Mr. Vikram Rajan     - Managing Director (In the Chair)
2. Mr. Anand Kumar      - Whole-time Director

IN ATTENDANCE:
1. Mr. R. Venkatesh     - Chief Financial Officer
2. Ms. S. Preethi       - Company Secretary

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

QUORUM:

The requisite quorum being present, the Chairman called the meeting to order.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ITEM NO. 1: CONFIRMATION OF MINUTES

RESOLVED THAT the Minutes of the previous Board Meeting held on 28th June, 2023
be and are hereby confirmed.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ITEM NO. 2: UNAUDITED FINANCIAL RESULTS FOR Q1 FY 2023-24

The CFO presented the unaudited financial results for the quarter ended
30th June, 2023.

Highlights:
- Revenue: Rs. 1,05,00,000
- EBITDA: Rs. 15,75,000
- PAT: Rs. 8,50,000

RESOLVED THAT the unaudited financial results for Q1 FY 2023-24 be and are
hereby approved and taken on record.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ITEM NO. 3: ADOPTION OF AUDITED FINANCIAL STATEMENTS FOR FY 2022-23

The Board reviewed the Audited Financial Statements for the year ended
31st March, 2023 along with the Auditor's Report thereon.

RESOLVED THAT the Audited Financial Statements comprising the Balance Sheet,
Statement of Profit and Loss, Cash Flow Statement, and Notes thereto for the
year ended 31st March, 2023 be and are hereby approved and adopted.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ITEM NO. 4: DIRECTORS' REPORT

RESOLVED THAT the Directors' Report for the year ended 31st March, 2023 be
and is hereby approved.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ITEM NO. 5: ANNUAL GENERAL MEETING

RESOLVED THAT the Fifth Annual General Meeting of the Company be convened on
Saturday, the 28th day of September, 2023 at 11:00 AM at the Registered Office.

RESOLVED FURTHER THAT the Notice of AGM along with the Annual Report be sent
to all members at least 21 clear days before the date of the meeting.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ITEM NO. 6: RE-APPOINTMENT OF AUDITORS

RESOLVED THAT, subject to the approval of the members at the AGM, M/s. Kumar &
Associates, Chartered Accountants (Firm Reg. No. 012345S) be and are hereby
re-appointed as the Statutory Auditors of the Company for a second term of
5 consecutive years from the conclusion of the ensuing AGM till the conclusion
of the 10th AGM.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ITEM NO. 7: DIVIDEND

After considering the financial position of the Company, the Board decided not
to recommend any dividend for the year ended 31st March, 2023 and to retain the
profits for business expansion.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ITEM NO. 8: CAPITAL EXPENDITURE

RESOLVED THAT the capital expenditure of Rs. 50,00,000 for the purchase of a
new 5-Axis CNC Machining Center be and is hereby approved.

RESOLVED FURTHER THAT Mr. Vikram Rajan be authorized to negotiate terms and
execute all documents for the said purchase.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ITEM NO. 9: RENEWAL OF WORKING CAPITAL FACILITY

RESOLVED THAT the renewal of the working capital facility of Rs. 1,50,00,000
with HDFC Bank Ltd. be and is hereby approved.

RESOLVED FURTHER THAT Mr. Vikram Rajan and Mr. Anand Kumar be authorized to
execute all documents including hypothecation agreement and other security
documents.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ITEM NO. 10: RELATED PARTY TRANSACTIONS

The Company Secretary presented the details of related party transactions
entered during the quarter.

RESOLVED THAT the related party transactions for the quarter ended
30th June, 2023 be and are hereby ratified.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ITEM NO. 11: COMPLIANCE CERTIFICATE

The Company Secretary presented the Compliance Certificate confirming compliance
with all applicable laws and regulations.

RESOLVED THAT the Compliance Certificate be taken on record.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ITEM NO. 12: DATE OF NEXT MEETING

RESOLVED THAT the next Board Meeting be held on 28th December, 2023.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

VOTE OF THANKS

There being no other business, the meeting concluded with a vote of thanks to
the Chair.
