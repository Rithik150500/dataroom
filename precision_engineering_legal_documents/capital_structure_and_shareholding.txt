                    5. SHARE TRANSFER RESTRICTIONS

================================================================================

As per Shareholders Agreement:

5.1 Promoter Lock-in
    - 100% promoter shares locked until IndoGrowth exit
    - Exception: Inter-family transfers

5.2 Right of First Refusal (ROFR)
    - All shareholders have ROFR on any proposed transfer
    - 30-day notice period
    - Price at Fair Market Value by independent valuer

5.3 Tag-Along Rights
    - Minority shareholders can tag along on promoter sale
    - Pro-rata participation

5.4 Drag-Along Rights
    - 75% shareholders can drag remaining shareholders
    - Only on full company sale
    - Floor price: Higher of (i) 20x TTM PAT or (ii) 2x invested capital

5.5 Pre-emptive Rights
    - All shareholders have pro-rata right on new issuances
    - 15-day exercise period
