("ESOP 2019" or "Plan")

================================================================================

ARTICLE 1: INTRODUCTION AND OBJECTIVES

1.1 INTRODUCTION

This Employee Stock Option Plan 2019 ("Plan") has been formulated and adopted
by Precision Engineering Works Private Limited ("Company") pursuant to the
resolution passed by the Board of Directors on April 1, 2019 and approved by
the shareholders in the Extra-ordinary General Meeting held on April 15, 2019.

1.2 OBJECTIVES

The objectives of this Plan are to:

(a) Attract and retain talented employees in the competitive precision
    engineering and manufacturing industry;
(b) Motivate employees to contribute to the growth and profitability of
    the Company;
(c) Align the interests of employees with those of the shareholders;
(d) Reward employees for their contribution to the Company's success;
(e) Create a sense of ownership and participation among employees.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 2: DEFINITIONS

2.1 "Board" means the Board of Directors of the Company.

2.2 "Compensation Committee" means the committee constituted by the Board to
    administer this Plan.

2.3 "Employee" means any person who is in employment of the Company on a
    permanent basis, working in India or outside India.

2.4 "Exercise Period" means the period during which the Grantee can exercise
    the vested Options.

2.5 "Exercise Price" means the price payable by the Grantee to acquire one
    Equity Share upon exercise of an Option.

2.6 "Grant" means the allocation of Options to an Eligible Employee.

2.7 "Grant Date" means the date on which Options are granted to an Eligible
    Employee.

2.8 "Grantee" means an Eligible Employee to whom Options have been granted.

2.9 "Option" means the right, but not an obligation, granted to an Eligible
    Employee to apply for Equity Shares at the Exercise Price.

2.10 "Vesting" means the process by which the Grantee acquires the right to
     exercise the Options.

2.11 "Vesting Period" means the period during which the Options vest.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 3: ADMINISTRATION

3.1 COMPENSATION COMMITTEE

The Plan shall be administered by the Compensation Committee comprising:

(a) Vikram Rajan - Managing Director (Chairman)
(b) Anand Kumar - Whole-time Director
(c) Independent Advisor (if appointed)

3.2 POWERS OF THE COMMITTEE

The Compensation Committee shall have the following powers:

(a) To determine the Eligible Employees to whom Options may be granted;
(b) To determine the number of Options to be granted to each Grantee;
(c) To determine the Exercise Price for each Grant;
(d) To determine the Vesting Period and Exercise Period;
(e) To prescribe forms and procedures for Grant, Vesting, and Exercise;
(f) To interpret the provisions of this Plan;
(g) To modify or amend the Plan subject to shareholder approval.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 4: ELIGIBILITY

4.1 ELIGIBLE EMPLOYEES

The following persons shall be eligible to participate in this Plan:

(a) Permanent employees of the Company working in India;
(b) Directors of the Company (whether whole-time or not);
(c) Employees of subsidiary companies (if any);

4.2 EXCLUSIONS

The following persons shall NOT be eligible:

(a) An employee who is a Promoter or belongs to the Promoter Group;
(b) A Director who directly or indirectly holds more than 10% of the
    outstanding Equity Shares;
(c) Consultants and contractors;
(d) Employees on probation;
(e) Employees who have resigned or are under notice period.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 5: SHARES RESERVED

5.1 ESOP POOL

The Company has reserved 25,000 (Twenty-Five Thousand) Equity Shares of Rs. 10/-
each for issuance under this Plan, representing 10% of the paid-up Equity Share
Capital as on the date of approval of the Plan.

5.2 UNUTILIZED OPTIONS

Options that are forfeited, cancelled, or lapsed shall be available for
re-grant under the Plan.

5.3 INCREASE IN POOL

The ESOP Pool may be increased by the Board with the approval of shareholders
in a General Meeting.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 6: GRANT OF OPTIONS

6.1 GRANT

The Compensation Committee may, at its discretion, grant Options to Eligible
Employees from time to time.

6.2 GRANT LETTER

Each Grant shall be evidenced by a Grant Letter which shall specify:

(a) Name of the Grantee;
(b) Number of Options granted;
(c) Grant Date;
(d) Exercise Price;
(e) Vesting Schedule;
(f) Exercise Period;
(g) Terms and conditions.

6.3 ACCEPTANCE

The Grantee shall accept the Grant by signing and returning the Grant Letter
within 30 days of receipt.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 7: EXERCISE PRICE

7.1 DETERMINATION

The Exercise Price shall be determined by the Compensation Committee and may be:

(a) At face value (Rs. 10/- per share);
(b) At fair market value as determined by an independent valuer;
(c) At a discount to fair market value;
(d) At a premium to fair market value.

7.2 CURRENT EXERCISE PRICE

For Grants made in 2019-2020, the Exercise Price shall be Rs. 10/- (Rupees Ten
Only) per Equity Share, being the face value of the shares.

7.3 VARIATION

Different Grants may have different Exercise Prices as determined by the
Compensation Committee.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 8: VESTING

8.1 VESTING SCHEDULE

Options shall vest as per the following schedule:

Years of Service    | Cumulative Vesting | Options Vested
After Grant Date    | Percentage         | (For 1000 Options)
--------------------+--------------------+-------------------
1 year (Cliff)      | 25%                | 250
2 years             | 50%                | 500
3 years             | 75%                | 750
4 years             | 100%               | 1000

8.2 VESTING CONDITIONS

Vesting shall be subject to:

(a) Continued employment with the Company;
(b) Satisfactory performance ratings;
(c) No disciplinary action pending or served;
(d) Compliance with Company policies.

8.3 ACCELERATED VESTING

Vesting may be accelerated in the following cases:

(a) Death or permanent disability of the Grantee;
(b) Change of Control of the Company;
(c) Any other circumstance as determined by the Compensation Committee.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 9: EXERCISE OF OPTIONS

9.1 EXERCISE PERIOD

Vested Options may be exercised during the Exercise Period, which shall be:

(a) Starting from the Vesting Date; and
(b) Ending on the 5th anniversary of the Vesting Date.

9.2 EXERCISE PROCESS

To exercise Options, the Grantee shall:

(a) Submit an Exercise Application to the Company;
(b) Specify the number of Options to be exercised;
(c) Pay the aggregate Exercise Price;
(d) Provide such other documents as may be required.

9.3 MINIMUM EXERCISE

The minimum number of Options that can be exercised at a time is 100.

9.4 ALLOTMENT

Upon valid exercise, the Company shall allot Equity Shares to the Grantee
within 30 days and issue Share Certificates within 60 days.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 10: TERMINATION OF EMPLOYMENT

10.1 VOLUNTARY RESIGNATION

If a Grantee voluntarily resigns:

(a) Unvested Options shall immediately lapse;
(b) Vested but unexercised Options must be exercised within 30 days of
    resignation, failing which they shall lapse.

10.2 TERMINATION FOR CAUSE

If a Grantee is terminated for cause (misconduct, fraud, etc.):

(a) All Options (vested and unvested) shall immediately lapse;
(b) No exercise shall be permitted.

10.3 TERMINATION WITHOUT CAUSE

If a Grantee is terminated without cause:

(a) Unvested Options shall lapse;
(b) Vested Options must be exercised within 90 days of termination.

10.4 RETIREMENT

Upon retirement as per Company policy:

(a) Unvested Options may vest on a pro-rata basis at the discretion of the
    Compensation Committee;
(b) Vested Options must be exercised within 180 days of retirement.

10.5 DEATH OR DISABILITY

In case of death or permanent disability:

(a) All unvested Options shall immediately vest;
(b) The legal heirs or Grantee (in case of disability) may exercise all
    Options within 12 months.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 11: NON-TRANSFERABILITY

11.1 Options granted under this Plan are personal to the Grantee and shall not
     be transferable.

11.2 Options cannot be pledged, hypothecated, or encumbered.

11.3 Any attempted transfer shall render the Options null and void.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 12: LOCK-IN PERIOD

12.1 There shall be no lock-in period for shares allotted pursuant to exercise
     of Options.

12.2 Shares once allotted shall be subject to the transfer restrictions as per
     the Articles of Association.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 13: TAX IMPLICATIONS

13.1 The Grantee shall be solely responsible for all tax implications arising
     from the Grant, Vesting, Exercise, or sale of shares.

13.2 The Company shall deduct Tax at Source (TDS) as per applicable laws.

13.3 The Grantee is advised to consult a tax advisor for tax planning.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 14: AMENDMENT AND TERMINATION

14.1 The Board may amend the Plan with shareholder approval if the amendment:

(a) Increases the total number of shares reserved;
(b) Changes the eligibility criteria;
(c) Extends the Exercise Period;
(d) Reduces the Exercise Price.

14.2 The Board may terminate the Plan at any time, provided that existing
     Grants shall continue to be governed by the Plan.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 15: CORPORATE ACTIONS

15.1 BONUS SHARES

If the Company issues bonus shares, the number of Options and Exercise Price
shall be adjusted proportionately.

15.2 STOCK SPLIT / CONSOLIDATION

If the Company undertakes a stock split or consolidation, the number of Options
and Exercise Price shall be adjusted accordingly.

15.3 RIGHTS ISSUE

In case of a rights issue, the Compensation Committee may make appropriate
adjustments.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 16: GENERAL PROVISIONS

16.1 NO RIGHT TO EMPLOYMENT

Nothing in this Plan shall confer upon any Grantee the right to continue in
employment or affect the Company's right to terminate employment.

16.2 NO SHAREHOLDER RIGHTS

A Grantee shall have no rights as a shareholder until shares are actually
allotted upon exercise.

16.3 GOVERNING LAW

This Plan shall be governed by the laws of India.

16.4 DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any disputes shall be resolved through arbitration in Chennai.
