RECITALS

WHEREAS:

A. The Buyer is engaged in the business of precision engineering, CNC machining,
   and manufacturing of precision components;

B. The Vendor is engaged in the business of supplying [raw materials / tools /
   equipment / services] to industrial customers;

C. The Buyer desires to purchase goods and/or services from the Vendor, and the
   Vendor desires to supply such goods and/or services to the Buyer;

D. The Parties wish to establish a framework governing such supply.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the
Parties agree as follows:

================================================================================

ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

1.1 "Goods" means the materials, products, tools, equipment, or other items to
    be supplied by the Vendor under this Agreement.

1.2 "Services" means the services to be provided by the Vendor under this
    Agreement.

1.3 "Purchase Order" or "PO" means an order issued by the Buyer specifying the
    Goods or Services, quantities, prices, and delivery requirements.

1.4 "Specifications" means the technical requirements, standards, and quality
    criteria for the Goods or Services.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 2: SCOPE OF SUPPLY

2.1 GOODS AND SERVICES

    The Vendor shall supply the following categories of Goods/Services:

    [ ] Raw Materials (steel, aluminum, brass, etc.)
    [ ] Cutting Tools and Inserts
    [ ] Measuring Instruments and Gauges
    [ ] Machine Spares and Consumables
    [ ] Heat Treatment Services
    [ ] Surface Treatment Services
    [ ] Sub-contracted Machining Services
    [ ] Other: [_______________]

2.2 PURCHASE ORDERS

    (a) All purchases shall be made through Purchase Orders;
    (b) Purchase Orders shall specify item descriptions, quantities, prices,
        delivery dates, and other terms;
    (c) The Vendor shall acknowledge receipt of Purchase Order within [2]
        business days;
    (d) Failure to acknowledge shall be deemed acceptance.

2.3 ORDER MODIFICATIONS

    The Buyer may modify or cancel a Purchase Order, in which case:

    (a) If Goods are not yet manufactured: No cancellation charges;
    (b) If Goods are in progress: Buyer pays for work-in-progress;
    (c) If Goods are ready: Buyer accepts delivery and pays full price.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 3: PRICING AND PAYMENT

3.1 PRICING

    (a) Prices shall be as specified in the Purchase Order or agreed price list;
    (b) Prices are in Indian Rupees unless otherwise specified;
    (c) Prices include packing and delivery to Buyer's facility (DDP);
    (d) Prices exclude GST, which will be charged at applicable rates;
    (e) Prices shall be valid for [6] months unless otherwise agreed.

3.2 INVOICING

    (a) The Vendor shall submit invoices along with delivery;
    (b) Invoices must reference the Purchase Order number;
    (c) Invoices shall comply with GST requirements;
    (d) Tax invoices must include: GSTIN, HSN/SAC code, taxable value, tax rate.

3.3 PAYMENT TERMS

    (a) Payment terms: [30] days from date of invoice;
    (b) Payment shall be made by [NEFT/RTGS] to the Vendor's bank account;
    (c) The Buyer may set off amounts due from the Vendor against payments.

3.4 PRICE ADJUSTMENTS

    (a) Prices are firm for the validity period;
    (b) Price increases require [60] days' advance notice with justification;
    (c) The Buyer may terminate the Agreement if it does not accept price increase.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 4: DELIVERY

4.1 DELIVERY SCHEDULE

    (a) The Vendor shall deliver Goods as per the schedule in the Purchase Order;
    (b) Time is of the essence for delivery;
    (c) The Vendor shall notify the Buyer immediately of any anticipated delays.

4.2 DELIVERY TERMS

    (a) Delivery shall be DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) to Buyer's facility unless
        otherwise specified;
    (b) Risk and title shall pass to Buyer upon delivery and acceptance;
    (c) All Goods must be accompanied by delivery challan and test certificates.

4.3 PARTIAL DELIVERY

    Partial deliveries are allowed only with prior approval of the Buyer.

4.4 PACKING

    (a) Goods shall be packed suitable for transport and storage;
    (b) Packing shall protect Goods from damage, corrosion, and contamination;
    (c) Each package shall be clearly labeled with PO number, part number, and
        quantity.

4.5 LIQUIDATED DAMAGES

    For delays attributable to the Vendor, liquidated damages of [1%] per week
    of the order value shall apply, up to a maximum of [10%].

================================================================================

ARTICLE 5: QUALITY AND INSPECTION

5.1 QUALITY REQUIREMENTS

    (a) Goods shall conform to Specifications, drawings, and industry standards;
    (b) Goods shall be free from defects in material and workmanship;
    (c) The Vendor shall maintain appropriate quality management systems;
    (d) The Vendor shall provide material test certificates (MTC), certificates
        of conformance (CoC), and other documentation as required.

5.2 INCOMING INSPECTION

    (a) The Buyer shall inspect Goods upon receipt;
    (b) The Buyer may accept, reject, or accept with deviation;
    (c) Acceptance does not waive latent defects or warranty claims.

5.3 REJECTION

    (a) Rejected Goods shall be held for Vendor's disposition;
    (b) The Vendor shall arrange for collection or authorize disposal;
    (c) The Vendor shall replace rejected Goods at no additional cost;
    (d) Replacement shall be delivered within [7] days.

5.4 RIGHT TO AUDIT

    The Buyer reserves the right to audit the Vendor's facilities, processes,
    and quality systems upon reasonable notice.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 6: WARRANTIES

6.1 The Vendor warrants that:

    (a) Goods conform to Specifications and are fit for intended purpose;
    (b) Goods are free from defects in material and workmanship;
    (c) Goods are new and not refurbished (unless otherwise agreed);
    (d) Services are performed in a professional and workmanlike manner;
    (e) The Vendor has the right to sell the Goods.

6.2 WARRANTY PERIOD

    Warranties shall be valid for [12] months from delivery or [6] months from
    first use, whichever is earlier.

6.3 WARRANTY REMEDIES

    For warranty claims, the Vendor shall:

    (a) Replace defective Goods at no cost;
    (b) Reimburse the Buyer for any costs incurred due to defective Goods;
    (c) Be liable for consequential damages if defective Goods cause damage
        to Buyer's products or equipment.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 7: CONFIDENTIALITY

7.1 The Vendor shall maintain confidentiality of:

    (a) Buyer's technical information and drawings;
    (b) Pricing and commercial terms;
    (c) Business information and plans.

7.2 The Vendor shall not supply identical Goods to Buyer's competitors using
    Buyer's Specifications without prior consent.

7.3 Confidentiality obligations survive termination for [5] years.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 8: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

8.1 The Vendor represents that Goods do not infringe any third-party
    intellectual property rights.

8.2 The Vendor shall indemnify the Buyer against any claims of infringement.

8.3 Tooling or patterns paid for by the Buyer shall be Buyer's property.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 9: COMPLIANCE

9.1 LEGAL COMPLIANCE

    The Vendor shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations, including:

    (a) Labor laws and fair employment practices;
    (b) Environmental laws and regulations;
    (c) Health and safety regulations;
    (d) Anti-corruption and anti-bribery laws;
    (e) Tax laws (GST, TDS, etc.).

9.2 REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

    Goods shall comply with applicable Indian standards (BIS) and international
    standards (ISO, ASTM, etc.) as specified.

9.3 DOCUMENTATION

    The Vendor shall maintain and provide upon request:

    (a) Material test certificates;
    (b) Certificates of conformance;
    (c) MSDS (Material Safety Data Sheets);
    (d) RoHS/REACH compliance certificates (if applicable).

================================================================================

ARTICLE 10: LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

10.1 INDEMNIFICATION

     The Vendor shall indemnify and hold harmless the Buyer against claims
     arising from:

     (a) Defective Goods causing injury or damage;
     (b) Infringement of third-party intellectual property;
     (c) Breach of this Agreement by the Vendor;
     (d) Negligence or misconduct of the Vendor.

10.2 INSURANCE

     The Vendor shall maintain adequate insurance including:

     (a) Product liability insurance;
     (b) Commercial general liability insurance;
     (c) Workers' compensation insurance.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 11: TERM AND TERMINATION

11.1 TERM

     This Agreement shall be effective from the Effective Date and shall
     continue for [2] years, automatically renewing for successive [1] year
     periods unless terminated.

11.2 TERMINATION FOR CONVENIENCE

     Either Party may terminate this Agreement by giving [90] days' written
     notice.

11.3 TERMINATION FOR CAUSE

     The Buyer may terminate this Agreement immediately upon:

     (a) Material breach not cured within [15] days of notice;
     (b) Repeated quality failures or delivery delays;
     (c) Insolvency or bankruptcy of the Vendor;
     (d) Change of control of the Vendor.

11.4 EFFECT OF TERMINATION

     Upon termination:

     (a) All outstanding Purchase Orders shall be fulfilled or cancelled;
     (b) The Vendor shall deliver all Goods in progress;
     (c) The Buyer shall pay for Goods delivered and accepted.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 12: GENERAL PROVISIONS

12.1 GOVERNING LAW

     This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.

12.2 DISPUTE RESOLUTION

     Any dispute shall be resolved through arbitration in Chennai under the
     Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

12.3 ENTIRE AGREEMENT

     This Agreement, together with Purchase Orders, constitutes the entire
     agreement.

12.4 AMENDMENT

     This Agreement may be amended only in writing signed by both Parties.

12.5 ASSIGNMENT

     The Vendor may not assign this Agreement without Buyer's written consent.

12.6 NOTICES

     All notices shall be in writing to the addresses specified herein.

12.7 INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR

     The Vendor is an independent contractor, not an employee or agent of the
     Buyer.
