RECITALS

WHEREAS:

A. The Service Provider is engaged in the business of precision engineering,
   CNC machining, manufacturing of precision components, and related services;

B. The Customer desires to engage the Service Provider for the provision of
   precision manufacturing services;

C. The Parties wish to establish a framework governing the provision of such
   services.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the
Parties agree as follows:

================================================================================

ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

1.1 "Deliverables" means the products, components, or work output to be
    delivered by the Service Provider under a Work Order.

1.2 "Intellectual Property" means all patents, copyrights, trademarks, trade
    secrets, designs, and other intellectual property rights.

1.3 "Services" means the precision manufacturing, machining, and related
    services provided by the Service Provider.

1.4 "Specifications" means the technical requirements, drawings, tolerances,
    and standards specified by the Customer for the Deliverables.

1.5 "Work Order" means an order issued by the Customer under this Agreement
    specifying the Services, Deliverables, pricing, and delivery schedule.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 2: SERVICES

2.1 SCOPE OF SERVICES

    The Service Provider shall provide the following Services:

    (a) CNC machining (turning, milling, grinding)
    (b) Precision component manufacturing
    (c) Tool and die making
    (d) Prototype development
    (e) Quality inspection and testing
    (f) Surface treatment and finishing
    (g) Assembly services
    (h) Other services as specified in Work Orders

2.2 WORK ORDERS

    (a) Each engagement shall be governed by a Work Order;
    (b) Work Orders shall specify: description of work, quantities,
        Specifications, pricing, delivery dates, and acceptance criteria;
    (c) Work Orders are subject to acceptance by the Service Provider;
    (d) Work Orders are incorporated by reference into this Agreement.

2.3 CUSTOMER RESPONSIBILITIES

    The Customer shall:

    (a) Provide complete and accurate Specifications and drawings;
    (b) Provide raw materials if specified (free-issue material);
    (c) Respond promptly to queries and requests for clarification;
    (d) Provide timely feedback and approvals;
    (e) Pay invoices as per agreed terms.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 3: QUALITY AND ACCEPTANCE

3.1 QUALITY STANDARDS

    The Service Provider shall:

    (a) Manufacture Deliverables in accordance with the Specifications;
    (b) Maintain ISO 9001:2015 quality management system;
    (c) Use calibrated measuring instruments traceable to national standards;
    (d) Perform in-process and final inspections;
    (e) Provide inspection reports and certificates as required.

3.2 FIRST ARTICLE INSPECTION

    For new parts, the Service Provider shall submit first article samples with
    First Article Inspection Report (FAIR) for Customer approval before
    commencing production.

3.3 ACCEPTANCE

    (a) The Customer shall inspect Deliverables within [10] business days of
        receipt;
    (b) The Customer shall notify the Service Provider of any non-conformances
        within the inspection period;
    (c) Deliverables shall be deemed accepted if not rejected within the
        inspection period.

3.4 REJECTION

    (a) Rejected Deliverables shall be returned to the Service Provider;
    (b) The Service Provider shall repair or replace rejected Deliverables at
        no additional cost;
    (c) Repair or replacement shall be completed within [15] business days.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 4: PRICING AND PAYMENT

4.1 PRICING

    (a) Prices for Services shall be as specified in Work Orders;
    (b) Prices are in Indian Rupees unless otherwise specified;
    (c) Prices exclude applicable taxes (GST, etc.);
    (d) Prices shall remain valid for the period specified in the quotation.

4.2 INVOICING

    (a) The Service Provider shall invoice upon delivery of Deliverables;
    (b) Invoices shall reference the Work Order number;
    (c) Invoices shall comply with GST requirements.

4.3 PAYMENT TERMS

    (a) Payment is due within [30] days from the date of invoice;
    (b) Late payments shall attract interest at [18%] per annum;
    (c) The Customer may not withhold payment due to disputes on other orders.

4.4 PRICE ADJUSTMENTS

    The Service Provider may adjust prices with [30] days' notice in case of:

    (a) Increase in raw material costs exceeding [10%];
    (b) Changes in Specifications requested by Customer;
    (c) Annual revision (not more than once per year).

================================================================================

ARTICLE 5: DELIVERY

5.1 DELIVERY SCHEDULE

    (a) The Service Provider shall deliver Deliverables as per the schedule in
        the Work Order;
    (b) Delivery dates are estimates unless expressly stated as firm;
    (c) The Service Provider shall notify the Customer of potential delays.

5.2 DELIVERY TERMS

    (a) Delivery shall be Ex-Works (EXW) Service Provider's facility unless
        otherwise specified;
    (b) Risk and title shall pass to Customer upon delivery;
    (c) Freight and insurance are to Customer's account unless otherwise agreed.

5.3 FORCE MAJEURE

    Neither Party shall be liable for delays due to events beyond reasonable
    control, including natural disasters, war, strikes, or government actions.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 6: WARRANTIES

6.1 SERVICE PROVIDER WARRANTIES

    The Service Provider warrants that:

    (a) Deliverables shall conform to Specifications;
    (b) Deliverables shall be free from defects in materials and workmanship;
    (c) Services shall be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner;
    (d) The Service Provider has the right to provide the Services.

6.2 WARRANTY PERIOD

    Warranties shall be valid for [12] months from the date of delivery.

6.3 WARRANTY REMEDIES

    For valid warranty claims, the Service Provider shall, at its option:

    (a) Repair the defective Deliverable;
    (b) Replace the defective Deliverable; or
    (c) Refund the price paid for the defective Deliverable.

6.4 WARRANTY EXCLUSIONS

    Warranties do not cover defects caused by:

    (a) Customer's design or Specifications;
    (b) Misuse, abuse, or improper handling;
    (c) Modifications made without Service Provider's consent;
    (d) Normal wear and tear.

6.5 DISCLAIMER

    EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY PROVIDED, THE SERVICE PROVIDER DISCLAIMS ALL OTHER
    WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY
    AND FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 7: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

7.1 CUSTOMER IP

    The Customer retains ownership of:

    (a) Specifications, drawings, and designs provided by Customer;
    (b) Tooling paid for by Customer;
    (c) Any Customer-provided materials and information.

7.2 SERVICE PROVIDER IP

    The Service Provider retains ownership of:

    (a) Pre-existing intellectual property;
    (b) Manufacturing processes and know-how;
    (c) General improvements not specific to Customer.

7.3 NO INFRINGEMENT

    Each Party represents that materials provided by it do not infringe any
    third-party intellectual property rights.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 8: CONFIDENTIALITY

8.1 Each Party shall maintain the confidentiality of the other Party's
    confidential information.

8.2 Confidential information includes Specifications, pricing, business plans,
    and any information marked as confidential.

8.3 Confidentiality obligations survive termination for [5] years.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 9: LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

9.1 LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

    (a) Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential,
        or punitive damages;
    (b) The Service Provider's total liability shall not exceed the amounts
        paid under the relevant Work Order.

9.2 INDEMNIFICATION

    Each Party shall indemnify the other against claims arising from:

    (a) Breach of this Agreement;
    (b) Negligence or willful misconduct;
    (c) Infringement of third-party intellectual property rights.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 10: TERM AND TERMINATION

10.1 TERM

     This Agreement shall be effective from the Effective Date and shall
     continue for [3] years unless terminated earlier.

10.2 TERMINATION FOR CONVENIENCE

     Either Party may terminate this Agreement by giving [90] days' written
     notice.

10.3 TERMINATION FOR CAUSE

     Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon:

     (a) Material breach not cured within [30] days of notice;
     (b) Insolvency or bankruptcy of the other Party;
     (c) Assignment without consent.

10.4 EFFECT OF TERMINATION

     Upon termination:

     (a) All outstanding Work Orders shall be completed or cancelled;
     (b) All outstanding payments shall become due;
     (c) Each Party shall return the other's confidential information;
     (d) Accrued rights and obligations shall survive.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 11: GENERAL PROVISIONS

11.1 GOVERNING LAW

     This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.

11.2 DISPUTE RESOLUTION

     Any dispute shall be resolved through arbitration in Chennai under the
     Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

11.3 ENTIRE AGREEMENT

     This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties.

11.4 AMENDMENT

     This Agreement may be amended only in writing signed by both Parties.

11.5 ASSIGNMENT

     Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the other's written consent.

11.6 NOTICES

     All notices shall be in writing to the addresses specified herein.
