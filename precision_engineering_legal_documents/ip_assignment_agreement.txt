RECITALS

WHEREAS:

A. The Company is engaged in the business of precision engineering,
   manufacturing, CNC machining, and related services;

B. The Assignor is employed by / engaged with the Company as [Designation];

C. The Assignor has created, developed, or contributed to certain intellectual
   property in the course of employment/engagement with the Company;

D. The Parties wish to record the assignment of all such intellectual property
   to the Company.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein and
the employment/engagement of the Assignor by the Company, the Parties agree as
follows:

================================================================================

ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

1.1 "Assigned IP" means all Intellectual Property Rights created, developed,
    conceived, or reduced to practice by the Assignor during the term of
    employment/engagement with the Company.

1.2 "Confidential Information" means all non-public information relating to
    the Company's business, technology, operations, and affairs.

1.3 "Intellectual Property Rights" or "IPR" means all intellectual property
    rights including but not limited to:

    (a) Patents, patent applications, inventions, discoveries;
    (b) Copyrights, designs, drawings, documentation;
    (c) Trademarks, trade names, logos, brand names;
    (d) Trade secrets, know-how, technical information;
    (e) Software, source code, algorithms;
    (f) Manufacturing processes, methods, techniques;
    (g) Formulas, compositions, specifications;
    (h) Tool designs, jig designs, fixture designs;
    (i) Any improvements, modifications, or derivatives thereof.

1.4 "Prior IP" means Intellectual Property Rights owned by the Assignor prior
    to the commencement of employment/engagement with the Company.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 2: ASSIGNMENT OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

2.1 ASSIGNMENT

The Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Company
all right, title, and interest in and to the Assigned IP, including but not
limited to:

(a) All patents, patent applications, and patentable inventions;
(b) All copyrights and copyrightable works;
(c) All trade secrets and know-how;
(d) All designs and design rights;
(e) All documentation, drawings, and specifications;
(f) All improvements, modifications, and derivatives;
(g) All rights to apply for, register, and enforce such rights;
(h) All rights to sue for past, present, and future infringement.

2.2 WORK PRODUCT

The Assignor agrees that all work product created during employment/engagement
shall be deemed "work made for hire" and shall be the sole property of the
Company.

2.3 WORLDWIDE ASSIGNMENT

This assignment shall be effective worldwide and shall include all rights in
all jurisdictions.

2.4 PERPETUAL ASSIGNMENT

This assignment shall be perpetual and shall survive termination of the
Assignor's employment/engagement with the Company.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 3: DISCLOSURE OBLIGATION

3.1 PROMPT DISCLOSURE

The Assignor shall promptly disclose to the Company all inventions, discoveries,
improvements, and other intellectual property created during employment/
engagement.

3.2 DISCLOSURE CONTENT

Each disclosure shall include:

(a) Description of the invention or IP;
(b) Date of conception or creation;
(c) Persons involved in the development;
(d) Prior art or existing solutions;
(e) Potential applications and benefits;
(f) Any third-party rights involved.

3.3 DISCLOSURE PROCEDURE

Disclosures shall be made in writing to the Technical Manager and the Company
Secretary using the prescribed Invention Disclosure Form.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 4: COOPERATION

4.1 The Assignor agrees to cooperate fully with the Company in:

(a) Preparing and filing patent, trademark, and copyright applications;
(b) Prosecuting and maintaining such applications;
(c) Enforcing the Company's intellectual property rights;
(d) Defending against any claims or challenges;
(e) Executing all documents necessary to perfect the assignment.

4.2 This cooperation obligation shall survive termination of employment/
    engagement.

4.3 The Company shall reimburse the Assignor for reasonable out-of-pocket
    expenses incurred in providing such cooperation after termination.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 5: PRIOR INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1 SCHEDULE OF PRIOR IP

The Assignor represents that all Prior IP owned by the Assignor is listed in
Schedule A. Any IP not listed shall be deemed to be Assigned IP.

5.2 LICENSE TO PRIOR IP

To the extent any Prior IP is incorporated into Company products or services,
the Assignor hereby grants to the Company a non-exclusive, perpetual,
irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free license to use such Prior IP.

5.3 NO INFRINGEMENT

The Assignor represents that the incorporation of Prior IP into Company products
does not infringe any third-party rights.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 6: MORAL RIGHTS

6.1 To the extent permitted by applicable law, the Assignor hereby waives all
    moral rights in the Assigned IP, including the right of attribution and the
    right of integrity.

6.2 The Assignor consents to any modifications or adaptations of the Assigned IP
    by the Company.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 7: REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

7.1 The Assignor represents and warrants that:

(a) The Assignor has full right and authority to enter into this Agreement;
(b) The Assignor is the sole creator/owner of the Assigned IP;
(c) The Assigned IP does not infringe any third-party rights;
(d) The Assigned IP is free from liens, encumbrances, and claims;
(e) The Assignor has not assigned or licensed the Assigned IP to anyone else;
(f) All persons who contributed to the Assigned IP have assigned their rights
    to the Assignor or the Company;
(g) The information provided in Schedule A is complete and accurate.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 8: INDEMNIFICATION

8.1 The Assignor shall indemnify and hold harmless the Company against any
    claims, damages, losses, or expenses arising from:

(a) Breach of any representation or warranty;
(b) Any claim that the Assigned IP infringes third-party rights;
(c) Any undisclosed Prior IP or encumbrances.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 9: CONFIDENTIALITY

9.1 The Assignor shall maintain strict confidentiality of all Confidential
    Information of the Company.

9.2 The Assignor shall not disclose any Assigned IP to third parties without
    prior written consent of the Company.

9.3 This confidentiality obligation shall survive termination of this Agreement
    for a period of five (5) years.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 10: TERM AND TERMINATION

10.1 This Agreement shall be effective from the date of signing and shall
     continue until termination of the Assignor's employment/engagement.

10.2 The assignment of Assigned IP and the confidentiality obligations shall
     survive termination of this Agreement.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 11: GENERAL PROVISIONS

11.1 ENTIRE AGREEMENT
     This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with
     respect to the subject matter hereof.

11.2 AMENDMENT
     This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by both
     Parties.

11.3 WAIVER
     No waiver of any provision shall be deemed a waiver of any other provision.

11.4 SEVERABILITY
     If any provision is held invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue
     in full force and effect.

11.5 GOVERNING LAW
     This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.

11.6 DISPUTE RESOLUTION
     Any dispute shall be resolved through arbitration in Chennai under the
     Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

11.7 NOTICES
     All notices shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses specified
     herein.
