III. OBJECTS CLAUSE

A. THE OBJECTS TO BE PURSUED BY THE COMPANY ON ITS INCORPORATION:

1.  To carry on business of precision engineering, manufacturing, machining,
    fabrication, and production of high-precision components, parts, and
    assemblies for various industries including automotive, aerospace, defense,
    medical devices, and industrial machinery.

2.  To design, develop, manufacture, assemble, test, market, distribute, import,
    export, buy, sell, and deal in all types of precision-engineered products,
    components, tools, dies, jigs, fixtures, gauges, and machine parts.

3.  To provide CNC machining, turning, milling, grinding, drilling, boring,
    broaching, gear cutting, and other precision machining services.

4.  To manufacture and deal in prototype development, rapid prototyping,
    3D printing, additive manufacturing, and reverse engineering services.

5.  To undertake sheet metal fabrication, welding, surface treatment, heat
    treatment, and finishing operations.

6.  To provide quality inspection, calibration, and metrology services using
    advanced measurement equipment including CMM, surface roughness testers,
    and other precision instruments.

B. MATTERS WHICH ARE NECESSARY FOR FURTHERANCE OF THE OBJECTS SPECIFIED IN
   CLAUSE III(A):

1.  To acquire by purchase, lease, exchange, hire, or otherwise, any movable or
    immovable property, plant, machinery, equipment, patents, copyrights,
    designs, trademarks, licenses, and rights necessary for the business.

2.  To erect, construct, alter, improve, manage, develop, and maintain factories,
    workshops, warehouses, research facilities, and other buildings required for
    the business.

3.  To apply for, obtain, register, purchase, or otherwise acquire any patents,
    patent rights, copyrights, trademarks, trade names, formulas, licenses,
    inventions, and technical know-how.

4.  To enter into any arrangement with any Government or authority, supreme,
    municipal, local, or otherwise, for obtaining any rights, privileges, and
    concessions conducive to the Company's objects.

5.  To undertake and execute any contracts for works involving the supply or use
    of any machinery, and to carry out any ancillary or other works comprised in
    such contracts.

6.  To acquire by purchase, subscription, or otherwise, and to hold, sell,
    dispose of, and deal in shares, stocks, debentures, bonds, and securities of
    any other company.

7.  To enter into partnership or any arrangement for sharing profits, union of
    interests, co-operation, joint venture, or reciprocal concession with any
    person or company.

8.  To borrow or raise money in such manner as the Company thinks fit, including
    by the issue of debentures or debenture stock, and to secure repayment by
    mortgage or charge upon the property of the Company.

9.  To invest and deal with the moneys of the Company not immediately required
    in such manner as may from time to time be determined.

10. To establish and support associations, institutions, funds, trusts, and
    conveniences calculated to benefit employees or ex-employees of the Company.

11. To do all such other things as may be incidental or conducive to the
    attainment of the above objects or any of them.
