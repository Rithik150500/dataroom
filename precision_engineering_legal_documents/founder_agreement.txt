RECITALS

WHEREAS:

A. The Founders have agreed to incorporate a Private Limited Company under the
   Companies Act, 2013 to carry on the business of precision engineering,
   manufacturing, and CNC machining services;

B. The Company was incorporated on July 15, 2018 as Precision Engineering Works
   Private Limited with Corporate Identity Number U28999TN2018PTC125847;

C. The Founders wish to set forth their respective rights, duties, and
   obligations towards each other and the Company, including their respective
   roles, equity ownership, vesting schedules, and other matters;

D. The Founders wish to record the terms of their understanding and agreement
   regarding their mutual relationship as co-founders of the Company.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements set
forth herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and
sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Founders agree as follows:

================================================================================

ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

1.1 "Affiliate" means any entity that directly or indirectly controls, is
    controlled by, or is under common control with a party.

1.2 "Board" means the Board of Directors of the Company.

1.3 "Business" means the business of precision engineering, CNC machining,
    manufacturing of precision components, and related services.

1.4 "Confidential Information" means all non-public information relating to
    the Company's business, technology, operations, finances, or affairs.

1.5 "Intellectual Property" means all patents, trademarks, copyrights, trade
    secrets, designs, technical know-how, processes, and other proprietary
    rights.

1.6 "Vesting Period" means the period during which a Founder's shares vest as
    specified in this Agreement.

================================================================================

3.2 CAPITAL CONTRIBUTION
    Each Founder has contributed capital to the Company as set forth above.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 4: VESTING OF SHARES

4.1 VESTING SCHEDULE
    The shares held by each Founder shall vest as follows:

    (a) 25% of the shares shall vest on completion of 12 months from the
        Effective Date ("Cliff Period");
    (b) Remaining 75% shall vest in equal monthly installments over the next
        36 months following the Cliff Period.

4.2 TOTAL VESTING PERIOD
    The total vesting period shall be 48 months (4 years) from the Effective
    Date.

4.3 ACCELERATION OF VESTING
    The unvested shares shall immediately vest in full upon:

    (a) A Change of Control of the Company;
    (b) Death or permanent disability of a Founder;
    (c) Termination without cause by the Board;
    (d) Any other event as mutually agreed by the Founders.

4.4 FORFEITURE OF UNVESTED SHARES
    If a Founder ceases to be associated with the Company before complete
    vesting, the unvested shares shall be forfeited and either:

    (a) Transferred to the remaining Founder at the original issue price; or
    (b) Cancelled by the Company.

ARTICLE 6: DECISION MAKING

6.1 BOARD COMPOSITION
    The Board shall initially comprise the two Founders. Additional Directors
    may be appointed with mutual consent of the Founders.

6.2 RESERVED MATTERS
    The following matters shall require unanimous consent of all Founders:

    (a) Amendment to the Memorandum or Articles of Association;
    (b) Issuance of new shares or securities;
    (c) Any merger, acquisition, or sale of the Company;
    (d) Entering into any debt exceeding Rs. 50,00,000/-;
    (e) Appointment or removal of senior management;
    (f) Entering into contracts exceeding Rs. 25,00,000/-;
    (g) Change in the nature of business;
    (h) Any related party transactions;
    (i) Approval of annual business plan and budget;
    (j) Declaration of dividends.

6.3 DEADLOCK RESOLUTION
    In case of a deadlock between the Founders on any matter, the Founders
    shall attempt to resolve the dispute through good faith negotiations. If
    unresolved within 30 days, the matter shall be referred to mediation.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 7: NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

7.1 NON-COMPETE
    During the term of this Agreement and for a period of 2 years after
    cessation of association with the Company, each Founder agrees not to:

    (a) Directly or indirectly engage in any business competitive with the
        Company's Business;
    (b) Own, manage, or be employed by any competing business;
    (c) Assist any person or entity in competing with the Company.

7.2 NON-SOLICITATION
    During the term and for 2 years thereafter, each Founder agrees not to:

    (a) Solicit or attempt to solicit any customer or client of the Company;
    (b) Induce or attempt to induce any employee to leave the Company;
    (c) Interfere with the Company's relationships with suppliers or vendors.

7.3 GEOGRAPHICAL SCOPE
    The non-compete restrictions shall apply within India.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 8: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

8.1 ASSIGNMENT TO COMPANY
    Each Founder hereby assigns to the Company all right, title, and interest
    in and to any Intellectual Property created, developed, or conceived by
    such Founder in connection with the Business.

8.2 PRE-EXISTING IP
    Any Intellectual Property owned by a Founder prior to the Effective Date
    and contributed to the Company shall be listed in Schedule B. Such IP shall
    be licensed to the Company on a perpetual, royalty-free basis.

8.3 WORK PRODUCT
    All work product, inventions, designs, processes, and technical know-how
    developed by any Founder during the term shall be the exclusive property
    of the Company.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 9: CONFIDENTIALITY

9.1 CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATION
    Each Founder shall maintain the confidentiality of all Confidential
    Information and shall not disclose such information to any third party
    without the Company's prior written consent.

9.2 EXCEPTIONS
    The confidentiality obligation shall not apply to information that:

    (a) Is or becomes publicly available without breach of this Agreement;
    (b) Was known to the Founder prior to disclosure;
    (c) Is independently developed without use of Confidential Information;
    (d) Is required to be disclosed by law or court order.

9.3 SURVIVAL
    The confidentiality obligations shall survive termination of this Agreement
    for a period of 5 years.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 10: EXIT AND TRANSFER OF SHARES

10.1 RIGHT OF FIRST REFUSAL
     If a Founder wishes to sell or transfer shares, the other Founder shall
     have the right of first refusal to purchase such shares on the same terms.

10.2 TAG-ALONG RIGHT
     If a Founder sells shares to a third party, the other Founder shall have
     the right to participate in such sale on the same terms and conditions.

10.3 DRAG-ALONG RIGHT
     If Founders holding 75% or more of the shares agree to sell the Company,
     the remaining Founders shall be required to participate in such sale.

10.4 BUY-OUT ON EXIT
     If a Founder wishes to exit the Company, the remaining Founder shall have
     the option to buy out such Founder's shares at fair market value
     determined by an independent valuer.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 11: FOUNDER EXIT

11.1 VOLUNTARY EXIT
     A Founder may exit by giving 90 days' prior written notice to the other
     Founder and the Company.

11.2 REMOVAL FOR CAUSE
     A Founder may be removed for cause, including:

     (a) Material breach of this Agreement;
     (b) Fraud, dishonesty, or misconduct;
     (c) Conviction for a criminal offense;
     (d) Willful neglect of duties;
     (e) Breach of fiduciary duties.

11.3 CONSEQUENCES OF EXIT
     Upon exit, the departing Founder shall:

     (a) Forfeit all unvested shares;
     (b) Transfer unvested shares to the Company or other Founder;
     (c) Resign from all positions held in the Company;
     (d) Continue to be bound by confidentiality and non-compete obligations.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 12: REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

12.1 Each Founder represents and warrants that:

     (a) He has the legal capacity to enter into this Agreement;
     (b) This Agreement constitutes a valid and binding obligation;
     (c) He is not bound by any agreement that would prevent performance;
     (d) He has not made any misrepresentation regarding his qualifications;
     (e) He will devote full time and attention to the Company's business.

     This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Founders and

13.7 NOTICES

13.8 COUNTERPARTS
     This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be
