RECITALS

WHEREAS:

A. The Company is engaged in the business of precision engineering, CNC
   machining, and manufacturing of precision components;

B. The Company desires to raise funds for working capital, capital expenditure,
   and business expansion;

C. The Investor desires to invest in the Company by way of a convertible note;

D. The Parties wish to record the terms and conditions of such investment.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the
Parties agree as follows:

================================================================================

ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

1.1 "Conversion Date" means the date on which the Note is converted into Equity
    Shares.

1.2 "Conversion Price" means the price per Equity Share at which the Note
    converts, calculated as per Article 4.

1.3 "Equity Shares" means the equity shares of the Company with a face value of
    Rs. 10/- each.

1.4 "Maturity Date" means [24] months from the Issue Date.

1.5 "Next Equity Financing" means the next round of equity financing by the
    Company resulting in gross proceeds of at least Rs. [1,00,00,000/-].

1.6 "Note" means the convertible note issued under this Agreement.

1.7 "Principal Amount" means Rs. [_______________] (Rupees [_______________]
    Only).

1.8 "Valuation Cap" means Rs. [_______________] (Rupees [_______________] Only).

================================================================================

ARTICLE 2: INVESTMENT

2.1 ISSUANCE OF NOTE

    The Company agrees to issue, and the Investor agrees to subscribe to, a
    convertible note for the Principal Amount.

2.2 DISBURSEMENT

    The Investor shall transfer the Principal Amount to the Company's bank
    account within [7] days of execution of this Agreement.

2.3 USE OF FUNDS

    The Company shall use the funds for:

    (a) Working capital requirements;
    (b) Purchase of machinery and equipment;
    (c) Business expansion and marketing;
    (d) General corporate purposes.

2.4 ACKNOWLEDGMENT

    Upon receipt of the Principal Amount, the Company shall issue a receipt and
    the Note certificate to the Investor.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 3: INTEREST

3.1 The Note shall carry simple interest at the rate of [8%] per annum.

3.2 Interest shall accrue from the date of disbursement until conversion or
    repayment.

3.3 Accrued interest shall be added to the Principal Amount for the purpose of
    calculating the number of shares upon conversion.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 4: CONVERSION

4.1 AUTOMATIC CONVERSION

    The Note shall automatically convert into Equity Shares upon the occurrence
    of the Next Equity Financing.

4.2 CONVERSION PRICE

    The Conversion Price shall be the lower of:

    (a) The price per share paid by investors in the Next Equity Financing
        multiplied by [0.80] (20% discount); or
    (b) The Valuation Cap divided by the fully diluted share capital of the
        Company immediately prior to the Next Equity Financing.

4.3 NUMBER OF SHARES

    The number of Equity Shares to be issued upon conversion shall be:

    (Principal Amount + Accrued Interest) / Conversion Price

4.4 FRACTIONAL SHARES

    Any fractional shares shall be rounded down to the nearest whole number.

4.5 CONVERSION MECHANICS

    Upon conversion:

    (a) The Company shall issue Equity Shares to the Investor;
    (b) The Note shall be cancelled;
    (c) The Investor shall execute subscription documents;
    (d) The Investor shall be entitled to the same rights as other investors
        in the Next Equity Financing.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 5: OPTIONAL CONVERSION

5.1 CONVERSION ON MATURITY

    If the Next Equity Financing has not occurred by the Maturity Date, the
    Investor may elect to:

    (a) Convert the Note at a price based on the Valuation Cap; or
    (b) Extend the Maturity Date by [12] months.

5.2 CONVERSION ON CHANGE OF CONTROL

    If a Change of Control occurs before conversion, the Investor may elect to:

    (a) Convert the Note at a price based on the Valuation Cap; or
    (b) Receive repayment of the Principal Amount plus accrued interest plus
        a premium of [50%].

5.3 VOLUNTARY CONVERSION

    The Investor may elect to convert the Note at any time at a price based on
    the Valuation Cap, with [30] days' prior notice.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 6: REPAYMENT

6.1 DEFAULT REPAYMENT

    If the Note has not converted by the extended Maturity Date, the Company
    shall repay the Principal Amount plus accrued interest within [30] days.

6.2 PREPAYMENT

    The Company may prepay the Note with the Investor's consent.

6.3 REPAYMENT PRIORITY

    The Note shall rank:

    (a) Senior to all equity shareholders;
    (b) Pari passu with other convertible note holders;
    (c) Subordinate to secured creditors.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 7: REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

7.1 COMPANY REPRESENTATIONS

    The Company represents and warrants that:

    (a) It is duly incorporated and validly existing;
    (b) It has the authority to enter into this Agreement;
    (c) This Agreement is binding and enforceable;
    (d) No consents are required other than those already obtained;
    (e) There is no litigation that would materially affect the Company;
    (f) The Company is in compliance with applicable laws;
    (g) The Company's financial statements are accurate;
    (h) The Company owns or has rights to its intellectual property;
    (i) There are no undisclosed liabilities.

7.2 INVESTOR REPRESENTATIONS

    The Investor represents and warrants that:

    (a) It has the authority to enter into this Agreement;
    (b) It is an accredited investor / sophisticated investor;
    (c) It has reviewed the Company's business and financials;
    (d) It understands the risks of this investment;
    (e) Funds invested are from legitimate sources.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 8: COVENANTS

8.1 AFFIRMATIVE COVENANTS

    The Company shall:

    (a) Maintain its corporate existence;
    (b) Comply with all applicable laws;
    (c) Maintain adequate insurance;
    (d) Keep proper books of account;
    (e) Provide quarterly financial statements to the Investor;
    (f) Notify the Investor of any material adverse events;
    (g) Use funds for the agreed purposes.

8.2 NEGATIVE COVENANTS

    Without Investor's consent, the Company shall not:

    (a) Issue any securities senior to or pari passu with the Note (except
        for notes on substantially similar terms up to Rs. [_______________]);
    (b) Declare or pay dividends;
    (c) Make any loans to related parties;
    (d) Enter into any material contracts outside ordinary course;
    (e) Sell or dispose of substantial assets;
    (f) Amend the charter documents in a manner adverse to the Investor.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 9: INFORMATION RIGHTS

9.1 The Company shall provide to the Investor:

    (a) Quarterly unaudited financial statements within [30] days of quarter end;
    (b) Annual audited financial statements within [90] days of year end;
    (c) Annual budget and business plan;
    (d) Capitalization table;
    (e) Notice of any material events.

9.2 The Investor shall have the right to inspect the Company's books and records
    upon reasonable notice.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 10: EVENTS OF DEFAULT

10.1 The following shall constitute Events of Default:

     (a) Failure to pay any amount when due;
     (b) Breach of any representation or warranty;
     (c) Breach of any covenant not cured within [30] days;
     (d) Insolvency or bankruptcy of the Company;
     (e) Material adverse change in the Company's business;
     (f) Change of control without compliance with this Agreement.

10.2 Upon an Event of Default, the Investor may:

     (a) Declare the Note immediately due and payable;
     (b) Convert the Note at the Valuation Cap;
     (c) Exercise any other remedies available at law.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 11: TRANSFER RESTRICTIONS

11.1 The Note may not be transferred without the Company's prior written
     consent.

11.2 Permitted transfers include transfers to Affiliates of the Investor.

11.3 Any transfer must comply with applicable securities laws.

================================================================================

ARTICLE 12: CONFIDENTIALITY

12.1 Each Party shall maintain confidentiality of the other's confidential
     information.

12.2 This Agreement and its terms are confidential except as required by law.

12.3 Confidentiality obligations survive termination for [5] years.

================================================================================

13.3 ENTIRE AGREEMENT

13.4 AMENDMENT

13.5 WAIVER

     No waiver of any provision shall be deemed a waiver of any other provision.

13.6 SEVERABILITY

13.7 NOTICES

     All notices shall be in writing to the addresses specified herein.
