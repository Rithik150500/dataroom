                    EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

================================================================================

Company Name        : Precision Engineering Works Private Limited
Incorporation Date  : 15th July, 2018
Industry           : Precision Engineering & Manufacturing
Location           : SIPCOT Industrial Estate, Hosur, Tamil Nadu

VISION: To become a leading precision engineering company in South India,
delivering world-class components to automotive, aerospace, and industrial
sectors.

MISSION: To provide high-quality precision machined components with exceptional
accuracy, reliability, and customer service while fostering a culture of
continuous improvement and employee development.

================================================================================

                    BUSINESS OVERVIEW

================================================================================

1. PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

   A. CNC Machining Services:
      - CNC Turning (up to 500mm diameter)
      - CNC Milling (3-axis, 4-axis, 5-axis)
      - CNC Grinding (surface, cylindrical, centerless)
      - Wire EDM and Die Sinking EDM

   B. Precision Components:
      - Automotive: Transmission parts, engine components, steering parts
      - Aerospace: Structural components, fasteners, brackets
      - Medical: Surgical instruments, implant components
      - Industrial: Hydraulic components, valve parts, pump components

   C. Value-Added Services:
      - Design for Manufacturing (DFM) consultation
      - Prototype development
      - Small batch production
      - Assembly and sub-assembly

2. TARGET MARKETS

   A. Primary Markets:
      - Automotive OEMs and Tier-1 suppliers (50%)
      - Aerospace and defense (20%)
      - Industrial machinery (20%)
      - Medical devices (10%)

   B. Geographic Focus:
      - South India (Primary)
      - North India (Secondary)
      - Export markets (Future)

3. COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGES

   - Advanced 5-axis CNC machining capabilities
   - ISO 9001:2015 certified quality management system
   - Skilled workforce with strong technical expertise
   - Strategic location in industrial hub (Hosur)
   - Competitive pricing with quick turnaround
   - Strong customer relationships
