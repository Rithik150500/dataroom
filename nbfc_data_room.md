### 4. LOAN ORIGINATION & UNDERWRITING

#### 4.1 Credit Policy
- Board-approved Credit Policy (Last updated: April 2025)
- Covers: Target segments, ticket size, geography, credit assessment, deviation matrix
- Annual review by Board
- Delegated Powers of Approval:
  * Branch Manager: Up to ₹5 lakhs
  * Cluster Head: Up to ₹15 lakhs
  * Regional Head: Up to ₹50 lakhs
  * Credit Committee: >₹50 lakhs
  * Board: >₹2 Crores (rare, typically decline such large tickets)

#### 4.2 Underwriting Standards
- **MSME Loans:**
  * Minimum 2 years in business
  * ITR/GST Returns for 2 years
  * Banking analysis (6-12 months statements)
  * Bureau check (CIBIL/Experian/Equifax/CRIF)
  * Physical verification of business premises
  * Collateral valuation by empaneled valuers
  * FOIR (Fixed Obligation to Income Ratio): <50%

- **Two-Wheeler Loans:**
  * Minimum monthly income: ₹12,000
  * Employment stability (min 1 year)
  * Bureau score: >650
  * LTV: 80-90% (Depending on profile)
  * Co-applicant for lower credit scores

- **Gold Loans:**
  * Purity check by trained staff (XRF machine)
  * LTV capped at 65% of current gold value
  * Valuation as per daily gold rates
  * Customer KYC as per PML Act
  * Jewellery storage in bank-grade lockers

#### 4.3 Bureau Integration
- Real-time integration with 4 credit bureaus (CIBIL, Experian, Equifax, CRIF)
- Bureau pull for all loan applications
- Account-level reporting to bureaus (Monthly)
- Dispute resolution mechanism for customer bureau grievances

#### 4.4 Fraud Prevention
- Aadhaar-based e-KYC for identity verification
- Penny-drop verification for bank account validation
- GST number validation via API
- Negative list database (Fraudulent customers, blacklisted PANs)
- FIU sanctions list screening
- Residence & Business address verification (Physical + Digital)

#### 4.5 Loan Agreements & Documentation
- Standard Loan Agreement templates (Approved by legal)
- Digitally signed agreements (e-Sign under IT Act)
- Disbursement only to borrower's verified bank account
- NACH mandate for EMI collection
- Hypothecation agreements registered with CERSAI (for vehicle loans)
