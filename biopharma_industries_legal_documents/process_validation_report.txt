1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
====================

This report presents the results of prospective process validation for
Metformin Hydrochloride Tablets 500mg. Three consecutive batches (PV-01,
PV-02, PV-03) were manufactured at commercial scale following the approved
master batch record.

CONCLUSION: The manufacturing process for Metformin Hydrochloride Tablets
500mg is VALIDATED. All three batches met in-process and finished product
specifications, demonstrating that the process is capable of consistently
producing product meeting predetermined quality attributes.

================================================================================

2. OBJECTIVE
===========

To validate that the manufacturing process for Metformin Hydrochloride
Tablets 500mg consistently produces a product meeting all predetermined
specifications and quality attributes.

- All critical process parameters

15. DISCUSSION
=============

15.1 PROCESS CAPABILITY

All critical process parameters were within the defined acceptable ranges.
The process demonstrated good reproducibility across three consecutive batches.

15.2 IN-PROCESS CONTROLS

Blend uniformity testing confirmed homogeneous distribution of the active
ingredient with RSD values well below the 5% acceptance criterion.

Compression parameters were consistent throughout the tablet compression
operation, with weight and hardness values showing good uniformity.

15.3 FINISHED PRODUCT QUALITY

All three batches met finished product specifications:
- Assay: 98.8-100.5% (Specification: 95-105%)
- Content Uniformity: AV 4.9-7.1 (Specification: NMT 15)
- Dissolution: 90.8-93.5% at 45 min (Specification: NLT 70%)
- Related substances well within limits

The dissolution profiles of all three batches were similar (f2 > 50),
indicating consistent drug release characteristics.

15.4 YIELD ANALYSIS

Overall yields ranged from 96.5% to 97.7%, within the expected range.
Material losses were primarily due to:
- Sampling for in-process and finished product testing
- Normal processing losses (transfer, equipment surfaces)

================================================================================

16. CONCLUSION
=============

Based on the results of this validation study:

1. All three validation batches were manufactured successfully following
   the approved master batch record.

2. All critical process parameters were within the defined acceptable ranges.

3. All in-process controls met acceptance criteria.

4. All finished product tests met specifications.

5. Yields were within acceptable limits.

6. No deviations occurred during validation.

CONCLUSION: The manufacturing process for Metformin Hydrochloride Tablets 500mg
is VALIDATED and is capable of consistently producing product that meets all
predetermined specifications and quality attributes.
