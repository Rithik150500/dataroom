                     RECITALS
================================================================================

A. The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship involving

B. In connection with the Purpose, each Party may disclose to the other Party
   certain confidential and proprietary information.

C. The Parties desire to protect such confidential information on the terms
   set forth herein.

================================================================================
                1. DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

1.1 "Confidential Information" means all information disclosed by either Party
    (the "Disclosing Party") to the other Party (the "Receiving Party"),
    whether oral, written, electronic, or visual, that is:

    (a) Marked as "Confidential," "Proprietary," or similar designation;
    (b) Identified as confidential at the time of disclosure; or
    (c) Should reasonably be understood to be confidential given the nature
        of the information and circumstances of disclosure.

1.2 Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to:
    - Business plans, strategies, and forecasts
    - Financial information and projections
    - Customer and supplier information
    - Technical data, specifications, and designs
    - Software, source code, and algorithms
    - Trade secrets and know-how
    - Marketing and pricing information
    - Personnel information
    - Any other proprietary information

1.3 Confidential Information does not include information that:
    (a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party;
    (b) Was known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure;
    (c) Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of
        Confidential Information;
    (d) Is rightfully obtained from a third party without restriction;
    (e) Is required to be disclosed by law or legal process.

================================================================================
                2. OBLIGATIONS
================================================================================

2.1 NON-DISCLOSURE

    The Receiving Party shall:
    (a) Maintain the confidentiality of all Confidential Information;
    (b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior
        written consent of the Disclosing Party;
    (c) Use the same degree of care to protect Confidential Information as it
        uses for its own confidential information, but not less than reasonable
        care.

2.2 PERMITTED USE

    The Receiving Party may use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose
    and for no other purpose.

2.3 PERMITTED DISCLOSURE

    The Receiving Party may disclose Confidential Information to its employees,
    directors, advisors, and contractors who:
    (a) Have a need to know for the Purpose;
    (b) Are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as restrictive as
        this Agreement.

2.4 LEGAL COMPULSION

    If the Receiving Party is required by law to disclose Confidential
    Information, it shall:
    (a) Provide prompt written notice to the Disclosing Party;
    (b) Cooperate with the Disclosing Party's efforts to obtain protective order;
    (c) Disclose only that portion required to be disclosed.

================================================================================
                3. OWNERSHIP
================================================================================

3.1 All Confidential Information shall remain the property of the Disclosing
    Party.

3.2 No license or right to Confidential Information is granted except the
    limited right to use for the Purpose.

3.3 Nothing in this Agreement obligates either Party to enter into any further
    agreement or proceed with the Purpose.

================================================================================
                4. RETURN OF INFORMATION
================================================================================

4.1 Upon request of the Disclosing Party or termination of this Agreement, the
    Receiving Party shall:
    (a) Return or destroy all Confidential Information;
    (b) Provide written certification of destruction if requested;
    (c) Delete all electronic copies from systems.

4.2 The Receiving Party may retain copies:
    (a) Required by law or regulatory requirements;
    (b) Stored in routine backup systems (subject to ongoing confidentiality).

================================================================================
                5. TERM AND TERMINATION
================================================================================

5.1 This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of 2 (two) years from
    the date hereof.

5.2 Either Party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days' written notice.

5.3 The confidentiality obligations shall survive termination for a period of
    5 (five) years.

================================================================================
                6. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES
================================================================================

6.1 Each Party represents and warrants that it has the authority to enter into
    this Agreement and to disclose its Confidential Information.

6.2 DISCLAIMER: Confidential Information is provided "AS IS" without warranty
    of any kind.

================================================================================
                7. REMEDIES
================================================================================

7.1 The Parties acknowledge that breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable
    harm for which monetary damages may be inadequate.

7.2 The Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief in addition
    to any other remedies available at law or equity.

================================================================================
                8. MISCELLANEOUS
================================================================================

8.1 GOVERNING LAW
    This Agreement shall be governed by laws of India.

8.2 JURISDICTION
    Courts in Mumbai shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

8.3 ENTIRE AGREEMENT
    This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement regarding confidentiality.

8.4 AMENDMENT
    This Agreement may be amended only by written instrument signed by both
    Parties.

8.5 ASSIGNMENT
    This Agreement may not be assigned without prior written consent.

8.6 SEVERABILITY
    Invalid provisions shall be severed; remaining provisions continue.

8.7 WAIVER
    No waiver of any breach shall constitute waiver of any other breach.

8.8 NOTICES
    All notices shall be in writing and delivered to the addresses below.
