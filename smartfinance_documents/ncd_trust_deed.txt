================================================================================
                    EVENTS OF DEFAULT
================================================================================

1. Non-payment of principal or interest within 7 days of due date
2. Breach of any covenant not cured within 30 days
3. Cross-default on other debt > ₹5 Crores
4. Insolvency or winding-up proceedings
5. Cancellation or suspension of RBI registration
6. Change in control without Trustee consent
7. Cessation of business
8. Material adverse change
9. Asset cover falling below 1.0x

CONSEQUENCES OF DEFAULT:
- Acceleration of all outstanding amounts
- Enforcement of security
- Legal action for recovery
