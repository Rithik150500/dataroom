ITEM 2: QUARTERLY FINANCIAL RESULTS - Q2 FY 2024-25

The CFO presented the unaudited financial results for Q2 FY 2024-25:

| Particulars                    | Q2 FY25    | Q2 FY24    | Growth   |
|--------------------------------|------------|------------|----------|
| Total Disbursements            | ₹126 Cr    | ₹78 Cr     | 61.5%    |
| Assets Under Management (AUM)  | ₹245 Cr    | ₹142 Cr    | 72.5%    |
| Net Interest Income            | ₹8.2 Cr    | ₹4.8 Cr    | 70.8%    |
| Total Revenue                  | ₹12.5 Cr   | ₹7.3 Cr    | 71.2%    |
| EBITDA                         | ₹2.8 Cr    | ₹0.9 Cr    | 211.1%   |
| Profit After Tax               | ₹1.2 Cr    | (₹0.5 Cr)  | Turn-around|

Key Highlights:
- Active borrowers crossed 1,25,000 milestone
- NPA ratio improved to 2.8% from 3.4% in previous quarter
- Collection efficiency at 97.2%
- Customer acquisition cost reduced by 18%

RESOLUTION: RESOLVED THAT the unaudited financial results for the quarter
ended September 30, 2024 be and are hereby noted and approved for
publication and filing with regulatory authorities.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ITEM 3: CO-LENDING ARRANGEMENT WITH YES BANK

The MD presented the proposed co-lending arrangement with Yes Bank for
priority sector lending:

Key Terms:
- Partnership for personal loans up to ₹10 lakhs
- Yes Bank to fund 80%, SmartFinance to fund 20%
- Blended interest rate of 14.5% p.a.
- Target disbursement: ₹200 Cr in first year
- First Loss Default Guarantee (FLDG) of 5%

After detailed discussion on risk sharing mechanism and operational workflow:

RESOLUTION: RESOLVED THAT the Board hereby approves the co-lending
arrangement with Yes Bank Limited on the terms presented and authorizes
the Managing Director to negotiate, finalize and execute the Co-Lending
Agreement and all related documents.

FURTHER RESOLVED THAT the Company Secretary be authorized to file necessary
forms with RBI and other regulatory authorities.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ITEM 4: RISK MANAGEMENT UPDATE

The Chief Risk Officer presented the quarterly risk management report:

Credit Risk:
- Portfolio at Risk (PAR 30+): 4.2%
- Expected Credit Loss (ECL) provision: ₹6.8 Cr
- Concentration in top 100 borrowers: 2.1%

Operational Risk:
- System uptime: 99.95%
- Zero data breaches in the quarter
- Fraud detection rate: 99.8%

Liquidity Risk:
- ALM mismatch within RBI prescribed limits
- Liquid assets coverage: 125%

Regulatory Risk:
- All RBI returns filed on time
- No adverse observations in latest RBI inspection

RESOLUTION: RESOLVED THAT the risk management report for Q2 FY 2024-25 be
and is hereby noted and the Board appreciates the Risk Management
Committee's efforts in maintaining risk parameters within approved limits.

Committee.
