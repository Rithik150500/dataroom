================================================================================
                    ARTICLES OF ASSOCIATION
                              OF
                  SMARTFINANCE INDIA LIMITED

            [Pursuant to Section 5 of Companies Act, 2013]
================================================================================

                         PRELIMINARY

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. INTERPRETATION

   1.1 In these Articles, unless the context otherwise requires:

       "Act" means the Companies Act, 2013 or any statutory modification or
       re-enactment thereof for the time being in force;

       "Articles" means these Articles of Association as originally framed
       or as altered from time to time;

       "Board" or "Board of Directors" means the Board of Directors of the
       Company;

       "Company" means SmartFinance India Limited;

       "Director" means a Director of the Company;

       "Dividend" includes interim dividend;

       "Member" means a person holding shares in the Company;

       "RBI" means the Reserve Bank of India;

       "Seal" means the Common Seal of the Company;

       "Secretary" means the Company Secretary of the Company;

       "Special Resolution" has the meaning assigned to it by Section 114
       of the Act.

   1.2 Words importing the singular number include the plural number and
       vice versa.

   1.3 Words importing the masculine gender include the feminine gender.

================================================================================
                         SHARE CAPITAL
================================================================================

2. AUTHORIZED SHARE CAPITAL

   The Authorized Share Capital of the Company is ₹15,00,00,000 (Rupees
   Fifteen Crores only) divided into 50,00,000 Equity Shares of ₹10/- each
   and 10,00,000 Preference Shares of ₹100/- each.

3. ALTERATION OF CAPITAL

   The Company may, by ordinary resolution:
   (a) Increase its share capital by such sum as it thinks expedient;
   (b) Consolidate and divide all or any of its share capital;
   (c) Convert fully paid-up shares into stock and reconvert that stock
       into fully paid-up shares;
   (d) Sub-divide its shares into shares of smaller amount;
   (e) Cancel shares which have not been taken or agreed to be taken.

4. REDUCTION OF CAPITAL

   Subject to the provisions of the Act, the Company may reduce its share
   capital in any manner.

5. ISSUE OF SHARES

   5.1 Subject to the provisions of the Act, the shares shall be under the
       control of the Board who may allot or otherwise dispose of the same
       on such terms and conditions as they think fit.

   5.2 The Company shall not issue shares at a discount except as permitted
       by the Act.

   5.3 The Company may issue sweat equity shares in accordance with Section
       54 of the Act.

6. CALLS ON SHARES

   6.1 The Board may make calls upon the Members in respect of any moneys
       unpaid on their shares.

   6.2 Each Member shall pay the amount of every call so made on him to the
       persons and at the times and places appointed by the Board.

   6.3 A call shall be deemed to have been made at the time when the
       resolution of the Board authorizing the call was passed.

================================================================================
                    TRANSFER OF SHARES
================================================================================

7. TRANSFER OF SHARES

   7.1 The instrument of transfer of any share shall be in writing and shall
       be executed by or on behalf of both the transferor and transferee.

   7.2 The Board may, subject to the right of appeal conferred by the Act,
       decline to register any transfer of shares.

   7.3 The registration of transfers may be suspended at such times and for
       such periods as the Board may determine.

8. TRANSMISSION OF SHARES

   8.1 On the death of a Member, the survivor or survivors where the Member
       was a joint holder, and the legal representatives of the deceased
       where he was a sole holder, shall be the only persons recognized by
       the Company as having any title to his interest in the shares.

9. RIGHT OF FIRST REFUSAL

   9.1 No Member shall transfer any shares to any person who is not a Member
       without first offering them to the existing Members in proportion to
       their existing shareholdings.

   9.2 The offer shall be made by written notice to the Company specifying
       the number of shares to be transferred and the price per share.

   9.3 This right of first refusal shall not apply to transfers:
       (a) To any affiliate of the transferring Member;
       (b) Pursuant to an initial public offering;
       (c) With the prior written consent of shareholders holding at least
           75% of the issued share capital.

================================================================================
                    INVESTOR RIGHTS
================================================================================

10. SERIES A PREFERRED SHARES

    10.1 The Series A Preferred Shares shall have the following rights:
         (a) Liquidation preference at 1x the original issue price;
         (b) Participating preferred rights on remaining proceeds;
         (c) Anti-dilution protection on a broad-based weighted average basis;
         (d) Conversion rights to equity shares at any time;
         (e) Automatic conversion upon qualified IPO.

11. BOARD COMPOSITION

    11.1 The Board shall consist of not less than 3 Directors and not more
         than 15 Directors.

    11.2 Investor Directors may be appointed as follows:
         (a) Investors holding at least 10% of fully diluted capital may
             appoint one Director;
         (b) Investors holding at least 25% of fully diluted capital may
             appoint two Directors.

12. RESERVED MATTERS

    12.1 The following matters shall require approval of Directors appointed
         by Series A Investors:
         (a) Any amendment to the Memorandum or Articles of Association;
         (b) Issuance of new securities or convertible instruments;
         (c) Declaration of dividends;
         (d) Related party transactions exceeding ₹50 lakhs;
         (e) Capital expenditure exceeding ₹2 crores;
         (f) Acquisition or disposal of material assets;
         (g) Entry into new lines of business;
         (h) Any merger, acquisition, or change of control transaction;
         (i) Appointment or removal of CEO, CFO, or CTO;
         (j) Change in auditors.

13. INFORMATION RIGHTS

    13.1 Investors holding at least 5% of the fully diluted capital shall be
         entitled to receive:
         (a) Monthly MIS within 15 days of month end;
         (b) Quarterly unaudited financials within 30 days;
         (c) Annual audited financials within 90 days;
         (d) Annual operating plan and budget before fiscal year;
         (e) Cap table updates upon any change.

================================================================================
                    BOARD OF DIRECTORS
================================================================================

14. POWERS OF THE BOARD

    14.1 The business of the Company shall be managed by the Board who may
         exercise all such powers as are not required by the Act or these
         Articles to be exercised by the Company in general meeting.

15. MEETINGS OF THE BOARD

    15.1 The Board shall meet at least four times a year with a maximum
         interval of 120 days between two consecutive meetings.

    15.2 The quorum for a Board meeting shall be one-third of total strength
         or two Directors, whichever is higher.

    15.3 Directors may participate in meetings through video conferencing
         as permitted by the Act.

16. MANAGING DIRECTOR

    16.1 The Board may appoint a Managing Director for such term and at such
         remuneration as it thinks fit.

    16.2 A Managing Director shall not hold office for a term exceeding five
         years at a time.

17. WHOLE-TIME DIRECTOR

    17.1 The Board may appoint a Whole-time Director subject to the approval
         of Members by special resolution.

18. INDEPENDENT DIRECTORS

    18.1 The Company shall appoint Independent Directors as required under
         Section 149 of the Act and RBI guidelines for NBFCs.

    18.2 At least one-third of the total number of Directors shall be
         Independent Directors.

================================================================================
                    GENERAL MEETINGS
================================================================================

19. ANNUAL GENERAL MEETING

    19.1 The Company shall hold an Annual General Meeting within six months
         from the close of the financial year.

    19.2 The AGM shall be held during business hours on a day that is not a
         public holiday at the registered office or at any other place
         within the city where the registered office is situated.

20. EXTRAORDINARY GENERAL MEETING

    20.1 The Board may convene an Extraordinary General Meeting whenever it
         thinks fit.

21. NOTICE OF GENERAL MEETINGS

    21.1 A general meeting may be called by giving not less than 21 clear
         days' notice in writing.

22. QUORUM FOR GENERAL MEETINGS

    22.1 The quorum for a general meeting shall be 5 Members present in
         person or through authorized representatives.

23. VOTING

    23.1 Every Member shall have one vote for every share held by him.
    23.2 A resolution put to the vote shall be decided on a show of hands
         unless a poll is demanded.

================================================================================
                    DIVIDENDS
================================================================================

24. DECLARATION OF DIVIDENDS

    24.1 The Company in general meeting may declare dividends but no dividend
         shall exceed the amount recommended by the Board.

    24.2 The Board may pay interim dividends as appear to the Board to be
         justified by the profits of the Company.

25. UNPAID DIVIDENDS

    25.1 Any dividend unclaimed for 7 years shall be transferred to the
         Investor Education and Protection Fund.

================================================================================
                    ACCOUNTS AND AUDIT
================================================================================

26. BOOKS OF ACCOUNT

    26.1 The Company shall keep proper books of account as required by
         Section 128 of the Act.

    26.2 The books of account shall be kept at the registered office.

27. AUDITORS

    27.1 Auditors shall be appointed as provided in the Act.
    27.2 As an NBFC, the Company shall comply with RBI guidelines on
         statutory audit.

================================================================================
                    NBFC SPECIFIC PROVISIONS
================================================================================

28. RBI COMPLIANCE

    28.1 The Company shall at all times comply with the Reserve Bank of
         India Act, 1934, and all directions, guidelines, and circulars
         issued by RBI for NBFCs.

    28.2 The Company shall maintain minimum capital adequacy ratio as
         prescribed by RBI.

    28.3 The Company shall not accept public deposits without obtaining
         necessary approvals.

29. FIT AND PROPER CRITERIA

    29.1 All Directors shall meet the fit and proper criteria prescribed
         by RBI for Directors of NBFCs.

    29.2 Any change in Directors shall be reported to RBI within the
         prescribed time.

30. CONCENTRATION NORMS

    30.1 The Company shall comply with concentration of credit norms as
         prescribed by RBI.

================================================================================
                    INDEMNITY
================================================================================

31. INDEMNIFICATION

    31.1 Every Director, Managing Director, Manager, Secretary, and other
         officer of the Company shall be indemnified by the Company against
         all costs, charges, losses, expenses, and liabilities incurred by
         them in the proper conduct of the Company's business.

================================================================================
                    WINDING UP
================================================================================

32. DISTRIBUTION ON WINDING UP

    32.1 In the event of winding up, the assets available for distribution
         shall be applied first in payment of the liquidation preference to
         preference shareholders and thereafter among the equity shareholders
         in proportion to their shareholding.
