                2. PROBATION
================================================================================

2.1 The Employee shall be on probation for a period of 6 (six) months from the

    is unsatisfactory.

================================================================================

3.1 The Employee shall:

    (b) Devote full time and attention to Company work;
    (c) Follow all Company policies and procedures;
    (d) Act in the best interests of the Company;
    (e) Maintain professional conduct and ethics.

3.2 The Employee shall not, without prior written consent:
    (a) Engage in any other employment or business;
    (b) Accept any position in other organizations;
    (c) Undertake consulting or advisory roles.

================================================================================
                7. WORKING HOURS
================================================================================

7.1 Standard working hours: 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday.

7.2 The Employee may be required to work additional hours as necessary for
    business requirements without additional compensation.

7.3 Employees in managerial positions are not entitled to overtime.

================================================================================
                8. CONFIDENTIALITY
================================================================================

8.1 The Employee shall maintain strict confidentiality of all Company
    information including but not limited to:
    - Customer data and personal information
    - Business strategies and plans
    - Financial information
    - Technical information and source code
    - Employee information
    - Investor information

8.2 The Employee shall not disclose any Confidential Information to any person
    during or after employment without prior written consent.

8.3 This obligation shall survive termination of employment indefinitely for
    trade secrets and for 5 years for other Confidential Information.

8.4 Breach of confidentiality may result in disciplinary action, termination,
    and legal action for damages.

================================================================================
                9. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
================================================================================

9.1 All intellectual property created by the Employee during employment and
    related to Company business shall belong exclusively to the Company.

9.2 The Employee assigns all rights in such intellectual property to the
    Company including copyrights, patents, and trade secrets.

9.3 The Employee shall promptly disclose all inventions and developments and
    execute documents required for registration.

9.4 The Employee shall not use Company intellectual property after termination.

================================================================================
                10. NON-COMPETE
================================================================================

10.1 During employment and for 12 months after termination, the Employee shall
     not, within India:
     (a) Work for or provide services to any Competing Business;
     (b) Establish or invest in any Competing Business;
     (c) Solicit Company customers or business partners.

10.2 "Competing Business" means any business engaged in digital lending, NBFC
     operations, or financial technology services competitive with the Company.

10.3 The Company may waive this restriction in writing or pay 6 months' salary
     for enforcement.

================================================================================
                11. NON-SOLICITATION
================================================================================

11.1 During employment and for 12 months after termination, the Employee shall
     not:
     (a) Solicit or recruit any Company employee;
     (b) Induce any employee to leave Company employment;
     (c) Interfere with Company's relationships with employees.

================================================================================
                12. TERMINATION
================================================================================

12.1 NOTICE PERIOD

| Status          | By Company      | By Employee     |
|-----------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| During Probation| 15 days         | 15 days         |
| After Confirmation| 90 days       | 90 days         |

12.2 PAYMENT IN LIEU OF NOTICE
     Either party may pay salary in lieu of notice period.

12.3 TERMINATION FOR CAUSE
     The Company may terminate immediately without notice or payment for:
     (a) Misconduct or insubordination
     (b) Fraud or dishonesty
     (c) Breach of Company policies
     (d) Conviction of criminal offense
     (e) Breach of confidentiality
     (f) Conflict of interest
     (g) Willful damage to Company property
     (h) Gross negligence

12.4 RESIGNATION PROCESS
     (a) Written resignation to reporting manager and HR
     (b) Proper handover of work and materials
     (c) Full and final settlement within 45 days
     (d) Exit interview and clearance

12.5 GARDEN LEAVE
     The Company may require the Employee to remain away from office during
     notice period while continuing to receive salary.

================================================================================
                13. RETURN OF PROPERTY

13.1 Upon termination, the Employee shall return:

     (b) ID card and access cards
     (c) Documents, files, and records
     (d) Customer lists and databases
     (e) All Confidential Information
     (f) Any other Company property

================================================================================
                14. BACKGROUND VERIFICATION
================================================================================

14.1 This offer is subject to satisfactory background verification including:
     (a) Identity verification
     (b) Education verification
     (c) Employment history verification
     (d) Criminal background check
     (e) Credit check (for relevant positions)
     (f) Reference checks

14.2 False information may result in termination.

================================================================================

     including RBI regulations applicable to NBFCs.

15.2 The Employee shall complete mandatory compliance training.

15.3 The Employee shall report any compliance violations.

================================================================================
                16. DATA PROTECTION
================================================================================

16.1 The Employee shall handle personal data in compliance with applicable data
     protection laws.

16.2 The Employee shall only access data necessary for job duties.

16.3 The Employee shall report any data breaches immediately.

17.2 If unresolved, disputes shall be referred to arbitration in Mumbai.

17.3 This Agreement shall be governed by laws of India.
