                       III. OBJECTS CLAUSE
================================================================================

A. THE MAIN OBJECTS TO BE PURSUED BY THE COMPANY ON ITS INCORPORATION:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. To carry on the business of a Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC) as
   defined under the Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, and to engage in
   lending, financing, and other financial activities as permitted by RBI.

2. To provide personal loans, consumer loans, business loans, and other
   credit facilities to individuals, sole proprietorships, partnerships,
   and corporate entities through digital platforms and technology-enabled
   channels.

3. To develop, own, operate, and maintain digital lending platforms, mobile
   applications, and web-based solutions for origination, underwriting,
   disbursement, and servicing of loans.

4. To employ artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data analytics
   for credit assessment, risk modeling, fraud detection, and customer
   service automation.

5. To act as a lending service provider (LSP) or digital lending app (DLA)
   in partnership with banks and other financial institutions as per RBI
   guidelines on Digital Lending.

6. To engage in co-lending arrangements with banks and other NBFCs for
   priority sector lending and other loan products.

7. To provide embedded finance solutions, buy-now-pay-later (BNPL) services,
   and point-of-sale financing to merchants and e-commerce platforms.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

B. OBJECTS INCIDENTAL OR ANCILLARY TO THE ATTAINMENT OF MAIN OBJECTS:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. To acquire, develop, license, and protect intellectual property rights
   including patents, copyrights, trademarks, and trade secrets related to
   financial technology.

2. To collect, process, and analyze data for credit scoring, risk assessment,
   and fraud prevention in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

3. To integrate with credit information companies (CICs) including CIBIL,
   Experian, Equifax, and CRIF High Mark for credit bureau services.

4. To partner with payment aggregators, payment gateways, and banking
   correspondents for loan disbursement and collection services.

5. To engage with third-party service providers for KYC verification,
   e-signing, video KYC, and Aadhaar-based authentication services.

6. To establish customer service centers, collection centers, and branch
   offices as required for business operations.

7. To raise capital through equity, debt, non-convertible debentures,
   commercial paper, external commercial borrowings, and securitization
   of loan assets.

8. To invest surplus funds in permitted investments as per RBI guidelines
   for NBFCs.

9. To enter into strategic partnerships, joint ventures, and business
   alliances with domestic and international financial institutions,
   technology companies, and fintech entities.

10. To acquire, merge with, or invest in other companies, firms, or entities
    engaged in similar or complementary businesses.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

C. OTHER OBJECTS:

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. To provide financial literacy programs, credit counseling, and debt
   management services to borrowers.

2. To develop and operate insurance distribution platforms as a corporate
   agent or web aggregator upon obtaining necessary licenses from IRDAI.

3. To engage in wealth management, investment advisory, and mutual fund
   distribution upon obtaining necessary registrations from SEBI.

4. To operate as an Account Aggregator upon obtaining license from RBI
   under the Account Aggregator framework.

5. To provide payment services and operate as a payment aggregator upon
   obtaining authorization from RBI.

6. To engage in cross-border remittances and forex services upon obtaining
   necessary licenses from RBI.

7. To undertake any other business activity as may be permitted by law and
   for which necessary licenses or approvals have been obtained.
