            1. POLICY OVERVIEW
================================================================================

1.1 PURPOSE
This Credit Policy establishes the framework for credit risk management,
underwriting standards, and approval processes for all lending products
offered by SmartFinance India Limited.

1.2 SCOPE
Applicable to all lending products including Personal Loans, Business Loans,
and Consumer Durable Loans originated through all channels.

1.3 OBJECTIVES
- Maintain portfolio quality with Gross NPA < 4%
- Achieve risk-adjusted returns with NIM > 7%
- Ensure regulatory compliance with RBI norms
- Balance growth with prudent risk management

4.3 NEGATIVE LIST (DO NOT LEND)

Industries:
- Real estate speculation
- Chit funds
- Gambling/betting
- Adult entertainment
- Cryptocurrency trading
- Loan against shares
- Political funding

Profiles:
- Willful defaulters (RBI list)
- Fraud cases (internal database)
- DPD 90+ in last 12 months
- Active legal cases
- Negative sectors under stress

================================================================================
            5. CREDIT ASSESSMENT
================================================================================

5.1 CREDIT BUREAU CHECK

| Bureau        | Score Used       | Min Score | Weightage |
|---------------|------------------|-----------|-----------|
| CIBIL         | TransUnion Score | 600       | 40%       |
| Experian      | Experian Score   | 600       | 25%       |
| Equifax       | Equifax Score    | 600       | 20%       |
| CRIF High Mark| CRIF Score       | 600       | 15%       |

Bureau Flags - Auto Decline:
- Written-off accounts in last 36 months
- Settlement in last 24 months
- DPD 90+ in last 12 months
- 5+ active unsecured loans
- 10+ inquiries in last 6 months

5.2 INCOME ASSESSMENT

Salaried:
- Salary slip (last 3 months)
- Bank statement (6 months)
- Form 16 / ITR (for high tickets)
- Income = Avg of last 3 months net salary

Self-Employed:
- ITR (last 2 years)
- GST returns (12 months)
- Bank statement (12 months)
- P&L and Balance Sheet
- Income = Average of last 2 years ITR income

5.3 FOIR CALCULATION

Fixed Obligation to Income Ratio = (All EMIs + Proposed EMI) / Net Monthly Income

| Risk Segment    | Max FOIR  |
|-----------------|-----------|
| Prime           | 50%       |
| Near-Prime      | 45%       |
| Sub-Prime / NTC | 40%       |

5.4 DEBT-BURDEN RATIO (for Business Loans)

DBR = Total Debt Service / EBITDA
Maximum DBR: 60%

================================================================================
            8. PRICING FRAMEWORK
================================================================================

RISK-BASED PRICING MODEL:

Base Rate: 10.5% (Benchmark - linked to cost of funds)

Risk Premium Matrix:

| Risk Factor           | Premium Range |
|-----------------------|---------------|
| CIBIL Score           | 0% - 8%       |
| FOIR Utilization      | 0% - 3%       |
| Customer Vintage      | 0% - 2%       |
| Product Type          | 0% - 4%       |
| Geographic Risk       | 0% - 2%       |

Final Rate = Base Rate + Sum of Risk Premiums
Capped at regulatory ceiling (as applicable)

================================================================================
            9. FRAUD PREVENTION
================================================================================

PRE-SANCTION CHECKS:
- Aadhaar OTP verification
- PAN verification via NSDL/UTIITSL
- Bank account verification (penny drop)
- Face match with ID photo
- Negative list check (internal + external)
- Dedupe check for multiple applications

POST-SANCTION CHECKS:
- Disbursement only to verified bank account
- No third-party disbursements
- Employment/business verification for high tickets
- Address verification (physical/digital)

================================================================================
            10. COLLECTIONS STRATEGY INTEGRATION
================================================================================

Early Warning Indicators:
- NACH bounce in Month 1
- Multiple NACH presentation failures
- Customer not reachable
- Bureau score drop >50 points

Proactive Action:
- Restructuring consideration for genuine hardship
- Top-up/refinance for good customers with temporary stress
- Accelerated collection for fraud indicators
