================================================================================
                    OFFICE LEASE AGREEMENT
                              OF
                  SMARTFINANCE INDIA LIMITED

                    REGISTERED OFFICE PREMISES
================================================================================

This Lease Deed ("Agreement") is executed on this 1st day of June, 2020

BETWEEN:

ONE BKC OFFICE PARKS PRIVATE LIMITED, a company incorporated under the
Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at One BKC, Plot C-66, G
Block, Bandra Kurla Complex, Mumbai - 400051, represented by its Authorized
Signatory (hereinafter referred to as the "Lessor")

AND

SMARTFINANCE INDIA LIMITED, a company incorporated under the Companies Act,
2013, having its registered office at 15th Floor, Tower B, One BKC, Bandra
Kurla Complex, Mumbai - 400051 (hereinafter referred to as the "Lessee")

================================================================================
                         RECITALS
================================================================================

A. The Lessor is the owner of the commercial property known as One BKC situated
   at Bandra Kurla Complex, Mumbai.

B. The Lessee is engaged in the business of providing digital lending and
   financial services and requires office space for its operations.

C. The Lessor has agreed to lease the Demised Premises to the Lessee on the
   terms and conditions set out herein.

================================================================================
                    1. DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

1.1 "Building" means Tower B, One BKC, Bandra Kurla Complex, Mumbai - 400051.

1.2 "Carpet Area" means 8,500 square feet.

1.3 "Common Areas" means lobbies, lifts, staircases, parking, and other shared
    facilities.

1.4 "Demised Premises" means the office space on 15th Floor, Tower B, One BKC.

1.5 "Fit-Out Period" means 60 days from handover for interior fit-out.

1.6 "Lease Term" means the period of lease as specified in Clause 3.

1.7 "Lock-In Period" means the initial period during which neither party can
    terminate.

1.8 "Rent" means the monthly rent payable by the Lessee.

1.9 "Security Deposit" means the interest-free refundable deposit.

================================================================================
                    2. DEMISED PREMISES
================================================================================

The Lessor hereby demises and leases to the Lessee the following premises:

| Parameter              | Details                                      |
|------------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| Location               | 15th Floor, Tower B, One BKC                 |
| Address                | Bandra Kurla Complex, Mumbai - 400051        |
| Carpet Area            | 8,500 sq. ft.                                |
| Super Built-Up Area    | 12,750 sq. ft. (1.5x loading)                |
| Car Parking            | 15 designated slots in basement              |
| Two-Wheeler Parking    | 20 slots                                     |

The premises shall be delivered in warm shell condition with:
- False ceiling grid
- Electrical points to floor
- Air-conditioning provision
- Fire safety systems
- Access control system

================================================================================
                    5. SECURITY DEPOSIT
================================================================================

5.1 AMOUNT
    Security Deposit: ₹1,91,25,000 (equivalent to 6 months' base rent)

5.2 PAYMENT SCHEDULE
    - ₹95,62,500 at signing of this Agreement
    - ₹95,62,500 at handover of premises

5.3 TERMS
    - Interest-free refundable deposit
    - Adjustable against last 3 months' rent
    - Balance refundable within 60 days of vacation
    - Subject to deduction for damages and dues

================================================================================
                    6. PAYMENT TERMS
================================================================================

6.1 Rent shall be payable monthly in advance by 5th of each month.

6.2 Payment to be made via NEFT/RTGS to:
    Account Name: One BKC Office Parks Private Limited
    Account No.: 1234567890
    Bank: HDFC Bank, BKC Branch
    IFSC: HDFC0001234

6.3 Interest on delayed payment: 18% p.a.

6.4 TDS to be deducted as per Income Tax Act and certificates provided quarterly.

================================================================================
                    7. UTILITIES
================================================================================

7.1 ELECTRICITY
    - Separate meter for Demised Premises
    - Direct billing from BEST
    - DG backup: 100% for lighting, 50% for AC

7.2 WATER
    - Included in CAM charges
    - Additional charges for excessive use

7.3 TELECOMMUNICATIONS
    - Lessee to arrange own telecom and internet services
    - Building has fiber connectivity from multiple providers

================================================================================
                    8. FIT-OUT AND ALTERATIONS
================================================================================

8.1 The Lessee shall be entitled to a rent-free fit-out period of 60 days.

8.2 The Lessee may undertake interior fit-out subject to:
    - Prior approval of plans by Lessor
    - Compliance with building regulations
    - Use of Lessor's empaneled contractors for MEP works
    - No structural alterations

8.3 Estimated fit-out cost borne by Lessee: ₹1,500 - 2,000 per sq. ft.

8.4 Upon termination, Lessee may either:
    - Remove fit-outs and restore to original condition, or
    - Leave fit-outs as is if Lessor agrees

================================================================================
                    9. USE OF PREMISES
================================================================================

9.1 The Demised Premises shall be used only for:
    - Corporate office
    - Back-office operations
    - Technology development center
    - Related lawful business activities

9.2 Prohibited activities:
    - Manufacturing or industrial use
    - Retail or customer-facing operations
    - Storage of hazardous materials
    - Any illegal activity

================================================================================
                    10. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
================================================================================

10.1 LESSOR'S RESPONSIBILITY
     - Structural repairs and maintenance
     - Common area upkeep
     - Building systems (lifts, AC plant, fire safety)
     - External façade

10.2 LESSEE'S RESPONSIBILITY
     - Interior maintenance
     - Fit-out maintenance
     - Repairs due to Lessee's negligence
     - Day-to-day upkeep of Demised Premises

================================================================================
                    11. INSURANCE
================================================================================

11.1 LESSOR'S INSURANCE
     - Building structure and common areas
     - Public liability for common areas

11.2 LESSEE'S INSURANCE
     - Contents and fit-outs: Sum insured of ₹2 Crores
     - Public liability: Sum insured of ₹5 Crores
     - Workmen compensation for Lessee's employees

================================================================================
                    12. TERMINATION
================================================================================

12.1 TERMINATION BY LESSOR
     - Material breach not cured within 30 days
     - Non-payment of rent for 60 days
     - Insolvency or winding-up of Lessee
     - Use for prohibited purposes

12.2 TERMINATION BY LESSEE
     - Post lock-in with 6 months' notice
     - Material breach by Lessor not cured within 30 days

12.3 CONSEQUENCES OF TERMINATION
     - Settlement of all dues within 30 days
     - Vacation of premises within notice period
     - Return of security deposit per Clause 5.3

================================================================================
                    13. ASSIGNMENT AND SUBLETTING
================================================================================

13.1 The Lessee shall not assign, transfer, or sublet the Demised Premises
     without prior written consent of the Lessor.

13.2 Assignment to affiliates permitted with 30 days' prior notice.

================================================================================
                    14. COMPLIANCE
================================================================================

14.1 The Lessee shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

14.2 The Lessee shall obtain all necessary licenses and permits for its
     business operations.

14.3 The Lessee shall comply with building rules and regulations.

================================================================================
                    15. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
================================================================================

15.1 Disputes shall first be resolved through good faith negotiations.

15.2 If unresolved within 30 days, disputes shall be referred to arbitration
     under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

15.3 Seat of arbitration: Mumbai
     Language: English

================================================================================
                    16. REGISTRATION
================================================================================

16.1 This Agreement shall be registered with the Sub-Registrar of Assurances,
     Mumbai.

16.2 Registration charges and stamp duty shall be borne equally by both parties.
