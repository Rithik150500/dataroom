================================================================================
            1. SARFAESI ACT APPLICABILITY
================================================================================

The Securitisation and Reconstruction of Financial Assets and Enforcement of
Security Interest Act, 2002 (SARFAESI Act) applies to SmartFinance India Limited
as a registered NBFC for recovery of secured loans exceeding ₹1 lakh.

ELIGIBILITY:
- SmartFinance is registered with RBI as NBFC (Reg. No. N-13.02216)
- Applicable for secured loans where principal outstanding > ₹1,00,000
- Not applicable for: Agricultural land, unsecured loans, loans < ₹1 lakh

================================================================================
            2. SARFAESI PROCESS WORKFLOW
================================================================================

STAGE 1: CLASSIFICATION AS NPA
- Account classified as NPA after 90 days past due (DPD)
- Review by Credit/Recovery Committee
- Decision on SARFAESI initiation

STAGE 2: DEMAND NOTICE UNDER SECTION 13(2)
- Issue 60-day demand notice to borrower
- Notice to guarantor and co-borrower
- Notice to be sent via registered post/speed post and email
- Public notice in two newspapers (one vernacular)

STAGE 3: POSSESSION NOTICE UNDER SECTION 13(4)
- After 60 days, if no payment/settlement
- Take possession of secured assets
- Physical possession with police assistance if required
- Symbolic possession for immovable property

STAGE 4: SALE OF ASSETS
- Public auction after 30 days from possession
- Reserve price based on valuation (min 2 valuers)
- E-auction through authorized platforms
- Sale proceeds appropriated towards dues

            11. RECOVERY AGENTS - CODE OF CONDUCT
================================================================================

All recovery agents must comply with:

1. RBI Fair Practice Code for Recovery
2. No calls before 8:00 AM or after 7:00 PM
3. No use of abusive language or threats
4. No contact at borrower's workplace without consent
5. Proper identification and authorization letter
6. No third-party disclosure of default
7. Recording of all recovery calls
8. GPS tracking for field visits
