            1. ALM POLICY FRAMEWORK
================================================================================

POLICY OBJECTIVE:
To manage liquidity risk, interest rate risk, and funding risk within
acceptable limits while ensuring sustainable profitability.

REGULATORY FRAMEWORK:
- RBI Master Direction on Liquidity Risk Management Framework for NBFCs
- RBI Guidelines on ALM System for NBFCs
- Scale Based Regulation for NBFCs

POLICY APPROVAL:
- Board approved: April 1, 2024
- Last reviewed: October 15, 2024
- Next review: April 2025
