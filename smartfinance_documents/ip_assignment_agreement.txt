================================================================================
                     RECITALS
================================================================================

A. The Assignor is one of the founders of the Company and has developed certain
   intellectual property, including software code, algorithms, designs, and
   business concepts prior to and during the incorporation of the Company.

B. The Assignor desires to assign all such intellectual property to the Company
   to ensure that the Company owns all rights necessary for its business.

C. The Company desires to acquire such intellectual property on the terms set
   forth herein.

================================================================================
                1. DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

1.1 "Assigned IP" means all intellectual property described in Schedule A,
    including but not limited to:
    - Software source code and object code
    - Algorithms and data models
    - User interface designs
    - Technical documentation
    - Business plans and strategies
    - Customer lists and data
    - Trade secrets and know-how
    - Trademarks and service marks
    - Domain names
    - Any other intellectual property

1.2 "Derivative Works" means any improvements, modifications, or enhancements
    to the Assigned IP.

1.3 "Intellectual Property Rights" means all patents, copyrights, trademarks,
    trade secrets, and other proprietary rights.

================================================================================
                2. ASSIGNMENT
================================================================================

2.1 ASSIGNMENT OF IP

    The Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to the
    Company, its successors and assigns, all right, title, and interest in
    and to the Assigned IP, including all Intellectual Property Rights therein.

2.2 SCOPE OF ASSIGNMENT

    The assignment includes:
    (a) Worldwide, perpetual, exclusive rights
    (b) Right to use, reproduce, modify, distribute, and display
    (c) Right to create Derivative Works
    (d) Right to sublicense
    (e) Right to enforce Intellectual Property Rights
    (f) All causes of action and rights to sue for past infringement

2.3 FUTURE DEVELOPMENTS

    Any intellectual property developed by the Assignor in connection with
    the Company's business after the date hereof shall automatically vest
    in the Company.

================================================================================
                3. CONSIDERATION
================================================================================

3.1 In consideration for the assignment, the Company shall:
    (a) Issue Equity Shares to the Assignor as per the Subscription Agreement
    (b) Provide employment/engagement to the Assignor as Managing Director

3.2 The Assignor acknowledges that the consideration is adequate.

================================================================================
                4. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES
================================================================================

4.1 The Assignor represents and warrants that:

    (a) The Assignor is the sole owner of the Assigned IP.

    (b) The Assigned IP is free from any liens, claims, or encumbrances.

    (c) The Assigned IP does not infringe any third-party rights.

    (d) The Assignor has not granted any licenses to third parties.

    (e) There is no litigation pending or threatened regarding the Assigned IP.

    (f) The Assignor has all necessary rights to make this assignment.

================================================================================
                5. FURTHER ASSURANCES
================================================================================

5.1 The Assignor agrees to:

    (a) Execute any additional documents required to perfect the assignment.

    (b) Cooperate with the Company in registration and enforcement of IP rights.

    (c) Provide all source code, documentation, and materials.

    (d) Not challenge the Company's ownership of the Assigned IP.

5.2 Power of Attorney

    The Assignor hereby irrevocably appoints the Company as attorney-in-fact
    to execute any documents necessary to register or protect the Assigned IP.

================================================================================
                6. CONFIDENTIALITY
================================================================================

6.1 The Assignor shall maintain confidentiality of all Assigned IP and shall
    not disclose it to any third party without the Company's prior consent.

================================================================================
                7. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION
================================================================================

7.1 For a period of 24 months from termination of employment/engagement, the
    Assignor shall not:
    (a) Engage in any competing business in India
    (b) Solicit employees or customers of the Company
    (c) Use or disclose the Assigned IP

================================================================================
                8. INDEMNIFICATION
================================================================================

8.1 The Assignor shall indemnify the Company against any claims arising from:
    (a) Breach of representations and warranties
    (b) Third-party IP infringement claims
    (c) Prior unauthorized disclosure of Assigned IP

================================================================================
            EMPLOYEE IP ASSIGNMENT AGREEMENT
                 (Template for All Employees)
================================================================================

This Agreement is entered into by each employee upon joining SmartFinance India
Limited.

KEY TERMS:

1. SCOPE
   All intellectual property created during employment and related to Company
   business shall belong to the Company.

2. WORK FOR HIRE
   All works created within scope of employment are works for hire under
   Copyright Act.

3. ASSIGNMENT
   Employee assigns all IP rights that do not vest automatically.

4. DISCLOSURE
   Employee shall promptly disclose all inventions and developments.

5. ASSISTANCE
   Employee shall assist with patent applications and registrations.

6. MORAL RIGHTS
   Employee waives moral rights to the extent permitted by law.

7. RETURN OF MATERIALS
   Upon termination, employee shall return all Company materials and IP.

8. PRE-EXISTING IP
   Employee must disclose pre-existing IP at time of joining; such IP excluded
   from assignment unless used in Company work.
