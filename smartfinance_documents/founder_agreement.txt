                          OF
              SMARTFINANCE INDIA LIMITED
================================================================================

1. MR. VIKRAM MEHTA, residing at 45, Malabar Hill, Mumbai - 400006
   (hereinafter referred to as "Founder 1")

2. MS. ANJALI DESHMUKH, residing at 12, Carter Road, Bandra West,

3. MR. ROHAN KAPOOR, residing at 78, Linking Road, Santacruz West,

(Collectively referred to as "Founders" or "Parties")

================================================================================
                     RECITALS
================================================================================

A. The Founders have come together to establish and build a digital lending
   platform to provide accessible credit to underserved segments in India.

B. The Founders intend to incorporate a company under the name "SmartFinance
   India Limited" (the "Company") to carry on this business.

C. The Founders wish to record their understanding regarding their respective
   roles, responsibilities, contributions, and the terms governing their
   relationship as founders of the Company.

================================================================================
            1. FORMATION OF COMPANY
================================================================================

1.1 The Founders agree to incorporate the Company under the Companies Act, 2013
    as a public limited company with registered office in Mumbai.

1.2 The initial authorized share capital shall be ₹1,50,00,000 (One Crore Fifty
    Lakhs) divided into:
    - 50,00,000 Equity Shares of ₹10 each
    - 10,00,000 Preference Shares of ₹100 each

================================================================================
            2. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
================================================================================

2.1 FOUNDER ROLES

| Founder      | Designation               | Primary Responsibility         |
|--------------|---------------------------|--------------------------------|
| Vikram Mehta | Managing Director & CEO   | Overall strategy, investor     |
|              |                           | relations, business development|
| Anjali       | Whole-time Director & CFO | Finance, compliance, treasury, |
| Deshmukh     |                           | fundraising                    |
| Rohan Kapoor | Director & CTO            | Technology, product, data      |
|              |                           | science                        |

2.2 FULL-TIME COMMITMENT
    Each Founder shall devote full time and attention to the Company's business
    and shall not engage in any other business or employment without prior
    written consent of other Founders.

2.3 KEY DECISIONS
    The following decisions require unanimous Founder consent:
    (a) Changes to business plan or strategy
    (b) Hiring of C-level executives
    (c) Material contracts exceeding ₹10 lakhs
    (d) Related party transactions
    (e) Capital expenditure exceeding ₹25 lakhs
    (f) Incurring debt exceeding ₹25 lakhs
    (g) Changes to Founder roles or compensation

================================================================================
            3. EQUITY OWNERSHIP
================================================================================

3.1 INITIAL EQUITY ALLOCATION

| Founder          | Shares     | Percentage | Consideration (₹) |
|------------------|------------|------------|-------------------|
| Vikram Mehta     | 5,00,000   | 50%        | 50,00,000         |
| Anjali Deshmukh  | 3,00,000   | 30%        | 30,00,000         |
| Rohan Kapoor     | 2,00,000   | 20%        | 20,00,000         |
|------------------|------------|------------|-------------------|
| Total            | 10,00,000  | 100%       | 1,00,00,000       |

3.2 FOUNDER VESTING

    All Founder shares shall be subject to reverse vesting over 4 years:

    | Period        | Vested Cumulative |
    |---------------|-------------------|
    | Cliff (1 year)| 25%               |
    | Month 13-48   | 2.083% per month  |
    | After 4 years | 100%              |

3.3 ACCELERATION
    Vesting shall accelerate 100% upon:
    (a) Change of control
    (b) IPO
    (c) Termination without cause
    (d) Death or disability

3.4 UNVESTED SHARES ON DEPARTURE
    If a Founder departs before full vesting:
    - Unvested shares to be purchased by remaining Founders or Company
    - Price: Lower of original price or fair market value
    - Payment: Within 180 days

================================================================================
            4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
================================================================================

4.1 Each Founder shall execute an IP Assignment Agreement transferring all
    intellectual property related to the Company's business.

4.2 The Company shall own all intellectual property developed by Founders.

4.3 Pre-existing IP to be licensed to Company on royalty-free, perpetual basis.

================================================================================
            5. FOUNDER COMPENSATION
================================================================================

5.1 INITIAL COMPENSATION

| Founder          | Monthly CTC    | Annual CTC     |
|------------------|----------------|----------------|
| Vikram Mehta     | ₹2,50,000      | ₹30,00,000     |
| Anjali Deshmukh  | ₹2,00,000      | ₹24,00,000     |
| Rohan Kapoor     | ₹2,00,000      | ₹24,00,000     |

5.2 No Founder shall receive additional compensation (fees, commissions,
    bonuses) without consent of other Founders.

5.3 Compensation may be revised based on Company performance and upon mutual
    agreement.

5.4 Each Founder shall bear their own taxes.

================================================================================
            6. EXPENSES
================================================================================

6.1 Founders shall be entitled to reimbursement of reasonable business expenses
    incurred for Company purposes.

6.2 Expenses exceeding ₹50,000 per month require prior approval.

6.3 All expenses must be supported by receipts and documentation.

================================================================================
            7. NON-COMPETE
================================================================================

7.1 During the term of this Agreement and for 24 months after departure,
    no Founder shall:
    (a) Engage in any Competing Business in India
    (b) Invest in any Competing Business (except up to 1% in public companies)
    (c) Provide services to any Competing Business

7.2 "Competing Business" means any business engaged in digital lending,
    consumer lending, or NBFC operations in India.

================================================================================
            8. NON-SOLICITATION
================================================================================

8.1 During the term and for 24 months after departure, no Founder shall:
    (a) Solicit or recruit any employee of the Company
    (b) Induce any employee to leave the Company
    (c) Solicit any customer or partner of the Company

================================================================================
            9. CONFIDENTIALITY
================================================================================

9.1 Each Founder shall maintain strict confidentiality of all Company
    information including but not limited to:
    - Business plans and strategies
    - Financial information
    - Customer and partner data
    - Technical information
    - This Agreement

9.2 This obligation shall survive termination indefinitely.

================================================================================
            10. FOUNDER DEPARTURE
================================================================================

10.1 VOLUNTARY DEPARTURE

     If a Founder voluntarily departs:
     (a) Departing Founder forfeits unvested shares
     (b) Remaining Founders have option to purchase vested shares
     - Option period: 90 days
     - Price: Fair market value
     - Payment: 50% immediately, 50% over 12 months

10.2 TERMINATION FOR CAUSE

     A Founder may be removed for cause by unanimous vote of other Founders:
     (a) Fraud or dishonesty
     (b) Material breach of this Agreement
     (c) Conviction of crime
     (d) Gross negligence
     (e) Non-performance of duties

     Consequences:
     - Immediate termination of employment
     - Forfeiture of all unvested shares
     - Vested shares purchased at lower of original price or FMV

10.3 TERMINATION WITHOUT CAUSE

     If a Founder is terminated without cause:
     (a) Full acceleration of vesting
     (b) 6 months' salary severance
     (c) Right to sell shares at fair market value

10.4 DRAG-ALONG AND TAG-ALONG

     Standard drag-along and tag-along rights shall apply to all Founders.

================================================================================
            11. DECISION MAKING
================================================================================

11.1 MATTERS REQUIRING UNANIMOUS CONSENT

     As set out in Clause 2.3.

11.2 DEADLOCK RESOLUTION

     If Founders cannot reach unanimous decision:
     (a) Good faith negotiation for 15 days
     (b) Mediation by independent mediator for 30 days
     (c) If unresolved, CEO (Vikram) shall have casting vote, except for:
         - Changes to this Agreement
         - Founder compensation
         - Removal of any Founder

================================================================================
            12. FUNDRAISING
================================================================================

12.1 Founders agree to pursue external funding to grow the Company.

12.2 All Founders shall participate in investor discussions and due diligence.

12.3 Terms of external funding require unanimous Founder consent.

12.4 Founders shall execute all documents required by investors.

12.5 Founders acknowledge that external funding will dilute their ownership.

================================================================================
            13. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES
================================================================================

Each Founder represents and warrants that:

13.1 They have full power and authority to enter into this Agreement.

13.2 They are not subject to any agreement that would prevent performance.

13.3 They have disclosed all material conflicts of interest.

13.4 They will not engage in any activity harmful to the Company.

================================================================================
            14. INDEMNIFICATION
================================================================================

14.1 Each Founder shall indemnify and hold harmless the other Founders and
     the Company against claims arising from their breach of this Agreement.

================================================================================
            15. TERM AND TERMINATION
================================================================================

15.1 This Agreement shall come into effect on the date hereof and continue
     until:
     (a) Mutual written agreement of all Founders
     (b) Only one Founder remains
     (c) Liquidation or dissolution of the Company
     (d) IPO of the Company

15.2 Provisions regarding confidentiality, non-compete, non-solicitation,
     and indemnification shall survive termination.

================================================================================
            16. SUPERSEDING AGREEMENTS
================================================================================

16.1 This Agreement shall be superseded and replaced by the Shareholders
     Agreement upon external equity fundraising.

16.2 Upon execution of Shareholders Agreement, conflicting provisions of this
     Agreement shall be void; consistent provisions shall continue.

================================================================================
            17. MISCELLANEOUS
================================================================================

17.1 GOVERNING LAW
     This Agreement shall be governed by laws of India.

17.2 JURISDICTION
     Courts in Mumbai shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

17.3 NOTICES
     All notices shall be in writing and sent to addresses above.

17.4 AMENDMENT
     This Agreement may be amended only by written instrument signed by all
     Founders.

17.5 ENTIRE AGREEMENT
     This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement among the Founders.

17.6 SEVERABILITY
     Invalid provisions shall be severed; remaining provisions continue.

17.7 WAIVER
     No waiver of any breach shall constitute waiver of any other breach.

17.8 COUNTERPARTS
     This Agreement may be executed in counterparts.
