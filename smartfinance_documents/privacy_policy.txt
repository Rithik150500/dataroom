                    1. INTRODUCTION
================================================================================

SmartFinance India Limited ("SmartFinance", "Company", "we", "us", "our")
is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals ("you", "your", "user",
"borrower") who use our services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect,
use, disclose, and protect your personal data when you use our website
(www.smartfinance.in), mobile application ("App"), and lending services.

This policy is published in compliance with:
- Information Technology Act, 2000
- Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices) Rules, 2011
- Reserve Bank of India Guidelines on Digital Lending
- Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023

================================================================================
                    3. PURPOSE OF DATA COLLECTION
================================================================================

We collect and use your personal data for the following purposes:

3.1 LOAN PROCESSING
- Verify your identity and eligibility
- Assess creditworthiness and determine loan terms
- Process loan applications and disbursements
- Service and manage your loan account

3.2 LEGAL AND REGULATORY COMPLIANCE
- Comply with RBI regulations for NBFCs
- KYC/AML compliance
- Report to credit bureaus
- Tax compliance and reporting
- Respond to legal requests

3.3 CUSTOMER SERVICE
- Respond to inquiries and complaints
- Provide account information
- Send transaction alerts and reminders
- Resolve disputes

3.4 RISK MANAGEMENT
- Fraud prevention and detection
- Credit risk assessment
- Collection and recovery

3.5 BUSINESS IMPROVEMENT
- Analyze usage patterns
- Improve products and services
- Develop new offerings
- Conduct research and analytics

3.6 MARKETING (with consent)
- Send promotional communications
- Personalize offers
- Cross-sell and upsell products

================================================================================
                    6. DATA RETENTION
================================================================================

We retain your data for the following periods:

| Data Type             | Retention Period                                 |
|-----------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| KYC Documents         | 5 years after relationship ends                  |
| Loan Records          | 8 years after loan closure                       |
| Transaction Records   | 8 years from transaction date                    |
| Credit Bureau Data    | As per credit bureau norms                       |
| Communications        | 5 years                                          |
| Marketing Consents    | Until withdrawn                                  |
| Unsuccessful Apps     | 1 year from rejection                            |

After the retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

================================================================================
                    7. DATA SECURITY
================================================================================

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

7.1 TECHNICAL MEASURES
- Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+)
- Multi-factor authentication
- Access controls and audit logs
- Intrusion detection systems
- Regular security testing and audits
- Data loss prevention tools

7.2 ORGANIZATIONAL MEASURES
- Information security policy
- Employee background verification
- Regular security training
- Incident response procedures
- Vendor security assessments

7.3 CERTIFICATIONS
- ISO 27001:2013 (In Progress)
- SOC 2 Type II (Planned)
- PCI-DSS compliant payment processing
