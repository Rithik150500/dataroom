              APPOINTMENT LETTER - MANAGING DIRECTOR

Date: June 10, 2020

To,
Mr. Vikram Mehta
45, Malabar Hill

Dear Mr. Mehta,

            Re: Appointment as Managing Director & Chief Executive Officer

We are pleased to inform you that the Board of Directors of SmartFinance India
Limited ("Company") at its meeting held on June 8, 2020, has appointed you as
the Managing Director & Chief Executive Officer of the Company with effect from
June 10, 2020, subject to the terms and conditions set out herein.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                         TERMS OF APPOINTMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. DESIGNATION AND TERM

   You are appointed as Managing Director & CEO for a term of five (5) years
   from June 10, 2020 to June 9, 2025, unless terminated earlier in accordance
   with this letter or applicable law.

2. REMUNERATION

   Your remuneration shall be as follows:

   | Component              | Monthly (₹)    | Annual (₹)      |
   |------------------------|----------------|-----------------|
   | Basic Salary           | 3,00,000       | 36,00,000       |
   | House Rent Allowance   | 1,50,000       | 18,00,000       |
   | Special Allowance      | 1,00,000       | 12,00,000       |
   | Performance Bonus      | Variable       | Up to 50% of CTC|
   |------------------------|----------------|-----------------|
   | Total Fixed CTC        | 5,50,000       | 66,00,000       |

   Note: Remuneration subject to RBI guidelines on MD/CEO compensation for NBFCs.

3. STOCK OPTIONS

   You shall be entitled to stock options under the SmartFinance ESOP Scheme
   2021, subject to approval by the Nomination and Remuneration Committee.

4. DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

   (a) Overall management and operations of the Company
   (b) Implementation of Board decisions and strategies
   (c) Ensuring regulatory compliance, particularly RBI requirements
   (d) Representing the Company to investors, regulators, and stakeholders
   (e) Building and leading the senior management team
   (f) Risk management and governance oversight
   (g) Any other duties assigned by the Board

5. EXCLUSIVITY

   During your tenure, you shall devote your full time and attention to the
   affairs of the Company and shall not engage in any other employment or
   business without prior written consent of the Board.

6. COMPLIANCE

   You shall comply with the fit and proper criteria prescribed by RBI for
   Managing Directors of NBFCs and shall provide all necessary declarations.

7. TERMINATION

   (a) By Company: With 6 months' notice or payment in lieu thereof
   (b) By You: With 6 months' notice
   (c) Immediate termination for cause without notice or payment

8. POST-TERMINATION OBLIGATIONS

   (a) Non-compete: 12 months within India in digital lending
   (b) Non-solicitation: 12 months for employees and customers
   (c) Confidentiality: Perpetual

Please confirm your acceptance by signing the duplicate copy of this letter.

Dear Ms. Sanghvi,

            Re: Appointment as Independent Director

We are pleased to inform you that the Board of Directors of SmartFinance India
Limited ("Company") at its meeting held on August 25, 2022, has appointed you
as an Independent Director of the Company, subject to approval of shareholders
at the ensuing General Meeting.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                         TERMS OF APPOINTMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. TERM

   You are appointed for a term of five (5) consecutive years from September 1,
   2022 to August 31, 2027, not liable to retire by rotation.

2. ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITIES

   (a) Attend Board meetings and Committee meetings
   (b) Bring independent judgment on matters of strategy, performance, risk
   (c) Scrutinize management performance and company affairs
   (d) Satisfy yourself on integrity of financial information
   (e) Ensure appropriate disclosure to shareholders
   (f) Chair Audit Committee and Nomination & Remuneration Committee

3. COMMITTEE MEMBERSHIPS

   (a) Chairperson - Audit Committee
   (b) Chairperson - Nomination & Remuneration Committee
   (c) Member - Risk Management Committee

4. SITTING FEES

   | Meeting Type              | Sitting Fee (₹)  |
   |---------------------------|------------------|
   | Board Meeting             | 50,000           |
   | Audit Committee           | 30,000           |
   | Other Committees          | 20,000           |

5. INDEPENDENCE DECLARATION

   You shall provide an annual declaration of independence as required under
   Section 149(6) of the Companies Act, 2013 and Regulation 16 of SEBI LODR
   (as applicable).

6. FIT AND PROPER

   As an Independent Director of an NBFC, you shall comply with RBI's fit and
   proper criteria and shall not be disqualified under Section 164 of the Act.

7. TRAINING

   The Company shall provide familiarization programs on Company's operations,
   regulatory framework for NBFCs, and industry updates.

8. D&O INSURANCE

   The Company maintains Directors & Officers liability insurance with coverage
   of ₹10 Crores per annum.

9. TERMINATION

   Your appointment may be terminated:
   (a) By resignation with 30 days' notice
   (b) By shareholders through ordinary resolution
   (c) Upon disqualification under the Act or RBI guidelines

Please confirm your acceptance by signing the duplicate copy and returning it
along with the following documents:
- Declaration of Independence (Form DIR-8)
- Consent to act as Director (Form DIR-2)
- Fit and Proper declaration for RBI

            Re: Appointment as Nominee Director

meeting held on June 30, 2021, has appointed you as a Nominee Director of the

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                         TERMS OF APPOINTMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. NOMINATING INVESTOR

   Matrix Partners India II LLP

2. TERM

   Your appointment shall continue as long as Matrix Partners India II LLP
   holds at least 5% of the fully diluted share capital of the Company.

3. ROLE

   (a) Represent the interests of the nominating investor
   (b) Participate in Board and Committee meetings
   (c) Provide strategic guidance and oversight
   (d) Vote on matters as per fiduciary duties

4. COMMITTEE MEMBERSHIPS

   (a) Member - Audit Committee
   (b) Member - Nomination & Remuneration Committee

5. SITTING FEES

   As a Nominee Director representing an investor, you shall not be entitled
   to sitting fees. Any sitting fees that may become payable shall be directed
   to Matrix Partners India II LLP.

6. EXPENSES

   The Company shall reimburse reasonable travel and accommodation expenses
   for attending Board meetings.

7. COMPLIANCE

   (a) Provide Fit and Proper declaration as required by RBI for NBFC Directors
   (b) Comply with confidentiality obligations
   (c) Disclose any conflicts of interest

8. TERMINATION

   Your appointment shall terminate upon:
   (a) Written notice from Matrix Partners India II LLP
   (b) Matrix Partners holding less than 5% of fully diluted capital
   (c) Disqualification under Companies Act or RBI guidelines
