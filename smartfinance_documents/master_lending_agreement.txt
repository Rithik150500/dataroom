1.12 "Penal Charges" means charges payable for delay in payment of EMI.

1.13 "Prepayment" means payment of Outstanding Amount before Maturity Date.

================================================================================
                    3. DISBURSEMENT
================================================================================

3.1 The Lender shall disburse the Loan amount to the Borrower Account after:
    (a) Execution of this Agreement
    (b) Completion of KYC verification
    (c) Registration of E-NACH mandate
    (d) Payment of processing fee
    (e) Fulfillment of any other conditions

3.2 The Lender may disburse the Loan in one or more tranches.

3.3 The Borrower shall utilize the Loan only for the purpose declared in the
    loan application.

================================================================================
                    4. INTEREST AND CHARGES
================================================================================

4.1 INTEREST CALCULATION
    Interest shall be calculated on reducing balance method from the
    Disbursement Date until full repayment.

4.2 PROCESSING FEE
    A non-refundable processing fee as specified in the Schedule shall be
    deducted from the Loan amount at disbursement.

4.3 PENAL CHARGES
    In case of delay in payment of EMI, penal charges at the rate specified
    in the Schedule shall be levied on the overdue amount.

4.4 OTHER CHARGES
    | Charge Type              | Amount                     |
    |--------------------------|----------------------------|
    | Bounce Charges           | ₹500 per bounce            |
    | Prepayment Charges       | As per Schedule            |
    | Document Retrieval       | ₹500 per request           |
    | Duplicate Statement      | ₹100 per statement         |
    | GST                      | As applicable              |

4.5 INTEREST RATE REVISION
    The Lender reserves the right to revise the Interest Rate with 30 days'
    prior notice to the Borrower. The revised rate shall apply from the next
    EMI due date.

================================================================================
                    5. REPAYMENT
================================================================================

5.1 The Borrower shall repay the Loan by way of EMIs as per the repayment
    schedule provided.

5.2 EMIs shall be auto-debited from the Borrower Account on the due date
    through E-NACH mandate.

5.3 In case of insufficient balance in the Borrower Account, the Borrower
    shall ensure payment through other modes within 3 days.

5.4 Payments shall be appropriated in the following order:
    (a) Charges and fees
    (b) Penal charges
    (c) Interest
    (d) Principal

5.5 PREPAYMENT
    The Borrower may prepay the Outstanding Amount subject to:
    (a) Minimum 3 EMIs paid
    (b) Prepayment charges as per Schedule
    (c) Prior notice of 7 days
    (d) Full prepayment only (no part prepayment)

6.1 All information provided in the loan application is true and complete.

7.4 Cooperate with the Lender for recovery proceedings if required.

7.5 Provide additional documents or information as requested by the Lender.

7.6 Not assign or transfer obligations under this Agreement.

7.7 Comply with all Applicable Laws.

================================================================================
                    10. EVENTS OF DEFAULT
================================================================================

The following shall constitute Events of Default:

10.1 Non-payment of EMI or any amount due for more than 7 days.

10.2 Breach of any representation, warranty, or covenant.

10.3 Providing false or misleading information.

10.4 Borrower becoming insolvent or filing for bankruptcy.

10.5 Death or incapacity of the Borrower (for individuals).

10.6 Any adverse material change in the Borrower's financial condition.

10.7 Non-cooperation in recovery proceedings.

10.8 Cross-default on any other loan or credit facility.

================================================================================
                    11. CONSEQUENCES OF DEFAULT
================================================================================

Upon occurrence of an Event of Default, the Lender may:

11.1 Declare the entire Outstanding Amount immediately due and payable.

11.2 Charge penal interest on the Outstanding Amount.

11.3 Report the default to credit information companies.

11.4 Initiate legal proceedings for recovery.

11.5 Engage recovery agents to collect the Outstanding Amount.

11.6 Exercise any other remedy available under law.
