            1. INTRODUCTION AND COMPANY VALUES
================================================================================

ABOUT SMARTFINANCE

SmartFinance India Limited is a technology-driven NBFC committed to making
credit accessible to underserved segments through innovative digital solutions.

MISSION
To democratize access to credit through technology and data-driven decision
making.

VISION
To be India's most trusted digital lending platform.

CORE VALUES

1. CUSTOMER FIRST
   - We exist to serve our customers
   - We treat customers fairly and transparently
   - We protect customer data and privacy

2. INTEGRITY
   - We are honest and ethical in all dealings
   - We comply with all laws and regulations
   - We do what is right, not what is easy

3. INNOVATION
   - We embrace technology and new ideas
   - We continuously improve our products
   - We encourage experimentation and learning

4. COLLABORATION
   - We work as one team
   - We respect diverse perspectives
   - We communicate openly and transparently

5. EXCELLENCE
   - We strive for the highest quality
   - We hold ourselves accountable
   - We celebrate success and learn from failure

            2. EMPLOYMENT POLICIES
================================================================================

2.1 EQUAL OPPORTUNITY

SmartFinance is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate on
the basis of:
- Race, caste, religion, or ethnicity
- Gender or sexual orientation
- Age
- Disability
- Marital status
- Political affiliation

2.2 HIRING PROCESS

- All positions posted internally and externally
- Structured interview process
- Background verification mandatory
- Offer letters issued by HR

2.3 PROBATION

- 6 months for all employees
- Review at 3 months and 6 months
- Extension up to 3 months if required
- Confirmation in writing

2.4 WORKING HOURS

Regular Hours: 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday
Core Hours: 10:30 AM to 5:30 PM
Flexible timing within core hours permitted

2.5 ATTENDANCE

- Biometric attendance mandatory
- Regularization within 3 days
- 3+ late arrivals = 1 leave deduction
- Unauthorized absence = Leave Without Pay

================================================================================
            3. COMPENSATION AND BENEFITS
================================================================================

3.1 SALARY STRUCTURE

- Basic Salary: 40-50% of CTC
- HRA: 50% of Basic (metro cities)
- Special Allowance: Variable
- Statutory: PF, ESI, Gratuity

3.2 PAYROLL

- Pay Date: Last working day of month
- Payment Mode: Bank transfer
- Payslip: Available on HRMS portal

3.3 PROVIDENT FUND

- Employee Contribution: 12% of Basic
- Employer Contribution: 12% of Basic
- UAN assigned at joining
- Voluntary PF (VPF) allowed

3.4 HEALTH INSURANCE

Group Mediclaim Policy:
- Sum Insured: ₹5,00,000
- Coverage: Employee + Spouse + 2 Children
- Parents: Optional at employee cost
- Insurer: HDFC Ergo

Benefits:
- Cashless hospitalization at network hospitals
- Pre and post hospitalization
- Day care procedures
- Maternity coverage (after 1 year)

3.5 TERM LIFE INSURANCE

- Coverage: 3x Annual CTC
- Premium paid by Company
- Beneficiary nomination required

3.6 EMPLOYEE STOCK OPTIONS (ESOP)

Eligibility:
- Confirmed employees
- Minimum 1 year of service
- Performance rating of 3+ out of 5

Vesting Schedule:
- 25% after 1 year (cliff)
- 25% after 2 years
- 25% after 3 years
- 25% after 4 years

Exercise Period: 10 years from grant

3.7 PERFORMANCE BONUS

- Based on individual and company performance
- Paid annually in April
- Range: 0-30% of annual CTC
- Discretionary; not guaranteed

3.8 SALARY REVISION

- Annual appraisal cycle: January-March
- Effective: April 1
- Based on performance rating, market data, and affordability

            4. LEAVE POLICY
================================================================================

4.1 LEAVE ENTITLEMENT (Per Calendar Year)

| Leave Type          | Days | Carry Forward | Encashment     |
|---------------------|------|---------------|----------------|
| Earned Leave (EL)   | 18   | Up to 30 days | Yes (on exit)  |
| Sick Leave (SL)     | 12   | No            | No             |
| Casual Leave (CL)   | 6    | No            | No             |

4.2 SPECIAL LEAVES

| Leave Type          | Duration        | Applicability           |
|---------------------|-----------------|-------------------------|
| Maternity Leave     | 26 weeks        | As per Maternity Act    |
| Paternity Leave     | 5 days          | Within 1 month of birth |
| Marriage Leave      | 3 days          | Once during employment  |
| Bereavement Leave   | 3 days          | Immediate family        |
| Adoption Leave      | 12 weeks        | For legal adoption      |
| Comp Off            | As accrued      | For weekend work        |

4.3 PUBLIC HOLIDAYS

12 public holidays per year as announced. List published in January.

4.4 LEAVE RULES

- Prior approval required (except emergencies)
- Apply through HRMS
- Manager approval mandatory
- Medical certificate for SL > 2 days
- EL cannot be clubbed with SL without medical certificate
- Leave Without Pay (LWP) only with HR approval

            5. WORK FROM HOME POLICY
================================================================================

5.1 ELIGIBILITY

- Confirmed employees
- Roles suitable for remote work
- Prior manager approval

5.2 WFH ALLOWANCE

- Regular WFH: Nil
- Hybrid model: 3 days office, 2 days WFH

5.3 EXPECTATIONS

- Available during working hours
- Respond to communications promptly
- Attend virtual meetings with camera on
- Maintain productivity standards
- Ensure secure network connection
- Protect confidential information

5.4 EQUIPMENT

- Laptop provided by Company
- Additional equipment at employee cost
- Ergonomic setup responsibility of employee

================================================================================
            6. CODE OF CONDUCT
================================================================================

6.1 PROFESSIONAL BEHAVIOR

- Treat colleagues with respect
- Maintain professional communication
- Dress appropriately
- Be punctual for meetings
- Meet deadlines and commitments

6.2 CONFLICTS OF INTEREST

Employees must avoid and disclose:
- Financial interest in competitors or vendors
- Outside employment
- Family members working in competing organizations
- Acceptance of gifts beyond ₹2,000

6.3 ANTI-BRIBERY AND CORRUPTION

Strictly prohibited:
- Offering or accepting bribes
- Facilitating payments
- Inappropriate gifts to government officials
- Any form of kickback

6.4 FAIR LENDING PRACTICES

- Treat all borrowers fairly
- Transparent disclosure of terms
- No mis-selling or coercion
- Follow RBI Fair Practice Code
- Protect customer data

6.5 SOCIAL MEDIA

- No sharing of confidential information
- Distinguish personal views from Company views
- No negative comments about Company
- Prior approval for speaking on behalf of Company

            7. PREVENTION OF SEXUAL HARASSMENT (POSH)
================================================================================

7.1 POLICY

SmartFinance has zero tolerance for sexual harassment. This policy is in
compliance with the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention,
Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013.

7.2 DEFINITION

Sexual harassment includes:
- Physical contact and advances
- Demand or request for sexual favors
- Sexually colored remarks
- Showing pornography
- Any other unwelcome physical, verbal or non-verbal conduct of sexual nature

7.3 INTERNAL COMMITTEE (IC)

Presiding Officer: Ms. Priya Sharma (Company Secretary)
Members:
- Ms. Neha Gupta (Head HR)
- Mr. Aditya Jain (Chief Risk Officer)
- External Member: Adv. Kavita Menon (Women's Rights Advocate)

7.4 COMPLAINT PROCESS

1. Written complaint to IC within 3 months
2. IC inquiry within 90 days
3. Confidentiality maintained
4. No retaliation against complainant
5. Appropriate action based on findings

7.5 CONSEQUENCES

Depending on severity:
- Warning
- Suspension
- Termination
- Legal action

            8. INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY
================================================================================

8.1 DATA CLASSIFICATION

- Public: May be shared externally
- Internal: Company-wide access
- Confidential: Need-to-know basis
- Restricted: Highly sensitive (e.g., customer PII)

8.2 ACCESS CONTROLS

- Role-based access
- Strong passwords required
- Multi-factor authentication
- Access review quarterly

8.3 ACCEPTABLE USE

- Company systems for business use only
- No unauthorized software installation
- No copying data to personal devices
- Report security incidents immediately

8.4 CUSTOMER DATA

- Access only as required for job
- No sharing without authorization
- Encryption for transmission
- Secure disposal when no longer needed

8.5 INCIDENT REPORTING

Report immediately to: security@smartfinance.in
Contact: IT Security Team - Ext. 500

            9. GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL
================================================================================

9.1 SCOPE

This policy covers grievances related to:
- Working conditions
- Compensation and benefits
- Interpersonal issues
- Policy implementation
- Any other work-related concern

9.2 PROCESS

Level 1: Immediate Manager (Resolution within 7 days)
Level 2: Department Head (Resolution within 7 days)
Level 3: HR (Resolution within 14 days)
Level 4: Grievance Committee (Resolution within 14 days)

9.3 GRIEVANCE COMMITTEE

Chair: CEO
Members: CHRO, CFO, Legal Head

9.4 PRINCIPLES

- Confidentiality
- No retaliation
- Fair hearing
- Timely resolution

            10. DISCIPLINARY POLICY
================================================================================

10.1 MISCONDUCT

Minor Misconduct:
- Repeated late coming
- Unauthorized absence
- Violation of dress code
- Minor insubordination

Major Misconduct:
- Fraud or dishonesty
- Theft or misappropriation
- Harassment or violence
- Gross negligence
- Violation of confidentiality
- Substance abuse at work
- Willful damage to property

10.2 DISCIPLINARY ACTIONS

| Level    | Action                          | Authority        |
|----------|----------------------------------|------------------|
| Level 1  | Verbal warning                   | Manager          |
| Level 2  | Written warning                  | Manager + HR     |
| Level 3  | Final warning                    | HR Head          |
| Level 4  | Suspension / Demotion            | CEO              |
| Level 5  | Termination                      | CEO              |

10.3 PROCESS

1. Investigation by HR
2. Show cause notice to employee
3. Employee response
4. Hearing if required
5. Decision communicated in writing
6. Appeal to Grievance Committee

================================================================================
            11. SEPARATION POLICY
================================================================================

11.1 TYPES OF SEPARATION

- Resignation
- Termination
- Retirement (60 years)
- Abandonment of service
- Death

11.2 NOTICE PERIOD

| Category              | Notice Period |
|-----------------------|---------------|
| Probation             | 15 days       |
| Confirmed - Junior    | 30 days       |
| Confirmed - Mid       | 60 days       |
| Confirmed - Senior    | 90 days       |

11.3 EXIT PROCESS

1. Resignation letter to manager (copy to HR)
2. Acceptance and last working day confirmation
3. Knowledge transfer and handover
4. Exit interview with HR
5. Clearance from all departments
6. Return of Company property
7. Full and final settlement (within 45 days)

11.4 FULL AND FINAL SETTLEMENT

Payable:
- Salary for days worked
- Leave encashment
- Bonus (pro-rata)
- Reimbursements

Recoverable:
- Notice period shortfall
- Loans / advances
- Training bond (if applicable)
- Unreturned property
