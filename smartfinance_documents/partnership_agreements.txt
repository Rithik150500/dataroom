                    CO-LENDING AGREEMENT
                         WITH
                    YES BANK LIMITED
================================================================================

This Co-Lending Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on this 15th day of
November, 2024

BETWEEN:

YES BANK LIMITED, a banking company incorporated under the Banking Regulation
Act, 1949, having its registered office at Yes Bank Tower, IFC 2, 15th Floor,
Senapati Bapat Marg, Elphinstone (W), Mumbai - 400013
(hereinafter referred to as "Yes Bank" or "Bank")

AND

SMARTFINANCE INDIA LIMITED, a company incorporated under the Companies Act,
2013, having its registered office at 15th Floor, Tower B, One BKC, Bandra
Kurla Complex, Mumbai - 400051
(hereinafter referred to as "SmartFinance" or "NBFC")

(Yes Bank and SmartFinance collectively referred to as "Parties")

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                         RECITALS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A. Yes Bank is a scheduled commercial bank engaged in banking business including
   lending to retail customers.

B. SmartFinance is an NBFC registered with RBI, specializing in digital lending
   with strong customer acquisition and credit assessment capabilities.

C. RBI vide circular dated November 5, 2020 (updated September 21, 2022) has
   permitted Banks and NBFCs to co-lend to priority sector borrowers.

D. The Parties wish to enter into a co-lending arrangement for personal loans
   to eligible borrowers on the terms set out herein.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                    KEY TERMS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. PRODUCT DETAILS

   | Parameter                | Details                              |
   |--------------------------|--------------------------------------|
   | Loan Product             | Personal Loans                       |
   | Target Segment           | Salaried and Self-employed           |
   | Loan Amount Range        | ₹50,000 to ₹10,00,000                |
   | Tenure                   | 12 to 60 months                      |
   | Interest Rate (Customer) | 12% to 18% p.a.                      |
   | Processing Fee           | Up to 2.5%                           |

2. FUNDING RATIO

   | Party              | Contribution | Interest Rate Earned |
   |--------------------|--------------|----------------------|
   | Yes Bank           | 80%          | 9.5% p.a.            |
   | SmartFinance       | 20%          | Residual             |

   Blended Interest Rate: 14.5% p.a. (indicative)

3. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

   SmartFinance shall:
   - Customer acquisition and onboarding
   - KYC verification and documentation
   - Credit assessment and underwriting
   - Loan disbursement from escrow account
   - Customer service and communication
   - Collection and recovery management
   - Regulatory reporting and compliance

   Yes Bank shall:
   - Fund 80% of each loan at disbursement
   - Maintain necessary regulatory compliance
   - Provide oversight and audit rights
   - Priority sector lending classification

4. CREDIT RISK SHARING

   4.1 First Loss Default Guarantee (FLDG)
       - SmartFinance shall provide FLDG of 5% of portfolio
       - FLDG to be maintained as fixed deposit with Yes Bank
       - FLDG invocable for loans overdue beyond 90 days

   4.2 Loss Sharing (post FLDG exhaustion)
       - Yes Bank: 80% of losses
       - SmartFinance: 20% of losses

5. OPERATIONAL FRAMEWORK

   5.1 Loan Approval: All loans to be approved by Yes Bank within 24 hours of
       SmartFinance recommendation

   5.2 Disbursement: Through escrow account maintained with Yes Bank

   5.3 Collections: Through NACH mandate in favor of Yes Bank

   5.4 Reporting: Daily MIS, weekly reviews, monthly reconciliation

6. TARGET VOLUMES

   | Year    | Disbursement Target |
   |---------|---------------------|
   | Year 1  | ₹200 Crores         |
   | Year 2  | ₹500 Crores         |
   | Year 3  | ₹1,000 Crores       |

7. TERM AND TERMINATION

   - Initial term: 3 years
   - Automatic renewal for 1-year periods
   - Either party may terminate with 90 days' notice
   - Run-off of existing portfolio upon termination

8. REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

   Both Parties shall comply with:
   - RBI Co-Lending Guidelines
   - Fair Practice Code
   - Digital Lending Guidelines
   - Data Protection Laws
   - All applicable prudential norms

9. GOVERNANCE

   - Joint Steering Committee with quarterly meetings
   - Portfolio review meetings - monthly
   - Audit rights for Yes Bank

================================================================================
              LENDING SERVICE PROVIDER AGREEMENT
                         WITH
                    AXIS BANK LIMITED
================================================================================

This Agreement dated March 1, 2024 appoints SmartFinance as a Lending Service
Provider (LSP) for Axis Bank under RBI's Digital Lending Guidelines.

KEY TERMS:

1. SCOPE
   - Customer acquisition through digital channels
   - Data collection and KYC verification
   - Credit assessment and recommendation
   - Loan servicing and collection support

2. COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
   - SmartFinance shall not disburse loans or collect payments
   - All disbursements directly from Axis Bank to borrower
   - All collections to Axis Bank designated account
   - SmartFinance to be disclosed to borrowers as LSP

3. COMPENSATION
   - Origination fee: 2% of loan amount
   - Servicing fee: 0.5% p.a. on outstanding
   - Collection incentive: Based on collection efficiency

4. DATA PROTECTION
   - SmartFinance to act as Data Processor
   - Borrower data to be deleted post loan closure
   - Annual data audit by Axis Bank
