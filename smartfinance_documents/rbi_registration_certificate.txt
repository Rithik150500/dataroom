================================================================================
        FAIR PRACTICE CODE
================================================================================

SmartFinance India Limited has adopted a Fair Practice Code in compliance with
RBI Master Direction on Fair Practice Code for NBFCs.

Key Provisions:

1. LOAN APPLICATION AND PROCESSING
   - Clear and transparent loan application process
   - Communication of loan terms in vernacular language
   - Processing within committed timeline

2. LOAN APPRAISAL AND TERMS
   - Assessment of borrower's repayment capacity
   - All-in-cost disclosure (APR)
   - No hidden charges

3. DISBURSEMENT
   - Direct disbursement to borrower's account
   - No third-party payments except permitted

4. GENERAL PROVISIONS
   - No interference in borrower's affairs
   - No discrimination
   - Reasonable security valuation
   - No excessive interest

5. RECOVERY
   - No harassment or intimidation
   - Recovery agents code of conduct
   - No calls before 8 AM or after 7 PM
   - No use of muscle power

================================================================================
        KYC/AML POLICY SUMMARY
================================================================================

Policy adopted in compliance with:
- Prevention of Money Laundering Act, 2002
- RBI Master Direction on KYC
- PMLA Rules, 2005

Key Elements:

1. CUSTOMER IDENTIFICATION
   - PAN mandatory for loans > ₹50,000
   - Officially Valid Documents (OVDs)
   - Video KYC for remote onboarding

2. CUSTOMER DUE DILIGENCE
   - Risk categorization (Low, Medium, High)
   - Enhanced due diligence for high-risk
   - Periodic KYC updates

3. TRANSACTION MONITORING
   - Automated alerts for suspicious patterns
   - Cash transaction reports (> ₹10 Lakhs)
   - Suspicious transaction reports

4. RECORD KEEPING
   - 5 years after end of relationship
   - 8 years for transaction records
