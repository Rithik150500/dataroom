================================================================================
                EMPLOYEE STOCK OPTION PLAN
                  ESOP SCHEME 2021
                        OF
              SMARTFINANCE INDIA LIMITED
================================================================================

            Approved by Board: March 15, 2021
            Approved by Shareholders: April 10, 2021

================================================================================
                1. INTRODUCTION
================================================================================

1.1 NAME
    This scheme shall be called "SmartFinance India Limited - Employee Stock
    Option Plan 2021" or "ESOP Scheme 2021".

1.2 PURPOSE
    The purpose of this Scheme is to:
    (a) Attract and retain talented employees
    (b) Align employee interests with shareholders
    (c) Reward employees for their contribution
    (d) Create long-term wealth for employees

1.3 EFFECTIVE DATE
    This Scheme shall be effective from April 10, 2021.

================================================================================
                2. DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

2.1 "Board" means the Board of Directors of the Company.

2.2 "Committee" means the Nomination and Remuneration Committee.

2.3 "Company" means SmartFinance India Limited.

2.4 "Employee" means a permanent employee of the Company working in India,
    including Directors (other than Independent Directors).

2.5 "Exercise" means the act of applying for and being allotted shares
    pursuant to the Options.

2.6 "Exercise Period" means the period during which an Employee can exercise
    vested Options.

2.7 "Exercise Price" means the price per share payable by the Employee to
    exercise the Options.

2.8 "Grant" means the issuance of Options to an Employee under this Scheme.

2.9 "Grant Date" means the date on which Options are granted.

2.10 "Option" means a right granted to an Employee to purchase shares at the
     Exercise Price.

2.11 "Scheme" means this Employee Stock Option Plan 2021.

2.12 "Vesting" means the process by which Options become exercisable.

2.13 "Vesting Date" means the date on which Options vest.

================================================================================
                3. ADMINISTRATION
================================================================================

3.1 COMMITTEE ADMINISTRATION
    The Scheme shall be administered by the Nomination and Remuneration
    Committee, which shall have the power to:
    (a) Grant Options to eligible Employees
    (b) Determine the number of Options to be granted
    (c) Determine Exercise Price and Vesting Schedule
    (d) Interpret provisions of the Scheme
    (e) Prescribe forms and procedures
    (f) Make all other determinations necessary for administration

3.2 COMMITTEE COMPOSITION
    - Chairperson: Ms. Meera Sanghvi (Independent Director)
    - Member: Mr. Tarun Davda (Nominee Director)
    - Member: Mr. Rajiv Anand (Independent Director)

================================================================================
                4. SHARES AVAILABLE
================================================================================

4.1 SHARE POOL
    The maximum number of shares that may be issued under this Scheme is
    6,00,000 (Six Lakh) Equity Shares of face value ₹10 each.

4.2 POOL AS PERCENTAGE
    This represents 15% of the fully diluted share capital as on the
    effective date.

4.3 RECYCLING
    If any Options are forfeited, expire, or are cancelled, such Options
    shall again become available for grant under this Scheme.

4.4 ADJUSTMENTS
    The number of shares and Exercise Price shall be adjusted for stock
    splits, bonus issues, and other capital changes.

================================================================================
                5. ELIGIBILITY
================================================================================

5.1 ELIGIBLE EMPLOYEES
    The following are eligible for grant of Options:
    (a) Permanent employees of the Company
    (b) Directors (other than Independent Directors)
    (c) Employees of subsidiary companies (if any)

5.2 NOT ELIGIBLE
    The following are not eligible:
    (a) Independent Directors
    (b) Consultants and contractors
    (c) Employees who are promoters or belong to promoter group holding
        more than 10% of share capital

5.3 CRITERIA
    The Committee may consider the following for grants:
    (a) Length of service
    (b) Performance rating
    (c) Grade and designation
    (d) Criticality of role
    (e) Future potential

================================================================================
                6. GRANT OF OPTIONS
================================================================================

6.1 GRANT LETTER
    Each grant shall be evidenced by a Grant Letter specifying:
    (a) Number of Options
    (b) Exercise Price
    (c) Vesting Schedule
    (d) Exercise Period
    (e) Other terms and conditions

6.2 GRANT FREQUENCY
    Options may be granted at any time as determined by the Committee.

6.3 ANNUAL GRANT CYCLE
    Typically, Options are granted annually following performance appraisal.

6.4 NO GUARANTEED GRANTS
    Grants are at the discretion of the Committee and are not guaranteed.

================================================================================
                7. EXERCISE PRICE
================================================================================

7.1 DETERMINATION
    The Exercise Price shall be determined by the Committee but shall not be
    less than the face value of the shares.

7.2 CURRENT EXERCISE PRICE
    Based on latest Series A valuation:
    - Fair Market Value: ₹47.22 per share
    - Exercise Price for new grants: ₹38.00 per share (discount for employees)

7.3 PRICING FOR DIFFERENT GRANTS

| Grant Year | Exercise Price | Basis                    |
|------------|----------------|--------------------------|
| 2021       | ₹10.00         | Face value               |
| 2022       | ₹25.00         | Pre-Series A valuation   |
| 2023       | ₹38.00         | Series A discount        |
| 2024       | ₹38.00         | Series A discount        |

================================================================================
                8. VESTING
================================================================================

8.1 VESTING SCHEDULE
    Options shall vest over a period of 4 years as follows:

    | Cliff/Year | Vesting %  | Cumulative % |
    |------------|------------|--------------|
    | Year 1     | 25%        | 25%          |
    | Year 2     | 25%        | 50%          |
    | Year 3     | 25%        | 75%          |
    | Year 4     | 25%        | 100%         |

8.2 CLIFF PERIOD
    No Options shall vest during the first 12 months from Grant Date
    (cliff period).

8.3 VESTING DATE
    Options shall vest on each anniversary of the Grant Date.

8.4 ACCELERATED VESTING
    The Committee may provide accelerated vesting in the following events:
    (a) Change of control
    (b) IPO
    (c) Death or disability of Employee

================================================================================
                9. EXERCISE OF OPTIONS
================================================================================

9.1 EXERCISE PERIOD
    - Options may be exercised after vesting
    - Exercise Period: 10 years from Grant Date
    - Options not exercised within Exercise Period shall lapse

9.2 EXERCISE PROCESS
    (a) Submit Exercise Application to Company
    (b) Pay Exercise Price via bank transfer
    (c) Company to allot shares within 30 days
    (d) Share certificate or demat credit within 15 days of allotment

9.3 PARTIAL EXERCISE
    Vested Options may be exercised in whole or in part.

9.4 TAX WITHHOLDING
    The Company shall deduct applicable taxes (perquisite tax) at exercise.

================================================================================
                10. TERMINATION OF EMPLOYMENT
================================================================================

10.1 VOLUNTARY RESIGNATION

     | Status              | Treatment                              |
     |---------------------|----------------------------------------|
     | Unvested Options    | Forfeited immediately                  |
     | Vested, Unexercised | Must exercise within 90 days of LWD    |

10.2 TERMINATION FOR CAUSE

     All Options (vested and unvested) forfeited immediately.

10.3 TERMINATION WITHOUT CAUSE / REDUNDANCY

     | Status              | Treatment                              |
     |---------------------|----------------------------------------|
     | Unvested Options    | Pro-rata vesting as on termination date|
     | Vested, Unexercised | Must exercise within 180 days          |

10.4 DEATH

     | Status              | Treatment                              |
     |---------------------|----------------------------------------|
     | Unvested Options    | Immediate full vesting                 |
     | Vested, Unexercised | Legal heirs may exercise within 1 year |

10.5 DISABILITY

     | Status              | Treatment                              |
     |---------------------|----------------------------------------|
     | Unvested Options    | Immediate full vesting                 |
     | Vested, Unexercised | Must exercise within 1 year            |

10.6 RETIREMENT

     | Status              | Treatment                              |
     |---------------------|----------------------------------------|
     | Unvested Options    | Pro-rata vesting                       |
     | Vested, Unexercised | Must exercise within 1 year            |

================================================================================
                11. CHANGE OF CONTROL
================================================================================

11.1 DEFINITION
     "Change of Control" means:
     (a) Acquisition of more than 50% of voting shares
     (b) Merger where Company is not the surviving entity
     (c) Sale of substantially all assets

11.2 TREATMENT
     Upon Change of Control:
     (a) All unvested Options shall immediately vest
     (b) Employee shall have right to exercise within 30 days
     (c) Alternatively, Options may be assumed or substituted by acquirer

================================================================================
                12. NON-TRANSFERABILITY
================================================================================

12.1 Options are personal to the Employee and are non-transferable.

12.2 Options cannot be pledged, hypothecated, or encumbered.

12.3 Exception: Transfer to legal heirs in case of death.

================================================================================
                13. LOCK-IN
================================================================================

13.1 Shares issued upon exercise shall not be subject to any lock-in, except
     as required by:
     (a) Shareholders Agreement
     (b) Applicable law
     (c) IPO lock-in requirements

================================================================================
                14. TAXATION
================================================================================

14.1 PERQUISITE TAX AT EXERCISE
     - Taxable perquisite = (FMV on exercise - Exercise Price) x Shares
     - Company to deduct TDS at applicable slab rate
     - Employee to pay any shortfall

14.2 CAPITAL GAINS TAX AT SALE
     - Short-term (< 24 months): As per slab rate
     - Long-term (> 24 months): 20% with indexation

14.3 TAX RESPONSIBILITY
     All taxes are the Employee's responsibility. Company shall provide
     Form 12BA and Form 16.

================================================================================
                15. RIGHTS AS SHAREHOLDER
================================================================================

15.1 Employees have no rights as shareholders until shares are actually
     allotted upon exercise.

15.2 Upon allotment, Employees shall have all rights including voting and
     dividend rights.

17.2 The Board may terminate the Scheme at any time, but existing grants
