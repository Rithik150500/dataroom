            1. AUDIT COMMITTEE CHARTER
================================================================================

1.1 COMPOSITION

| Member               | Designation              | Role        |
|----------------------|--------------------------|-------------|
| Ms. Meera Sanghvi    | Independent Director     | Chairperson |
| Mr. Tarun Davda      | Nominee Director         | Member      |
| Mr. Rajiv Anand      | Independent Director     | Member      |

Permanent Invitees: CFO, Internal Auditor, Statutory Auditor

1.2 MEETING FREQUENCY
Quarterly (minimum 4 times a year)

1.3 QUORUM
Two members present, majority being Independent Directors

1.4 RESPONSIBILITIES

Financial Reporting:
- Review quarterly and annual financial statements
- Ensure compliance with Ind AS and RBI prudential norms
- Review significant accounting policies and estimates
- Review management discussion and analysis

Internal Audit:
- Approve internal audit plan and scope
- Review internal audit reports and findings
- Monitor closure of audit observations
- Evaluate internal audit function effectiveness

External Audit:
- Recommend appointment/removal of statutory auditor
- Review audit plan and independence
- Review audit findings and management responses
- Approve audit and non-audit fees

Risk and Controls:
- Review adequacy of internal controls
- Review fraud risk management
- Oversee whistle-blower mechanism
- Review related party transactions

Compliance:
- Review compliance with RBI regulations
- Review regulatory inspection observations
- Monitor compliance with listing regulations (for NCDs)

1.5 POWERS
- Seek information from any employee
- Obtain external professional advice
- Investigate any activity within its terms of reference
- Full access to company records and personnel

            2. RISK MANAGEMENT COMMITTEE CHARTER
================================================================================

2.1 COMPOSITION

| Member               | Designation              | Role        |
|----------------------|--------------------------|-------------|
| Mr. Rajiv Anand      | Independent Director     | Chairperson |
| Mr. Vikram Mehta     | Managing Director        | Member      |
| Ms. Anjali Deshmukh  | CFO                      | Member      |
| Ms. Meera Sanghvi    | Independent Director     | Member      |
| Mr. Aditya Jain      | Chief Risk Officer       | Member      |

2.2 MEETING FREQUENCY
Bi-monthly (minimum 6 times a year)

2.3 QUORUM
Three members present, including at least one Independent Director

2.4 RESPONSIBILITIES

Risk Framework:
- Approve risk management policy and framework
- Define risk appetite and tolerance limits
- Review risk governance structure

Credit Risk:
- Review credit policy and underwriting standards
- Monitor portfolio quality and NPA trends
- Review concentration risks
- Approve write-off policy

Market Risk:
- Monitor interest rate risk
- Review ALM mismatches
- Oversee hedging strategies (if any)

Liquidity Risk:
- Review liquidity position and LCR
- Approve contingency funding plan
- Monitor funding concentration

Operational Risk:
- Review operational risk framework
- Monitor key risk indicators
- Review business continuity plan

Compliance & Regulatory Risk:
- Monitor regulatory changes and impact
- Review compliance risk assessment

Reporting:
- Report to Board on risk profile
- Escalate material risks and breaches

            3. NOMINATION & REMUNERATION COMMITTEE CHARTER
================================================================================

3.1 COMPOSITION

| Member               | Designation              | Role        |
|----------------------|--------------------------|-------------|
| Ms. Meera Sanghvi    | Independent Director     | Chairperson |
| Mr. Tarun Davda      | Nominee Director         | Member      |
| Mr. Shailendra Singh | Nominee Director         | Member      |

3.2 MEETING FREQUENCY
Quarterly or as required

3.3 QUORUM
Two members present

3.4 RESPONSIBILITIES

Board Composition:
- Recommend Board size and composition
- Identify candidates for Director positions
- Ensure fit and proper criteria compliance (RBI)
- Conduct due diligence on Director candidates
- Recommend appointment/removal of Directors

Succession Planning:
- Develop succession plan for Board and KMP
- Review leadership pipeline
- Emergency succession arrangements

Performance Evaluation:
- Define criteria for Board evaluation
- Conduct annual Board effectiveness review
- Evaluate individual Director performance
- Evaluate Committee effectiveness

Remuneration:
- Recommend remuneration policy
- Determine MD/WTD remuneration (for Board approval)
- Review KMP compensation
- Oversee ESOP grants and administration
- Ensure remuneration complies with RBI guidelines

3.5 FIT AND PROPER CRITERIA (RBI)
For all Directors:
- Educational qualification
- Experience in financial sector
- Track record of integrity
- No criminal proceedings
- No disqualification under Companies Act
- Declaration of interest

            4. ASSET-LIABILITY COMMITTEE (ALCO) CHARTER
================================================================================

4.1 COMPOSITION

| Member               | Designation              | Role        |
|----------------------|--------------------------|-------------|
| Mr. Vikram Mehta     | Managing Director        | Chairperson |
| Ms. Anjali Deshmukh  | CFO                      | Member      |
| Mr. Aditya Jain      | Chief Risk Officer       | Member      |
| Ms. Priya Sharma     | Company Secretary        | Member      |

4.2 MEETING FREQUENCY
Monthly

4.3 QUORUM
Three members including Chairperson

4.4 RESPONSIBILITIES

Liquidity Management:
- Review liquidity position daily/weekly
- Monitor LCR and other liquidity ratios
- Approve short-term funding decisions
- Manage liquidity stress situations

Interest Rate Management:
- Review interest rate gaps
- Recommend pricing for assets and liabilities
- Monitor NIM and spreads
- Recommend hedging strategies

Funding Strategy:
- Review funding plan vs actual
- Approve funding mix and sources
- Monitor cost of funds
- Manage lender relationships

ALM Reporting:
- Review structural liquidity statement
- Monitor maturity mismatches
- Submit returns to RBI (NBS-ALM)

Stress Testing:
- Review stress test scenarios and results
- Activate contingency plans if needed

4.5 DELEGATED AUTHORITY
- Approve borrowings up to ₹25 Crores per transaction
- Approve investments up to ₹10 Crores

================================================================================
            7. COMMITTEE GOVERNANCE
================================================================================

7.1 COMMON PROVISIONS

Minutes:
- Minutes to be recorded for all meetings
- Circulated within 15 days
- Confirmed at next meeting
- Maintained for 8 years

Reporting:
- Committee Chairperson to report to Board
- Significant matters to be escalated
- Annual report on Committee activities

Review:
- Annual review of Committee charter
- Board to evaluate Committee effectiveness

Attendance:
- Members to attend at least 75% of meetings
- Video conferencing permitted

7.2 BOARD OVERSIGHT

The Board retains ultimate responsibility for all matters delegated to
Committees. Committees shall make recommendations to the Board on matters
requiring Board approval.
