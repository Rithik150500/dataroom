RESOLUTION 4: APPROVAL OF RELATED PARTY TRANSACTION

The Chairperson informed the Board that the Company's existing office lease agreement with Sharma Properties LLP (in which Mr. Rahul Sharma holds 40% interest) is due for renewal on December 31, 2025.

Current Terms:
- Premises: 3,000 sq ft at Koramangala 5th Block, Bangalore
- Current Rent: Rs. 3,00,000 per month
- Security Deposit: Rs. 9,00,000 (already paid)

Proposed Renewal Terms:
- Period: 3 years (January 1, 2026 to December 31, 2028)
- Rent: Rs. 3,30,000 per month (10% increase, in line with market rates)
- Annual Escalation: 5%
- Lock-in: 2 years

The CFO presented a comparative analysis showing that the proposed rent is 5% below the market rate for similar properties in the area.

Mr. Rahul Sharma declared his interest in the transaction and abstained from voting.

After discussion, the following resolution was passed by the non-interested Directors:

"RESOLVED THAT pursuant to the provisions of Section 188 and other applicable provisions of the Companies Act, 2013, read with Rule 15 of the Companies (Meetings of Board and its Powers) Rules, 2014, and subject to such other approvals as may be required, consent of the Board be and is hereby accorded for renewal of the office lease agreement with Sharma Properties LLP for a period of 3 (three) years on the following terms:

- Monthly Rent: Rs. 3,30,000 (Rupees Three Lakhs Thirty Thousand Only)
- Annual Escalation: 5%
- Lock-in Period: 2 years
- Security Deposit: Rs. 9,00,000 (already deposited)

RESOLVED FURTHER THAT the Board notes and records that:
(a) Mr. Rahul Sharma has disclosed his interest as a 40% partner in Sharma Properties LLP;
(b) The proposed rent is below market rates based on independent valuation;
(c) The transaction is in the ordinary course of business and at arm's length;

RESOLVED FURTHER THAT the transaction be placed before the shareholders for their approval at the forthcoming Annual General Meeting as required under Section 188 of the Act.

RESOLVED FURTHER THAT Mr. Rahul Sharma, Director, be and is hereby authorized to execute the lease renewal agreement and all related documents on behalf of the Company."
